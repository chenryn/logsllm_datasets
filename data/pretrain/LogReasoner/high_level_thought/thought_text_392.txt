Problem Overview: The task involves categorizing log entries into specific alert types based on their content, facilitating the identification of system states or issues.
Key Challenges:
Key challenges include accurately interpreting diverse log formats, distinguishing between similar alert types, and recognizing contextual clues that indicate specific alarm categories.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify the main subject of the log entry to determine the system component involved.
2. Analyze keywords and phrases that indicate the nature of the event (e.g., 'failure', 'overload', 'configuration').
3. Match identified keywords to the predefined alert types based on their meanings and implications.
4. Validate against known patterns in the logs to enhance accuracy of the classification.
5. Select the most relevant alert type that best represents the log entry based on your analysis.
