Problem Overview: The task involves categorizing log entries into specific alert types based on their content. This requires understanding the context and implications of various log messages to accurately classify them into predefined categories.
Key Challenges:
Key challenges include distinguishing between different types of alerts based on specific keywords and phrases within diverse log formats. Additionally, interpreting technical terminology accurately is crucial for correct classification.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify the primary subject of the log entry to understand the context.
2. Look for keywords that correlate with predefined alert types.
3. Analyze the structure of the log message to extract relevant parameters.
4. Match the extracted parameters and keywords with the criteria for each alert type.
5. Make a decision on the alert type based on the strongest correlation to the identified keywords and structure.
6. Validate the classification against known examples to ensure accuracy.

2. Determine the context of the log entry, focusing on specific operational terms that indicate the nature of the alert.
3. Compare identified keywords against a reference set of criteria for each alert type.
4. Select the most appropriate alert type based on the strongest matching criteria.
5. Validate the selection by ensuring it aligns with the overall context of the log message.
