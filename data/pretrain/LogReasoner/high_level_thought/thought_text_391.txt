Problem Overview: The task aims to classify log entries into specific alert types based on contextual keywords and phrases. Each log entry indicates an event or condition in a system, and the goal is to associate it with one of the predefined alert categories.
Key Challenges:
The primary challenge lies in accurately interpreting the log structure and identifying key terms or phrases that correspond to the specified alert types. Ambiguities in wording or similar terminologies across different logs can complicate classification.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify the key components of the log entry, focusing on the structure and the main message.
2. Extract relevant keywords and terms related to the alert type from the log content.
3. Compare the extracted keywords with known categories to determine the most appropriate alert type.
4. Ensure that the selected alert type aligns with the context provided in the log entry.
5. Output the classified alert type based on the analysis.
