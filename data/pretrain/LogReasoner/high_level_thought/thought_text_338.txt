Problem Overview: The task involves analyzing log entries to categorize alerts into specific types based on predefined criteria. Each log entry contains information about system events or errors, and the objective is to classify these into one of the alert types: ['Event Trap', 'communicationsAlarm', 'qualityOfServiceAlarm', 'processingErrorAlarm', 'equipmentAlarm', 'environmentalAlarm'].
Key Challenges:
The primary challenges include accurately interpreting technical log messages, distinguishing between similar alert types, and understanding the specific wording and structure used in the logs that correlate with each alert type.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify the log type and structure from the provided input.
2. Analyze the keywords and phrases within the log message that indicate specific conditions or failures.
3. Compare the identified keywords against the definitions of the alert types to determine potential matches.
4. Evaluate context clues such as severity, affected components, and operational status to refine the classification.
5. Select the alert type that best fits the patterns and information extracted from the log.
6. Validate the chosen classification against known examples to ensure consistency and accuracy.
