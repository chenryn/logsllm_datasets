Problem Overview: The task involves classifying log entries into specific alert types based on their content, focusing on identifying the nature of the alert among predefined categories.
Key Challenges:
The main challenge lies in accurately interpreting log messages that can be complex and varied. Each log entry may contain multiple elements, and understanding the context and keywords is crucial for correct classification.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify keywords in the log entry that indicate the nature of the alert.
2. Match the identified keywords against the predefined list of alert types.
3. Consider the context provided in the log message to determine if it fits a specific alert category.
4. Select the most appropriate alert type based on the analysis.
5. Validate the selection against known examples to ensure accuracy.
