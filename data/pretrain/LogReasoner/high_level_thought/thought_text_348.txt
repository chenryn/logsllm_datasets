Problem Overview: The objective is to identify the type of alert indicated by the provided log entries. Each log entry corresponds to specific operational conditions or issues in a system, and the analysis should classify these entries into predefined alert categories.
Key Challenges:
The primary challenge lies in accurately mapping diverse log messages to the appropriate alert type based on specific keywords and phrases. This requires understanding the context and semantics of each log entry while discerning subtle differences between similar alert types.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify key terms and phrases within the log entry that signal a specific condition or alert.
2. Compare the identified terms against a predefined list of alert categories to find potential matches.
3. Evaluate the context of the log entry to confirm which alert type is most appropriate based on the defined criteria.
4. Assign the corresponding alert type based on the evaluation and context understanding.
5. Document the classification decision for future reference and validation.
