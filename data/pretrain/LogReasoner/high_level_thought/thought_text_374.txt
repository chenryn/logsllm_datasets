Problem Overview: Identify the type of alert represented in log entries by categorizing them into predefined alarm types such as Event Trap, communicationsAlarm, qualityOfServiceAlarm, processingErrorAlarm, equipmentAlarm, and environmentalAlarm based on the content of the logs.
Key Challenges:
The main challenge lies in accurately parsing the log structure to extract relevant context clues that hint at the appropriate category. Logs may contain technical jargon and varying formats, making it essential to focus on keywords and phrases indicative of specific alarm types.
Solution Steps:
1. Read the log entry to understand its structure and content.
2. Identify key terms or phrases that match the provided alert types.
3. Determine if the log entry indicates an event, communication issue, quality of service problem, processing error, equipment failure, or environmental concern.
4. Cross-reference identified keywords with the definitions of each alarm type to classify the log correctly.
5. Output the identified alarm type as the solution.
