Problem Overview: The task is to classify log entries into specific alert types based on their content. This involves identifying keywords and patterns within logs that indicate various alarm types, allowing for effective monitoring and response.
Key Challenges:
The primary challenge lies in accurately interpreting the diverse log formats and content to determine the appropriate alert type. Variability in log messages, the need for contextual understanding, and the potential for ambiguous phrasing complicate the analysis process.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify the key components of the log entry, including prefixes and messages.
2. Analyze the message content for specific phrases or keywords that correlate with defined alarm types.
3. Map identified keywords to respective alert types based on predefined rules or categories.
4. Validate the classification by cross-referencing with known examples of each alert type.
5. Document and report the identified alert type for the log entry.
