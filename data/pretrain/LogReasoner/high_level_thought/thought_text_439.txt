Problem Overview: The objective is to categorize log entries into specific alert types based on their content, identifying the nature of the alerts such as Event Trap, communicationsAlarm, qualityOfServiceAlarm, and others.
Key Challenges:
The main challenges include accurately interpreting technical jargon within logs, recognizing patterns that correspond to specific alert types, and ensuring consistent classification amidst varying log formats and terminologies.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify key phrases and indicators in the log entry that suggest a specific alert type.
2. Match these phrases against predefined criteria for each alert type.
3. Analyze contextual information within the log to determine the appropriate category.
4. Eliminate ambiguity by confirming that the log content aligns with the selected alert type's characteristics.
5. Document the classification decision for future reference or training purposes.

Problem Overview: The log analysis task involves identifying the type of alarm indicated by a log entry from a predefined list of categories based on specific keywords and context within the log.

4. Select the alarm type from the predefined list based on the analysis.
5. Validate the classification against examples to ensure correctness.
