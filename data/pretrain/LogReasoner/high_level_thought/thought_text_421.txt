Problem Overview: The task involves analyzing logs to determine the type of alert generated based on specific log messages. Alerts can be categorized into predefined types such as Event Trap, communicationsAlarm, qualityOfServiceAlarm, processingErrorAlarm, equipmentAlarm, or environmentalAlarm.
Key Challenges:
Identifying the correct category from logs requires understanding of the log structure and the ability to associate specific phrases or patterns with alert types. Log messages may contain complex information, potentially leading to misclassification if not carefully analyzed.
Solution Steps:
1. Parse the input log to extract key elements and patterns.
2. Identify keywords or phrases that correlate with predefined alert types.
3. Match the extracted information against the known alert types.
4. Determine the most appropriate alert type based on the log context and content.
5. Validate the decision by checking if it aligns with historical data or similar logs.
