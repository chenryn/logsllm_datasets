Problem Overview: The task involves categorizing log entries into specific alert types based on keywords and context within the log message. Each entry can indicate various operational conditions, such as equipment failures or environmental issues, which require proper classification.
Key Challenges:
The primary challenge lies in accurately interpreting the context and specific keywords within diverse log formats. Logs may contain technical jargon, and distinguishing between similar alert types can be difficult without a clear understanding of the underlying systems and their operations.
Solution Steps:
1. Identify key components in the log message, such as the prefix and the main event description.
2. Analyze the log message for specific keywords or phrases that correlate with the known alert types.
3. Match the identified keywords or phrases against predefined categories of alerts.
4. Determine the appropriate alert type based on the closest match to the keywords found in the log.
5. Validate the classification by ensuring it aligns with domain knowledge or historical data regarding similar logs.
