**ATT&CK ID:** M1026  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Privileged Account Management

Regularly audit both domain and local accounts, along with their associated permission levels, to identify any scenarios that could potentially allow an adversary to gain broad access by compromising the credentials of a privileged account. [3] [4] These audits should also verify whether default accounts have been enabled and if any unauthorized new local accounts have been created. Adhere to best practices for the design and administration of enterprise networks to minimize the use of privileged accounts across different administrative tiers. [5]
