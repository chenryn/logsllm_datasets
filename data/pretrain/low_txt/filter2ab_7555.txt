**ATT&CK ID:** G0021  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Molerats

**Description:**
Molerats has been known to distribute malicious files via email, enticing users to click on "Enable Content" to execute an embedded macro. This action then triggers the download of malicious archives.[147][148][149]
