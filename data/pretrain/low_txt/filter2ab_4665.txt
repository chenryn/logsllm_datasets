**ATT&CK ID:** C0025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack

In the 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack, the Sandworm Team employed a tactic of transferring executable files with a `.txt` extension and subsequently renaming them to `.exe`. This method was likely used to evade detection by security systems that monitor file extensions. [1]
