### Missing Data and Its Impact
The presence of missing data, which we addressed by reconstructing orders using feedback, may have led to an underestimation of the number of digital items sold.

### Despite These Limitations
Despite these limitations, our study represents the first empirical, quantitative analysis of cybercriminal security practices using ground-truth data from an online anonymous market. This work provides a deeper understanding of the (in)secure practices employed by cybercriminal entrepreneurs. Notably, we found that among vendors specializing in digital cybercrime items—who one might assume would have robust digital security—there is a high prevalence of insecure practices. These findings can help law enforcement allocate their limited resources more effectively, as they now know that building a case against seasoned cybercriminals is not an insurmountable task.
