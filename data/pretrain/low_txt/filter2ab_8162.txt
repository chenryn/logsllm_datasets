**ATT&CK ID:** M0930  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Segmentation

**Description:**
Implement network segmentation to categorize and isolate operational assets and their management devices based on their functional roles within the process. This approach enhances security by enabling stricter isolation for more critical control and operational information within the control environment.
