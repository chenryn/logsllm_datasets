# DuckyDebugDuck: Fun with Flags 2021

DuckyDebugDuck was a relatively straightforward Capture the Flag (CTF) challenge created by the Technion as part of the C2C CTF 2021 competition. For additional resources, including other write-ups and solutions, you can visit [this GitHub discussion](https://github.com/Dvd848/CTFs/discussions/21).

- **Event:** C2C CTF 2021
- **Organizer:** Technion
- **Challenge Name:** DuckyDebugDuck
- **Difficulty Level:** Easy
- **Resources:** [GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/Dvd848/CTFs/discussions/21)

This challenge provided an excellent opportunity for participants to test and improve their CTF skills in a fun and engaging environment.
