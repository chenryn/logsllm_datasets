If you wish to stay updated on the significant changes to this site, you have two options:

1. [Subscribe to the RSS feed](#rss_feed)
2. [Browse the Newsletter section](#newsletter_section)

## [RSS Feed](rss.md)
You can customize the frequency of updates according to your preference:
- [Daily](https://lyz-code.github.io/blue-book/daily.xml)
- [Weekly](https://lyz-code.github.io/blue-book/weekly.xml)
- [Monthly](https://lyz-code.github.io/blue-book/monthly.xml)
- [Yearly](https://lyz-code.github.io/blue-book/yearly.xml)

## Newsletter Section
The newsletter section organizes the changes by year, month, week, and day. You can navigate through this section to view the most recent updates.

## Credits
Both the newsletters and the RSS feeds are generated automatically from the commit messages in the repository, thanks to the [mkdocs-newsletter](https://lyz-code.github.io/mkdocs-newsletter/) plugin.
