**Sidewinder APT 组织的加密技术**

[Sidewinder](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0121) 使用 Base64 编码和 ECDH-P256 加密来保护其有效载荷。[1][2][3]

- [1] Cyble. (2020, September 26). SideWinder APT Targets with futuristic Tactics and Techniques. Retrieved January 29, 2021.
- [2] Hegel, T. (2021, January 13). A Global Perspective of the SideWinder APT. Retrieved January 27, 2021.
- [3] Rewterz. (2020, April 20). Sidewinder APT Group Campaign Analysis. Retrieved January 29, 2021.

**ROKRAT 恶意软件的通信协议**

[ROKRAT](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0240) 可以使用 HTTP 和 HTTPS 进行命令与控制（C2）通信。[4][5][6]

- [4] Mercer, W., Rascagneres, P. (2017, April 03). Introducing ROKRAT. Retrieved May 21, 2018.
- [5] Pantazopoulos, N. (2018, November 8). RokRat Analysis. Retrieved May 21, 2020.
- [6] Jazi, H. (2021, January 6). Retrohunting APT37: North Korean APT used VBA self decode technique to inject RokRat. Retrieved March 22, 2022.

**Revenge RAT 的系统工具利用**

[Revenge RAT](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0379) 利用 [Forfiles](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0193) 工具在受感染系统上执行命令。[7]

- [7] Gannon, M. (2019, February 11). With Upgrades in Delivery and Support Infrastructure, Revenge RAT Malware is a Bigger Threat. Retrieved May 1, 2019.

**SVCReady 恶意软件的信息收集能力**

[SVCReady](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S1064) 能够从被感染主机中收集已安装软件的列表。[8]

- [8] Schlapfer, P. (2022, June 6). A New Loader Gets Ready. Retrieved December 13, 2022.

**Brute Ratel C4 的网络横向移动技术**

[Brute Ratel C4](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S1063) 可以使用 WinRM 进行横向移动。[9]

- [9] Harbison, M. and Renals, P. (2022, July 5). When Pentest Tools Go Brutal: Red-Teaming Tool Being Abused by Malicious Actors. Retrieved February 1, 2023.

**BITTER APT 组织的恶意软件下载行为**

[BITTER](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G1002) 在受感染主机上下载额外的恶意软件和工具。[10][11]

- [10] Dela Paz, R. (2016, October 21). BITTER: a targeted attack against Pakistan. Retrieved June 1, 2022.
- [11] Raghuprasad, C. (2022, May 11). Bitter APT adds Bangladesh to their targets. Retrieved June 1, 2022.

**Dark Caracal APT 组织的 CHM 文件利用**

[Dark Caracal](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0070) 利用包含下载并运行可执行文件命令的编译 HTML 文件。[12]

- [12] Blaich, A., et al. (2018, January 18). Dark Caracal: Cyber-espionage at a Global Scale. Retrieved April 11, 2018.

**BLUELIGHT 恶意软件的数据编码技术**

[BLUELIGHT](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0657) 使用 XOR 对数据进行编码，生成二进制块。[13]

- [13] Cash, D., Grunzweig, J., Meltzer, M., Adair, S., Lancaster, T. (2021, August 17). North Korean APT InkySquid Infects Victims Using Browser Exploits. Retrieved September 30, 2021.

**Earth Lusca APT 组织的用户账户信息收集**

[Earth Lusca](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G1006) 通过 `whoami` 命令收集用户账户信息。[14]

- [14] Chen, J., et al. (2022). Delving Deep: An Analysis of Earth Lusca’s Operations. Retrieved July 1, 2022.
