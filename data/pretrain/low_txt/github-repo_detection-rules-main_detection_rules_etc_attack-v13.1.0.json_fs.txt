### Lazarus Group - Operation Dream Job
**描述**：在[Operation Dream Job](https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0022)行动中，[Lazarus Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0032)创建了计划任务，以定期执行远程XSL脚本。(引用来源: [ESET Lazarus Jun 2020](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ESET_Operation_Interception.pdf))

### Stuxnet
**描述**：[Stuxnet](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0603)会枚举可移动驱动器以进行感染。(引用来源: [Nicolas Falliere, Liam O Murchu, Eric Chien February 2011](https://www.wired.com/images_blogs/threatlevel/2011/02/Symantec-Stuxnet-Update-Feb-2011.pdf))

### Orangeworm
**描述**：[Orangeworm](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0071)将其后门程序复制到开放的网络共享中，包括ADMIN$、C$WINDOWS、D$WINDOWS和E$WINDOWS。(引用来源: [Symantec Orangeworm April 2018](https://www.symantec.com/blogs/threat-intelligence/orangeworm-targets-healthcare-us-europe-asia))

### FatDuke
**描述**：[FatDuke](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0512)针对每个目标组织使用多个C2服务器。(引用来源: [ESET Dukes October 2019](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/ESET_Operation_Ghost_Dukes.pdf))

### MacMa
**描述**：[MacMa](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S1016)可以从被攻陷系统收集并外泄文件。(引用来源: [ESET DazzleSpy Jan 2022](https://www.welivesecurity.com/2022/01/25/watering-hole-deploys-new-macos-malware-dazzlespy-asia/))

### Lazarus Group - Operation Dream Job (续)
**描述**：在[Operation Dream Job](https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0022)行动中，[Lazarus Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0032)在被攻陷主机上的文档中搜索与安全和财务相关的关键词。(引用来源: [ClearSky Lazarus Aug 2020](https://www.clearskysec.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/Dream-Job-Campaign.pdf))

### Dyre
**描述**：[Dyre](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0024)能够识别被攻陷主机上的用户。(引用来源: [Malwarebytes Dyreza November 2015](https://blog.malwarebytes.com/threat-analysis/2015/11/a-technical-look-at-dyreza/))

### Milan
**描述**：[Milan](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S1015)可以使用COM组件生成计划任务。(引用来源: [ClearSky Siamesekitten August 2021](https://www.clearskysec.com/siamesekitten/))

### Donut
**描述**：[Donut](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0695)可以生成代码模块，实现VBScript、JScript、EXE、DLL和dotNET载荷的内存执行。(引用来源: [Donut Github](https://github.com/TheWover/donut))

### FIN5
**描述**：(引用来源: [DarkReading FireEye FIN5 Oct 2015](https://www.darkreading.com/analytics/prolific-cybercrime-gang-favors-legit-login-credentials/d/d-id/1322645?); [Mandiant FIN5 GrrCON Oct 2016](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fevGZs0EQu8))

### Empire
**描述**：[Empire](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0363)主要利用PowerShell执行其客户端代理任务。此外，[Empire](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0363)还包含通过Invoke-PSRemoting模块进行PowerShell远程操作的能力。(引用来源: [Github PowerShell Empire](https://github.com/PowerShellEmpire/Empire); [NCSC Joint Report Public Tools](https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/report/joint-report-on-publicly-available-hacking-tools))

### Catchamas
**描述**：[Catchamas](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0261)将从机器上收集的数据存储在.db文件和.bmp文件中，并分布在四个不同的位置。(引用来源: [Symantec Catchamas April 2018](https://www-west.symantec.com/content/symantec/english/en/security-center/writeup.html/2018-040209-1742-99))
