### Interview Guide and Codebook

#### Mobile Data and Phone Details
1. **Q1:** What type of phone do you own and use? How long have you had this device?
2. **Q2:** What mobile data plan do you have? Why? How long have you been on this plan?
3. **Q3:** Which activities do you most commonly use your phone for? Why? When? How often?
4. **Q4:** Which social media sites do you most often visit online? Why? When? How often?

#### Social Media General Usage Patterns
5. **Q5:** When did you start using [social media platform]? Why? What do you like about it? What do you dislike about it?
6. **Q6:** How are your contacts on the app? How many do you think you have? Are there any contacts you did not want to add? Why?
7. **Q7:** Do you use [social media platform] on your mobile phone only? Where else do you use it? Why?
8. **Q8:** Is there anything you avoid doing on social media? What? Why?
9. **Q9:** Is there anything you do on social media that you do not do on other apps? What?
10. **Q10:** Have you ever considered not using [social media platform] anymore? Why or why not?
11. **Q11:** Have you ever heard criticisms of [social media platform], or other social media apps? What were they? Have they affected you? Have these affected your usage in any way?
12. **Q12:** Which of the social media apps you use do you believe is most compatible with your views on privacy?

#### Privacy on Social Media
13. **Q13:** How much do you trust the company that made [social media platform]? Why or why not?
14. **Q14:** Who do you think can see your profile on [social media platform]?
15. **Q15:** Have you thought about who can see what you’re sharing/posting on [social media platform]?
   - **Q15.1:** Who do you think can see the content you share?
   - **Q15.2:** Who do you share with most/least often? Why?
   - **Q15.3:** Do you share more or less on some apps? Why?
   - **Q15.4:** Have you ever changed what you post to maintain your privacy? What did you do? Why?
   - **Q15.5:** Have you changed who can see what you post to maintain your privacy? What did you do? Why?
16. **Q16:** Have you thought about who can see what posts/content/videos you are seeing on a social media platform?
   - **Q16.1:** Who else do you think can see what you see on your apps?
   - **Q16.2:** Tell me if anyone has ever blocked you from seeing content? Why?
   - **Q16.3:** Have you ever seen content you did not want to see? What did you do to stop it?
17. **Q17:** Have you ever shared your password for [social media platform]? Why or why not?
18. **Q18:** Have you ever shared your phone with someone else?
   - **Q18.1:** Do you think that the person who used/uses your phone can see what you do on [social media platform]? How does that make you feel?
   - **Q18.2:** Do you change what you do on the [social media platform] because you share your phone?
19. **Q19:** Tell me about whether you think [social media platform] collects any information about you?
   - **Q19.1:** What information do you think they collect?
   - **Q19.2:** Why do you think they collect this information?
20. **Q20:** Are you aware of any settings on any of the social media platforms [social media platform] that you use that can change who can see what you post?
   - **Q20.1:** For each app, have you ever used these settings?
   - **Q20.2:** How/When/Why did you use them?

#### Privacy in General
21. **Q21:** What does privacy on the Internet mean to you?
22. **Q22:** How do you maintain your privacy when you go on the Internet? Does it differ based on device?
23. **Q23:** Are there any tools you use to help you keep private on the Internet? What? When/why do you use them? Which devices do you use these tools on?
24. **Q24:** Tell me about some instances in which you felt your privacy was breached on one of your social media apps.
   - **Q24.1:** Which app? What happened? Why do you think it happened? Did you take any measures to prevent this from happening again?
25. **Q25:** Tell me about how your privacy practices differ depending on which application you are using?
26. **Q26:** Are you aware of privacy settings in any of the social media apps you use? Can you tell me more about how you use them on each app? Can you show me these settings?
