**ATT&CK ID:** S0079  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** MobileOrder

**Description:**
MobileOrder includes a command that uploads specific information from the victim's mobile device to its Command and Control (C2) server. This information includes:
- SD card size
- List of installed applications
- SMS content
- Contacts
- Call history
