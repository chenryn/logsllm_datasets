**Title: Black Hat Europe 2019 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways**

As the conference draws to a close, we invite you to join Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss and distinguished members of the Black Hat Review Board for an in-depth discussion on the most critical issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community. This closing session, known as the "Locknote," will feature a candid and insightful conversation on the key takeaways from Black Hat Europe 2019. The panel will explore how these emerging trends and insights will shape future InfoSec strategies and address the challenges ahead.
