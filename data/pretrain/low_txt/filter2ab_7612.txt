**ATT&CK ID:** T1539  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Steal Web Session Cookie

An adversary may steal session cookies from web applications or services to gain unauthorized access as an authenticated user, bypassing the need for valid credentials. Many web applications and services utilize session cookies as authentication tokens after a user has successfully logged in. By obtaining these cookies, an attacker can impersonate the legitimate user and perform actions within the application or service as if they were the authenticated user.
