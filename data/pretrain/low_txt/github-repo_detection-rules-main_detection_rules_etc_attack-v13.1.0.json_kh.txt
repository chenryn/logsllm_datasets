**SolarWinds Compromise**

在[SolarWinds Compromise](https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0024)期间，[APT29](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0016)通过攻陷云解决方案合作伙伴的账户获得了访问权限，并利用Mimecast签发的被篡改证书对Mimecast客户系统进行身份验证。(引用: [Cybersecurity Advisory SVR TTP May 2021](https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/files/Advisory-further-TTPs-associated-with-SVR-cyber-actors.pdf), [CrowdStrike StellarParticle January 2022](https://www.crowdstrike.com/blog/observations-from-the-stellarparticle-campaign/))

**Ke3chang Malware Communication**

[Ke3chang](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0004)的恶意软件，包括RoyalCli和BS2005，通过使用COM接口IWebBrowser2与C2服务器通过HTTP进行通信。(引用: [NCC Group APT15 Alive and Strong](https://research.nccgroup.com/2018/03/10/apt15-is-alive-and-strong-an-analysis-of-royalcli-and-royaldns/), [Microsoft NICKEL December 2021](https://www.microsoft.com/security/blog/2021/12/06/nickel-targeting-government-organizations-across-latin-america-and-europe))

**Operation Wocao Lateral Movement**

在[Operation Wocao](https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0014)中，威胁行为者使用了[Impacket](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0357)的smbexec.py脚本以及访问C$和IPC$共享来进行横向移动。(引用: [FoxIT Wocao December 2019](https://www.fox-it.com/media/kadlze5c/201912_report_operation_wocao.pdf))

**APT28 Brute Force Attacks**

[APT28](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0007)使用了一种支持两种模式的暴力破解/密码喷射工具：在暴力破解模式下，它通常每小时针对每个目标账户发送超过300次认证尝试，持续数小时或数天。(引用: [Microsoft STRONTIUM New Patterns Cred Harvesting Sept 2020](https://www.microsoft.com/security/blog/2020/09/10/strontium-detecting-new-patters-credential-harvesting/), [Cybersecurity Advisory GRU Brute Force Campaign July 2021](https://media.defense.gov/2021/Jul/01/2002753896/-1/-1/1/CSA_GRU_GLOBAL_BRUTE_FORCE_CAMPAIGN_UOO158036-21.PDF))
