**ATT&CK ID:** G0102  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Wizard Spider

**Description:**
Wizard Spider has utilized Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) and Lightweight Directory Access Protocol (LDAP) queries for network discovery and lateral movement.[133][134][135][136]
