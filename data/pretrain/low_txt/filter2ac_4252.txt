**ATT&CK ID:** T1195.001  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Compromise Software Dependencies and Development Tools

Adversaries may compromise software dependencies and development tools before they reach the end user, with the intent of compromising data or systems. Many applications rely on external software to function correctly. Popular open-source projects, which are often used as dependencies in numerous applications, can be targeted as a means to inject malicious code into the systems of users who depend on these projects.
