**ATT&CK ID:** S0498  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Cryptographic Deletion

**Description:**
The technique known as "Cryptographic Deletion" (S0498) enables adversaries to delete files from a compromised host. This method can be used to remove evidence of malicious activity or to disrupt the normal operation of the system.
