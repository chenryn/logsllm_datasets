## What is JTAG?
- **Joint Test Action Group (JTAG)**
- **Test Access Ports (TAPs) for Collecting Raw Data from Memory Chips**
- **Not Chip-Off or ISP**
- **Extreme Physical Data Acquisition**
- **Advanced Technique Requiring Soldering and De-soldering**
