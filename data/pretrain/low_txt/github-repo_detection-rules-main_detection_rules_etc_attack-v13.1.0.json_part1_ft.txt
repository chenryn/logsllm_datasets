**InvisiMole (S0260)**
- **功能描述**: InvisiMole 能够收集网络共享信息。
- **参考文献**: Hromcová, Z. (2018, June 7). InvisiMole: Surprisingly equipped spyware, undercover since 2013. Retrieved July 10, 2018.
- **链接**: [ESET InvisiMole June 2018](https://www.welivesecurity.com/2018/06/07/invisimole-equipped-spyware-undercover/)

**安全系统与登录信息关联**
- **描述**: 将其他安全系统与登录信息进行关联。

**PlugX (S0013)**
- **功能描述**: PlugX 可以配置为使用 DNS 进行命令和控制。
- **参考文献**: Dell SecureWorks Counter Threat Unit Threat Intelligence. (2015, August 5). Threat Group-3390 Targets Organizations for Cyberespionage. Retrieved August 18, 2018.
- **链接**: [Dell TG-3390](https://www.secureworks.com/research/threat-group-3390-targets-organizations-for-cyberespionage)

**Thrip (G0076)**
- **功能描述**: Thrip 利用 PowerShell 执行命令下载载荷，遍历受感染的网络，并进行侦察。
- **参考文献**: Security Response Attack Investigation Team. (2018, June 19). Thrip: Espionage Group Hits Satellite, Telecoms, and Defense Companies. Retrieved July 10, 2018.
- **链接**: [Symantec Thrip June 2018](https://www.symantec.com/blogs/threat-intelligence/thrip-hits-satellite-telecoms-defense-targets)

**KOCTOPUS (S0669)**
- **功能描述**: KOCTOPUS 使用 `wmic.exe` 和 `find` 命令检查操作系统版本。
- **参考文献**: Jazi, H. (2021, February). LazyScripter: From Empire to double RAT. Retrieved November 24, 2021.
- **链接**: [MalwareBytes LazyScripter Feb 2021](https://www.malwarebytes.com/resources/files/2021/02/lazyscripter.pdf)

**Bazar (S0534)**
- **功能描述**: Bazar 可以识别受感染用户的用户名。
- **参考文献**: Pantazopoulos, N. (2020, June 2). In-depth analysis of the new Team9 malware family. Retrieved December 1, 2020.
- **链接**: [NCC Group Team9 June 2020](https://research.nccgroup.com/2020/06/02/in-depth-analysis-of-the-new-team9-malware-family/)

**T9000 (S0098)**
- **功能描述**: T9000 在可移动存储设备上搜索具有预定义文件扩展名（如 *.doc, *.ppt, *.xls, *.docx, *.pptx, *.xlsx）的文件。匹配的文件将被加密并写入本地用户目录。
- **参考文献**: Grunzweig, J. and Miller-Osborn, J.. (2016, February 4). T9000: Advanced Modular Backdoor Uses Complex Anti-Analysis Techniques. Retrieved April 15, 2016.
- **链接**: [Palo Alto T9000 Feb 2016](http://researchcenter.paloaltonetworks.com/2016/02/t9000-advanced-modular-backdoor-uses-complex-anti-analysis-techniques/)

**Pteranodon (S0147)**
- **功能描述**: Pteranodon 将自身复制到启动文件夹以建立持久性。
- **参考文献**: Kasza, A. and Reichel, D. (2017, February 27). The Gamaredon Group Toolset Evolution. Retrieved March 1, 2017.
- **链接**: [Palo Alto Gamaredon Feb 2017](https://researchcenter.paloaltonetworks.com/2017/02/unit-42-title-gamaredon-group-toolset-evolution/)

**BlackTech (G0098)**
- **功能描述**: BlackTech 使用带有恶意链接的电子邮件来诱使受害者安装恶意软件。
- **参考文献**: Bermejo, L., et al. (2017, June 22). Following the Trail of BlackTech’s Cyber Espionage Campaigns. Retrieved May 5, 2020.
- **链接**: [TrendMicro BlackTech June 2017](https://blog.trendmicro.com/trendlabs-security-intelligence/following-trail-blacktech-cyber-espionage-campaigns/)

**APT39 (G0087)**
- **功能描述**: APT39 使用 PowerShell 执行恶意代码。
- **参考文献**:
  - Rusu, B. (2020, May 21). Iranian Chafer APT Targeted Air Transportation and Government in Kuwait and Saudi Arabia. Retrieved May 22, 2020.
  - Symantec. (2018, February 28). Chafer: Latest Attacks Reveal Heightened Ambitions. Retrieved May 22, 2020.
- **链接**:
  - [BitDefender Chafer May 2020](https://labs.bitdefender.com/2020/05/iranian-chafer-apt-targeted-air-transportation-and-government-in-kuwait-and-saudi-arabia/)
  - [Symantec Chafer February 2018](https://symantec-enterprise-blogs.security.com/blogs/threat-intelligence/chafer-latest-attacks-reveal-heightened-ambitions)

**XCSSET (S0658)**
- **功能描述**: XCSSET 使用 RC4 加密通过 TCP 与 C2 服务器通信。
- **参考文献**: Mac Threat Response, Mobile Research Team. (2020, August 13). The XCSSET Malware: Inserts Malicious Code Into Xcode Projects, Performs UXSS Backdoor Planting in Safari, and Leverages Two Zero-day Exploits. Retrieved October 5, 2021.
- **链接**: [trendmicro xcsset xcode project 2020](https://documents.trendmicro.com/assets/pdf/XCSSET_Technical_Brief.pdf)

**2016年乌克兰电力攻击 (C0025)**
- **描述**: 在 2016 年乌克兰电力攻击中，Sandworm Team (G0034) 将可执行文件伪装成 `.txt` 文件。
- **参考文献**: Joe Slowik. (2018, October 12). Anatomy of an Attack: Detecting and Defeating CRASHOVERRIDE. Retrieved December 18, 2020.
- **链接**: [Dragos Crashoverride 2018](https://www.dragos.com/wp-content/uploads/CRASHOVERRIDE2018.pdf)

**Attor (S0438)**
- **功能描述**: Attor 具有一个插件，能够使用可用的输入声音设备录制音频。
- **参考文献**: Hromcova, Z. (2019, October). AT COMMANDS, TOR-BASED COMMUNICATIONS: MEET ATTOR, A FANTASY CREATURE AND ALSO A SPY PLATFORM. Retrieved May 6, 2020.
- **链接**: [ESET Attor Oct 2019](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/ESET_Attor.pdf)
