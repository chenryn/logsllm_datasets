## Blockchain Security Challenges in CTFs
!!! note
    Note: Blockchain challenges, particularly those involving smart contracts and public blockchains, are not as common in CTFs. This list includes a comprehensive collection of such challenges, along with their write-ups. The list is not exhaustive and will be updated periodically. For more challenges, visit the [ctf-challenges/blockchain](https://github.com/ctf-wiki/ctf-challenges/tree/master/blockchain) repository or practice online at [ChainFlag](https://chainflag.org/).
