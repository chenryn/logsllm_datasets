**ATT&CK ID:** T1078.002  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Domain Accounts

Adversaries may obtain and misuse credentials of a domain account to gain initial access, establish persistence, escalate privileges, or evade defenses. Domain accounts are managed by Active Directory Domain Services (AD DS) and are used to configure access and permissions across systems and services within the domain. These accounts can include user accounts, administrator accounts, and service accounts.
