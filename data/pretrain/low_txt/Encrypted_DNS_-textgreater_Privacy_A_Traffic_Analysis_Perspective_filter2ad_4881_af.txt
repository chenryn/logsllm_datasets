#### B. Performance Metrics
In this section, we provide an overview of our classifier evaluation metrics. We use standard metrics to evaluate the performance of our classifier: Precision, Recall, and F1-Score. These metrics are computed per class, where each class represents a webpage. For each class, we treat the classification as a "one vs. all" binary classification: we label samples that belong to that class as "positives" and those that belong to other classes as "negatives."

- **Precision** is the ratio of true positives to the total number of samples classified as positive (true positives + false positives).
- **Recall** is the ratio of true positives to the total number of actual positives (true positives + false negatives).
- **F1-Score** is the harmonic mean of precision and recall, providing a balanced measure of both.

These metrics help us understand the effectiveness of our classifier in distinguishing between different webpages.
