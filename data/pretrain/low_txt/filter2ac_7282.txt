Rumors have circulated about remote vulnerabilities that can exploit the iPhone without any user interaction. However, detailed technical information about these attacks on modern devices remains scarce. This presentation delves into the remote, interaction-less attack surface of iOS, examining potential vulnerabilities in SMS, MMS, Visual Voicemail, iMessage, and Mail. It provides guidance on setting up the necessary tools to test these components and includes two case studies of vulnerabilities discovered through this approach.
