**ATT&CK ID:** M1040  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Behavior Prevention on Endpoint

On Windows 10, enable Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) rules to prevent the execution of processes created by WMI (Windows Management Instrumentation) commands. It is important to note, however, that many legitimate tools and applications also use WMI for command execution. Therefore, careful configuration and testing are recommended to avoid disrupting normal operations.
