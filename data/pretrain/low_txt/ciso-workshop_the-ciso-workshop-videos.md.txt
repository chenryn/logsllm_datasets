# Video Page

The CISO workshop offers comprehensive guidance and strategies for securing a 'hybrid of everything' technical environment, including on-premises, multi-cloud, IoT, and OT systems.

## Introduction and Overview

This introductory video sets the stage for the CISO workshop, giving an overview of its content.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50rY5]

## Part A - Key Context and Fundamentals

### Threat Environment and Trends
Explore the evolving threat landscape and the challenges posed by ransomware and "as a service" models. Learn how to keep security in step with business and technology transformations.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50rYg]

### Roles & Responsibilities
Understand the changing roles and responsibilities in security, and how they align with the broader organizational goals.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50XM1]

### Strategy and Recommended Initiatives
Discover the Zero Trust Transformation and modern security strategies that align with business, digital, and cloud transformations. This video outlines five strategic initiatives to modernize your security program using Zero Trust principles, while avoiding extreme approaches that can increase risk.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50zLL]

## Part B - Business Alignment

### Engaging Business Leaders on Security
Learn effective communication strategies to engage business leaders in security discussions, using a role-playing approach to bridge the gap between security and business priorities.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50F9t]

### Risk Insights
Align security priorities with business goals and existing risk management frameworks. This video covers the dual goal of enabling business and reducing risk, and the various sources of cybersecurity threats.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50XNA]

### Security Integration
Integrate security into IT and business processes, and structure collaboration between security functions. This includes insights into security posture management, a critical discipline for reducing risk.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50zM5]

### Business Resilience
Focus on business resilience as the ultimate goal of security programs, balancing investments to reduce business impact before, during, and after attacks.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50SQv]

### Maturity Model - Business Alignment
Review maturity models that describe the journey to improve risk insights, security integration, and business resilience, with specific actions to help you advance.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50zM8]

## Part C - Security Disciplines

### Access Control
Discuss the Zero Trust approach to access control, including strong authentication, blending identity and network access, and the Known-Trusted-Allowed model.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50SQQ]

### Security Operations
Explore modern security operations, key success metrics, and the importance of cultural elements in security.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50XOt]

### Asset Protection
Understand the key imperatives for managing and securing assets, prioritizing based on business criticality and scaling efficiently.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50XOu]

### Security Governance
Modernize security governance by bridging business goals and technology. This video covers various components of security governance, including risk, compliance, and threat intelligence.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50XOv]

### Innovation Security
Evolve application security into a modern approach, including DevSecOps, and focus on key areas to drive the success of this capability.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50PhV]

### Maturity Model - Security Governance
Review the maturity model for improving security architecture, posture management, and IT security maintenance, with actionable steps to elevate these disciplines.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50XO6]

### Summary & Next Steps
Conclude the workshop with key takeaways and next steps to implement the discussed strategies.
> [!VIDEO https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE50Pia]
