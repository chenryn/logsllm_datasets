**ATT&CK ID:** S1063  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Brute Ratel C4

**Description:**
Brute Ratel C4 has been observed using Microsoft Word icons to disguise malicious LNK (Shortcut) files. This technique aims to trick users into executing the malicious files by making them appear as legitimate Word documents.
