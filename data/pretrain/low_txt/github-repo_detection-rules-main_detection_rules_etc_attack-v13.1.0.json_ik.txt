### SombRAT
**描述**：SombRAT（[MITRE ATT&CK Software: S0615](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0615)）可以通过执行 `getinfo` 命令来发现被入侵主机的当前时间。
- **引用来源**：
  - [BlackBerry Research and Intelligence Team. (2020, November 12). The CostaRicto Campaign: Cyber-Espionage Outsourced. Retrieved May 24, 2021.](https://blogs.blackberry.com/en/2020/11/the-costaricto-campaign-cyber-espionage-outsourced)
  - [CISA. (2021, May 6). Analysis Report (AR21-126A) FiveHands Ransomware. Retrieved June 7, 2021.](https://us-cert.cisa.gov/ncas/analysis-reports/ar21-126a)

### Cobalt Group
**描述**：Cobalt Group（[MITRE ATT&CK Groups: G0080](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0080)）通过创建Windows任务来建立持久性。
- **引用来源**：
  - [Matveeva, V. (2017, August 15). Secrets of Cobalt. Retrieved October 10, 2018.](https://www.group-ib.com/blog/cobalt)

### Trojan.Mebromi
**描述**：Trojan.Mebromi（[MITRE ATT&CK Software: S0001](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0001)）可以修改BIOS，并能够下载和执行文件，同时保护自己免于被移除。
- **引用来源**：
  - [Ge, L. (2011, September 9). BIOS Threat is Showing up Again!. Retrieved November 14, 2014.](http://www.symantec.com/connect/blogs/bios-threat-showing-again)

### LightNeuron
**描述**：LightNeuron（[MITRE ATT&CK Software: S0395](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0395)）通过嵌入在PDF和JPG中的隐写命令进行控制。
- **引用来源**：
  - [Faou, M. (2019, May). Turla LightNeuron: One email away from remote code execution. Retrieved June 24, 2019.](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/ESET-LightNeuron.pdf)

### Stuxnet
**描述**：Stuxnet（[MITRE ATT&CK Software: S0603](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0603)）枚举与多种安全产品相关的当前运行进程。
- **引用来源**：
  - [Nicolas Falliere, Liam O Murchu, Eric Chien. (2011, February). W32.Stuxnet Dossier (Version 1.4). Retrieved. 2017/09/22.](https://www.wired.com/images_blogs/threatlevel/2011/02/Symantec-Stuxnet-Update-Feb-2011.pdf)

### WIRTE
**描述**：WIRTE（[MITRE ATT&CK Groups: G0090](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0090)）使用HTTP进行网络通信。
- **引用来源**：
  - [S2 Grupo. (2019, April 2). WIRTE Group attacking the Middle East. Retrieved May 24, 2019.](https://lab52.io/blog/wirte-group-attacking-the-middle-east/)

### IceApple
**描述**：IceApple（[MITRE ATT&CK Software: S1022](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S1022)）是一个IIS后利用框架，由18个模块组成，提供多种功能。
- **引用来源**：
  - [CrowdStrike. (2022, May). ICEAPPLE: A NOVEL INTERNET INFORMATION SERVICES (IIS) POST-EXPLOITATION FRAMEWORK. Retrieved June 27, 2022.](https://www.crowdstrike.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/crowdstrike-iceapple-a-novel-internet-information-services-post-exploitation-framework.pdf)

### Backdoor.Oldrea
**描述**：Backdoor.Oldrea（[MITRE ATT&CK Software: S0093](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0093)）收集有关正在运行的进程的信息。
- **引用来源**：
  - [Symantec Security Response. (2014, June 30). Dragonfly: Cyberespionage Attacks Against Energy Suppliers. Retrieved April 8, 2016.](https://community.broadcom.com/symantecenterprise/communities/community-home/librarydocuments/viewdocument?DocumentKey=7382dce7-0260-4782-84cc-890971ed3f17&CommunityKey=1ecf5f55-9545-44d6-b0f4-4e4a7f5f5e68&tab=librarydocuments)

### MuddyWater
**描述**：MuddyWater（[MITRE ATT&CK Groups: G0069](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0069)）在钓鱼邮件中分发链接到诱饵文档的URL。
- **引用来源**：
  - [Mele, G. et al. (2021, February 10). Probable Iranian Cyber Actors, Static Kitten, Conducting Cyberespionage Campaign Targeting UAE and Kuwait Government Agencies. Retrieved March 17, 2021.](https://www.anomali.com/blog/probable-iranian-cyber-actors-static-kitten-conducting-cyberespionage-campaign-targeting-uae-and-kuwait-government-agencies)
  - [Peretz, A. and Theck, E. (2021, March 5). Earth Vetala – MuddyWater Continues to Target Organizations in the Middle East. Retrieved March 18, 2021.](https://www.trendmicro.com/en_us/research/21/c/earth-)

### Patchwork
**描述**：Patchwork（[MITRE ATT&CK Groups: G0040](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0040)）使用AES加密收集的文件路径，然后使用Base64进行编码。
- **引用来源**：
  - [Lunghi, D., et al. (2017, December). Untangling the Patchwork Cyberespionage Group. Retrieved July 10, 2018.](https://documents.trendmicro.com/assets/tech-brief-untangling-the-patchwork-cyberespionage-group.pdf)
