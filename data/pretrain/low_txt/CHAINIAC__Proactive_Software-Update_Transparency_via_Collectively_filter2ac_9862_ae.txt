### Evaluation of Skipchains on Real-World Data

To evaluate the effectiveness of skipchains, we conducted experiments using real-world data from Debian. Additionally, we replayed 30 days of actual client requests to the Python Package Index (PyPI) repository. Our results demonstrate that the use of skipchains significantly limits the verification overhead.
