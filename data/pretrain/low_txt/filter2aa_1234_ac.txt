# DEFCON XVIII: Cryptography Challenge

## Introduction
Welcome to the DEFCON XVIII cryptography challenge. This document outlines a series of steps and clues to help you uncover a secret message. The challenge involves deciphering bar codes, using the Fibonacci sequence, and converting numeric values back into text.

## What About Those Bar Codes?
- **Common Sequence**: 
  - [x]1123581321[y]
  - 1 – 1 – 2 – 3 – 5 – 8 – 13 – 21
  - This is the Fibonacci Series.
- **First Eight Terms**:
  - The first eight terms of the Fibonacci Series are used in this challenge.
- **Eight Badges**:
  - There are eight badges involved in the challenge.
- **Eight Indexes (Indices)**:
  - These indices will be used to extract specific characters or values from the given data.
- **Next Steps**:
  - Think about how a shift register might be used to process these values.
