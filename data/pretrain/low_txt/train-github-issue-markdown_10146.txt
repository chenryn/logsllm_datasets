When using a `navbar-fixed-top` with items aligned to the right (`navbar-right`), you may notice that the last item extends 15px beyond the container's edge. This issue can be observed in the example provided on the Bootstrap website: [Navbar Fixed Top Example](http://getbootstrap.com/examples/navbar-fixed-top/).

To address this, you can apply custom CSS to adjust the positioning or padding of the last item, ensuring it aligns properly within the container.
