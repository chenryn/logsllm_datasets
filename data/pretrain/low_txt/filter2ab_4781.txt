**Title: Black Hat Asia 2019 - Decisions and Revisions: The Ever-Evolving Face of the Black Hat NOC**

In this session, you will have the unique opportunity to delve into the inner workings of the Black Hat Network Operations Center (NOC). We will guide you through our decision-making process for selecting and deploying the equipment and services that power the Black Hat network. You will gain insights into the rationale behind these choices, as well as the adjustments we make when setting up and securing networks in the U.S., Europe, and Asia.

We will also explore the behavioral differences observed among attendees at these events, highlighting both positive and negative trends. Additionally, we will share compelling stories and statistics from this year's activities, providing a comprehensive debrief on what has changed since previous shows and what these changes signify for the broader industry. 

Join us for an in-depth look at the dynamic and evolving nature of the Black Hat NOC.
