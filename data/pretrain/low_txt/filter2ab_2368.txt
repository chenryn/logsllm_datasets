**ATT&CK ID:** S0002  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Mimikatz

**Description:**

Mimikatz is a post-exploitation tool that performs credential dumping to obtain account and password information. This information is valuable for gaining unauthorized access to additional systems and enterprise network resources. Mimikatz offers a wide range of functionalities to extract credentials, including from the Data Protection API (DPAPI). [49][50][51][52]
