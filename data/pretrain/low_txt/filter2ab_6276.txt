**ATT&CK ID:** T1629  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Impair Defenses

Adversaries may maliciously modify components of a victim's environment to hinder or disable defensive mechanisms. This technique targets both preventative defenses, such as antivirus software, and detection capabilities that defenders use to audit activity and identify malicious behavior. The impairment can affect both native defenses and supplemental security tools installed by users or mobile endpoint administrators.
