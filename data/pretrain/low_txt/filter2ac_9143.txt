**ATT&CK ID:** T1078.003  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Local Accounts

Adversaries may exploit local account credentials to gain initial access, establish persistence, escalate privileges, or evade defenses. Local accounts are typically configured by an organization for use by users, remote support personnel, services, or administrators on a single system or service. These accounts can be targeted and abused to facilitate unauthorized activities within the network.
