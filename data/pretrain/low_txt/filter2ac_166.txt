**ATT&CK ID:** G0082  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT38

APT38 has been known to employ `rundll32.exe` for executing binaries, scripts, and Control Panel Item (CPL) files. This method is also used to execute code through a proxy, thereby evading detection by security tools.[15]
