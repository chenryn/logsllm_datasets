**ATT&CK ID:** S0137  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** CORESHELL

**Description:**
CORESHELL C2 (Command and Control) messages are encrypted using custom stream ciphers. The encryption keys used in this process are either six bytes or eight bytes in length.[33]
