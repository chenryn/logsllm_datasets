### Operation Wocao
在[Operation Wocao](https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0014)行动中，威胁行为者对远程主机上的会话和用户进行了枚举，并识别出登录到目标系统的特权用户。(引用: [FoxIT Wocao December 2019](https://www.fox-it.com/media/kadlze5c/201912_report_operation_wocao.pdf))

### GALLIUM
[GALLIUM](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0093)使用了似乎仅由该组织独占的位于台湾的服务器。(引用: [Microsoft GALLIUM December 2019](https://www.microsoft.com/security/blog/2019/12/12/gallium-targeting-global-telecom/))

### 网络监控
建议监控网络数据以检测不常见的数据流。对于通常没有网络通信或从未见过的进程进行网络活动的行为应被视为可疑。

### Cuba Ransomware
[Cuba](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0625)包含一个硬编码的服务和进程终止列表。(引用: [McAfee Cuba April 2021](https://www.mcafee.com/enterprise/en-us/assets/reports/rp-cuba-ransomware.pdf))

### LightNeuron
[LightNeuron](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0395)使用AES-256对其配置文件进行加密。(引用: [ESET LightNeuron May 2019](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/ESET-LightNeuron.pdf))

### AuTo Stealer
[AuTo Stealer](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S1029)能够根据已安装的安全软件类型，将恶意可执行文件放置在受害者的AutoRun注册表键或StartUp目录中，以此来维持持久性。(引用: [MalwareBytes SideCopy Dec 2021](https://www.malwarebytes.com/blog/news/2021/12/sidecopy-apt-connecting-lures-to-victims-payloads-to-infrastructure))

### Operation Spalax
在[Operation Spalax](https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0005)行动期间，攻击者依赖受害者打开PDF文档并点击嵌入其中的恶意链接来下载恶意软件。(引用: [ESET Operation Spalax Jan 2021](https://www.welivesecurity.com/2021/01/12/operation-spalax-targeted-malware-attacks-colombia/))

### FlawedAmmyy
[FlawedAmmyy](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0381)通过`HKCU\SOFTWARE\microsoft\windows\currentversion\run`注册表键建立了持久性。(引用: [Korean FSI TA505 2020](https://www.fsec.or.kr/user/bbs/fsec/163/344/bbsDataView/1382.do?page=1&column=&search=&searchSDate=&searchEDate=&bbsDataCategory=))

### SLOTHFULMEDIA
[SLOTHFULMEDIA](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0533)能够在受害机器上下载文件。(引用: [CISA MAR SLOTHFULMEDIA October 2020](https://us-cert.cisa.gov/ncas/analysis-reports/ar20-275a))

### Crutch
[Crutch](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0538)能够自动将窃取的文件外泄至Dropbox。(引用: [ESET Crutch December 2020](https://www.welivesecurity.com/2020/12/02/turla-crutch-keeping-back-door-open/))

### Ramsay
[Ramsay](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0458)曾通过恶意电子邮件附件被激活。(引用: [Antiy CERT Ramsay April 2020](https://www.programmersought.com/article/62493896999/))

### Sibot
[Sibot](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0589)曾通过MSHTA应用程序被执行。(引用: [MSTIC NOBELIUM Mar 2021](https://www.microsoft.com/security/blog/2021/03/04/goldmax-goldfinder-sibot-analyzing-nobelium-malware/))

### BoomBox
[BoomBox](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0635)可以将数据上传到为每个受害者设置的专用Dropbox文件夹中。(引用: [MSTIC Nobelium Toolset May 2021](https://www.microsoft.com/security/blog/2021/05/28/breaking-down-nobeliums-latest-early-stage-toolset/))
