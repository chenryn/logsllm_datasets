### The Six-Year-Old Hacker: References and Resources

#### Educational Theories and Philosophies
- **Jean Piaget**:
  - [Indiana University: Cognitive Development](http://education.indiana.edu/~p540/webcourse/develop.html)
  - [Ship Education: Piaget's Theory of Cognitive Development](http://www.ship.edu/~cgboeree/piaget.html)

- **Maria Montessori**:
  - [Montessori Education](http://www.montessori.edu/)
  - [American Montessori Society](http://www.montessori.org/)
  - **Stoll Lillard, Angeline**: *Montessori: The Science Behind the Genius* (Oxford University Press, 2005)

- **Seymour Papert**:
  - [Papert.org](http://www.papert.org/)
  - *Mindstorms: Children, Computers, and Powerful Ideas* (Basic Books, 1999)
  - *Constructionism Research Reports and Essays, 1985-1990* (Greenwood Pub Group, 1991)

#### Programming Resources
- **LOGO Foundation**:
  - [MIT Media Lab: LOGO Foundation](http://el.media.mit.edu/logo-foundation/)

- **UCB LOGO**:
  - [UC Berkeley: UCB LOGO](http://www.cs.berkeley.edu/~bh/logo.html)
  - This version runs on multiple operating systems and is a good source for serious LOGO programming texts.

- **Windows Version of LOGO**:
  - [Softronix: Windows LOGO](http://www.softronix.com/logo.html)
  - Derived from UCB LOGO, this version runs well under Wine and provides useful links.

- **Programming Projects**:
  - **Dewdney, A. K.**:
    - *Armchair Universe: An Exploration of Computer Worlds* (W. H. Freeman & Co., 1988)
    - *The Tinkertoy Computer and Other Machinations: Computer Recreations...* (W. H. Freeman & Co., 1993)
  - These books offer excellent resources for programming projects, including Life and CoreWar!

This structured format should make it easier to navigate and find the relevant resources.
