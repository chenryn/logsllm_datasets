### Testing Functionality with the Built-in Blink Sketch

To test the functionality using the built-in Blink sketch, follow these steps:

1. **Connect the Jumper Wire:**
   - Attach a jumper wire from location PB5 on the left side of the Gertboard to one of the buffered LED outputs. (BUF6 is commonly used for this purpose.)

2. **Upload the Blink Sketch:**
   - Open the Arduino IDE.
   - Navigate to `File` > `Examples` > `01.Basics` > `Blink`.
   - Click the `Upload` button (or `File` > `Upload using Programmer`) to send the sketch to the ATmega chip.

3. **Observe the LED:**
   - You should see the BUF6 LED begin to blink slowly.
