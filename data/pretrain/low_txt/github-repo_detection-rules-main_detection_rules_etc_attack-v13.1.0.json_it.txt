### 操作Wocao

在[操作Wocao](https://attack.mitre.org/campaigns/C0014)中，威胁行为者使用了一个Visual Basic脚本来检查互联网连接状态。(引用: [FoxIT Wocao December 2019](https://www.fox-it.com/media/kadlze5c/201912_report_operation_wocao.pdf))

### SMOKEDHAM

[SMOKEDHAM](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0649)可以从其C2服务器执行Powershell命令。(引用: [FireEye SMOKEDHAM June 2021](https://www.fireeye.com/blog/threat-research/2021/06/darkside-affiliate-supply-chain-software-compromise.html))

### IcedID

[IcedID](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0483)在其与C2的通信中使用了SSL和TLS协议。(引用: [IBM IcedID November 2017](https://securityintelligence.com/new-banking-trojan-icedid-discovered-by-ibm-x-force-research/) 和 [Juniper IcedID June 2020](https://blogs.juniper.net/en-us/threat-research/covid-19-and-fmla-campaigns-used-to-install-new-icedid-banking-malware))

### Magic Hound

[Magic Hound](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0059)通过RDP在受感染网络内复制工具。(引用: [DFIR Phosphorus November 2021](https://thedfirreport.com/2021/11/15/exchange-exploit-leads-to-domain-wide-ransomware/))

### RunningRAT

[RunningRAT](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0253)包含用于压缩文件的代码。(引用: [McAfee Gold Dragon](https://www.mcafee.com/blogs/other-blogs/mcafee-labs/gold-dragon-widens-olympics-malware-attacks-gains-permanent-presence-on-victims-systems/))

### Flame

[Flame](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0143)可以利用MS10-061漏洞来攻击远程系统中的打印后台处理程序，并在具有共享打印机的系统上进行横向移动。(引用: [Kaspersky Flame](https://securelist.com/the-flame-questions-and-answers-51/34344/) 和 [Kaspersky Flame Functionality](https://securelist.com/flame-bunny-frog-munch-and-beetlejuice-2/32855/))

### ROADTools

[ROADTools](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0684)能够枚举Azure AD应用程序和服务主体。(引用: [Roadtools](https://dirkjanm.io/introducing-roadtools-and-roadrecon-azure-ad-exploration-framework/))

### BRONZE BUTLER

[BRONZE BUTLER](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0060)使用RC4加密（针对Datper恶意软件）和AES（针对xxmm恶意软件）来混淆HTTP流量。该组织还使用了一种名为RarStar的工具，该工具通过自定义XOR算法对数据进行编码后将其发布到C2服务器。(引用: [Secureworks BRONZE BUTLER Oct 2017](https://www.secureworks.com/research/bronze-butler-targets-japanese-businesses))

### RedLeaves

[RedLeaves](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0153)可以通过非标准端口（例如995）使用HTTP进行C2通信。(引用: [PWC Cloud Hopper Technical Annex April 2017](https://www.pwc.co.uk/cyber-security/pdf/pwc-uk-operation-cloud-hopper-technical-annex-april-2017.pdf))

### Pteranodon

[Pteranodon](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0147)会将屏幕截图文件外泄至其C2服务器。(引用: [Palo Alto Gamaredon Feb 2017](https://researchcenter.paloaltonetworks.com/2017/02/unit-42-title-gamaredon-group-toolset-evolution/))

### P8RAT

[P8RAT](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0626)可以检查受感染主机上是否存在与VMware或VirtualBox环境相关的进程。(引用: [Securelist APT10 March 2021](https://securelist.com/apt10-sophisticated-multi-layered-loader-ecipekac-discovered-in-a41apt-campaign/101519/))

### BadPatch

[BadPatch](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0337)使用HTTP进行C2通信。(引用: [Unit 42 BadPatch Oct 2017](https://researchcenter.paloaltonetworks.com/2017/10/unit42-badpatch/))
