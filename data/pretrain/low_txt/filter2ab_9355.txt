**Title: Black Hat US 2022 - The Cyber Safety Review Board: Analyzing Incidents to Drive Systemic Change**

Join Rob Silvers, Undersecretary for Policy at the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) and Chair of the Cyber Safety Review Board, along with Heather Adkins, Deputy Chair and Vice President of Security Engineering at Google, for an in-depth discussion on the inaugural review of the Log4j vulnerability by the Cyber Safety Review Board. During this session, Rob and Heather will:

- Present key findings from the report.
- Discuss how both industry and government can effectively implement the recommendations.
- Explore how the Cyber Safety Review Board is transforming the cybersecurity landscape.

Don't miss this opportunity to gain insights into the collaborative efforts aimed at enhancing cyber resilience and driving systemic change.
