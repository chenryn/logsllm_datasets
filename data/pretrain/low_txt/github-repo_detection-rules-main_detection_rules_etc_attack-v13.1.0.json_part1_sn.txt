### Gold Dragon
**描述：**
Gold Dragon 恶意软件使用 `regkeyenum` 命令枚举注册表键，并获取 `HKEY_CURRENT_USER\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run` 注册表键的信息。[4]

**引用来源：**

### Lazarus Group
**描述：**
Lazarus Group 的恶意软件 IndiaIndia 通过检查 HKCU 和 HKLM 中的注册表键来确定某些应用程序是否存在，包括 SecureCRT、Terminal Services、RealVNC、TightVNC、UltraVNC、Radmin、mRemote、TeamViewer、FileZilla、pcAnyware 和 Remote Desktop。另一个 Lazarus Group 的恶意软件样本会检查以下注册表键的存在：`HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Bitcoin\Bitcoin-Qt`。[6][7][8]

**引用来源：**
6. Novetta Threat Research Group. (2016, February 24). Operation Blockbuster: Loaders, Installers and Uninstallers Report. Retrieved March 2, 2016. [Novetta](https://operationblockbuster.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Operation-Blockbuster-Loaders-Installers-and-Uninstallers-Report.pdf)
7. Novetta Threat Research Group. (2016, February 24). Operation Blockbuster: Unraveling the Long Thread of the Sony Attack. Retrieved February 25, 2016. [Novetta](https://web.archive.org/web/20160226161828/https://www.operationblockbuster.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/02/Operation-Blockbuster-Report.pdf)
