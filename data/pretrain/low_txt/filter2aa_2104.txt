**Encore: Visual Step-by-Step Impressioning:**
- **Uncut Key:**
  - Initial state
- **Key After KJS Treatment:**
  - Cut to the 1-1-1-1-1 position
  - Mark on position 2 (begin counting at the shoulder)
  - Close-up of mark (reflection)
- **Progression:**
  - 12112 (after jiggle)
  - Mark distorts reflection on position 2
  - 13112
  - Again a mark on position 2
  - 14112
  - Yet again, position 2
  - Different angle (the dark spot)
  - The key turns, but only with force
  - Tiny mark on position 5
  - Big crater-mark on position 2
  - Same mark, different angles
  - Working key
  - Cleaned up key (code 15112)

**Note:**
- I have a particular fondness for crater-marks, as they are highly effective in the impressioning process.
