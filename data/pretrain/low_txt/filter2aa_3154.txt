## The Evolution of Bug Bounty Programs

### Introduction
- **Since 2011:** Researching and participating in bug bounty programs.
- **Academy Final Project:** "If you can’t beat them, join them!"

### Key Concepts
- **Bounty Hunter:** A professional who identifies and reports security vulnerabilities.
- **Safe Bug Bounty Program:** Ensures a secure and controlled environment for testing.
- **Virtual Environment:** Simulated environments for safe testing.
- **Not Cost-Effective:** Traditional methods of vulnerability management.

### Targets
- **Politicians**
- **VIPs**
- **Men, Women, Kids, Animals**
- **Hitmen**

### Success Stories
- **Shay Fainberg:** Ideas worth spreading.
- **Oren Hafif (Case Study):** 
  - **Actual Risk:** Exposing all hosted email addresses in Google.
  - **1st Response:** DENIED!
  - **2nd Response:** $500 Reward

### Next Generation Bug Bounty Programs
- **Minimize Production Risks:**
  - **Web Services, Websites, Mobile Apps, WWW**
  - **Allow Penetration Tests**
  - **Prevent Malicious Exploitation**

### Identifying Black Hat Hackers
- **Legitimate SQL Injection:**
  - `SELECT * FROM myTable;`
- **Malicious SQL Injection:**
  - `DROP myTable;#`
  - `DR/**/OP/*bypassing filter*/myTable;#`
  - `' or 'a'<'b';#`
  - `SELECT 0x457578`
  - `SELECT LOAD_FILE(0x633A5C626F6F742E696E69)`
