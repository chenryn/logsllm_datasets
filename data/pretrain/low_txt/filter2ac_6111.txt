**ATT&CK ID:** C0025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack

In the 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack, the Sandworm Team utilized the `xp_cmdshell` command in Microsoft SQL Server (MS-SQL) to execute system-level commands. This technique allowed the attackers to gain deeper control over the compromised systems and further their objectives.
