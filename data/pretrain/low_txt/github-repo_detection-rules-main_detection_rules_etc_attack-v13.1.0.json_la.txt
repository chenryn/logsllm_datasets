**Koadic**
- **描述**: Koadic 是一种恶意软件工具，能够通过从 NTDS 中收集域控制器哈希值来获取密码哈希。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Magius, J., et al. (2017, July 19). Koadic. Retrieved June 18, 2018. https://github.com/zerosum0x0/koadic

**Patchwork**
- **描述**: Patchwork 组织在其合法网站的评论中隐藏了经过 Base64 编码并加密的 C2 服务器位置。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Kaspersky Lab's Global Research & Analysis Team. (2016, July 8). The Dropping Elephant – Aggressive Cyber-Espionage in the Asian Region. Retrieved August 3, 2016. https://securelist.com/the-dropping-elephant-actor/75328/

**APT41 活动 C0017**
- **描述**: 在活动 C0017 中，APT41 将恶意负载下载到被攻陷的系统上。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Rufus Brown, Van Ta, Douglas Bienstock, Geoff Ackerman, John Wolfram. (2022, March 8). Does This Look Infected? A Summary of APT41 Targeting U.S. State Governments. Retrieved July 8, 2022. https://www.mandiant.com/resources/apt41-us-state-governments

**FIN5**
- **描述**: FIN5 组织攻击了酒店和游戏行业，并在全球范围内进行了长达七年的跟踪。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Bromiley, M. and Lewis, P. (2016, October 7). Attacking the Hospitality and Gaming Industries: Tracking an Attacker Around the World in 7 Years. Retrieved October 6, 2017. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fevGZs0EQu8

**RATANKBA**
- **描述**: RATANKBA 运行 `whoami` 和 `query user` 命令来收集信息。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Trend Micro. (2017, February 27). RATANKBA: Delving into Large-scale Watering Holes against Enterprises. Retrieved May 22, 2018. https://www.trendmicro.com/en_us/research/17/b/ratankba-watering-holes-against-enterprises.html

**ShimRatReporter**
- **描述**: ShimRatReporter 使用多个 Windows API 函数从受感染系统中收集信息。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Yonathan Klijnsma. (2016, May 17). Mofang: A Politically Motivated Information Stealing Adversary. Retrieved May 12, 2020. https://foxitsecurity.files.wordpress.com/2016/06/fox-it_mofang_threatreport_tlp-white.pdf

**存储设备内容擦除检测**
- **描述**: 监控新执行的进程，这些进程可能在特定系统或网络中大量擦除存储设备的内容，以中断对系统和网络资源的访问。

**Kevin**
- **描述**: Kevin 将 `cmd.exe` 的图像重命名为一个随机名称，并添加 `.tmpl` 扩展名。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Kayal, A. et al. (2021, October). LYCEUM REBORN: Counterintelligence in the Middle East. Retrieved June 14, 2022. https://vblocalhost.com/uploads/VB2021-Kayal-etal.pdf

**Helminth**
- **描述**: Helminth 使用命令 `net group Exchange Trusted Subsystem /domain` 和 `net group domain admins /domain` 来检查域管理员组和 Exchange Trusted Subsystem 组。[1]
- **引用**: [1] Unit 42. (2017, December 15). Unit 42 Playbook Viewer. Retrieved December 20, 2017. https://pan-unit42.github.io/playbook_viewer/

**HTRAN**
- **描述**: HTRAN 可以代理 TCP 套接字连接，以混淆命令与控制基础设施。[1][2]
- **引用**: [1] Haq, T., Moran, N., Vashisht, S., Scott, M. (2014, September). OPERATION QUANTUM ENTANGLEMENT. Retrieved November 4, 2015. https://www.fireeye.com/content/dam/fireeye-www/global/en/current-threats/pdfs/wp-operation-quantum-entanglement.pdf
- **引用**: [2] The Australian Cyber Security Centre (ACSC), the Canadian Centre for Cyber Security (CCCS), the New Zealand National Cyber Security Centre (NZ NCSC), CERT New Zealand, the UK National Cyber Security Centre (UK NCSC) and the US National Cybersecurity and Communications Integration Center (NCCIC). (2018, October 11). Joint Report on Publicly Available Hacking Tools. Retrieved March 11, 2019. https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/report/joint-report-on-publicly-available-hacking-tools

**DLL 文件更改检测**
- **描述**: 监控 .manifest/.local 重定向文件或文件系统的更改，包括移动、重命名、替换或修改 DLL 文件。如果加载的 DLL 文件集与过去的记录相比发生变化，并且无法与已知软件或补丁关联，则应视为可疑。
