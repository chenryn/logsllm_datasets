**ATT&CK ID:** C0025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack

In the 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack, attackers utilized Dynamic Link Libraries (DLLs) and executable files (EXEs) with filenames that mimicked common electric power sector protocols. This technique was employed to disguise malicious files, making them appear legitimate and thereby evading detection. [1]
