## What is Research?
Research is the systematic search for knowledge, conducted with an open mind to establish novel facts, solve new or existing problems, prove new ideas, or develop new theories, typically using a scientific method.

- **Source:** [Wikipedia on Research](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Research)

## Strategies for Finding Research Problems
- **Challenge Assumptions**
- **Think Big**
  - **Example:** CAIDA 2007 IPv4 Census Map
    - **Source:** [CAIDA 2007 IPv4 Census Map](http://www.caida.org/research/id-consumption/census-map/)
- **Think Small**
  - **Examples:**
    - Microsoft Word 2003 .doc
    - Firefox Process Memory
    - Windows .dll
    - Neverwinter Nights Database
- **Irritate Software, Hardware, Protocols, and People**
- **Detect Patterns**
  - **Examples:**
    - Bitcoin Discussions
      - **Sources:**
        - [Sunday Squakbox: Thoughts on Bitcoin](http://justindupre.com/sunday-squakbox-what-are-your-thoughts-on-bitcoin/)

  - **Example:** BIOS Disassembly Ninjutsu Uncovered

- **Look at the Intersection of Your Interest Areas**

    - Error exploitation
