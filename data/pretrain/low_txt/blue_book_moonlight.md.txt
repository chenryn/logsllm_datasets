[Moonlight](https://github.com/moonlight-stream/moonlight-docs/wiki) is an open-source client implementation of NVIDIA GameStream, enabling you to stream your collection of games and applications from a GameStream-compatible PC to another device on your local network or over the Internet. With Moonlight, you can enjoy your favorite games on a variety of devices, including PCs, phones, tablets, and TVs.
