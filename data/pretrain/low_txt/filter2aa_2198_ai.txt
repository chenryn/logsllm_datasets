**Notice of Forfeiture**

**Statutes Involved:**
- 18 U.S.C. § 981(a)(1)(C)
- 18 U.S.C. § 982(a)(1)
- 18 U.S.C. § 1963
- 18 U.S.C. § 2323
- 21 U.S.C. § 853
- 28 U.S.C. § 2461(c)

**Re-allegations:**
The allegations contained in Counts One through Fourteen of this Indictment are hereby re-alleged and incorporated by reference for the purpose of alleging forfeiture.

**Grand Jury Findings:**
There is probable cause that the property described in this Notice of Forfeiture is subject to forfeiture pursuant to the statutes described herein.

**Notice to All Defendants:**
Pursuant to Federal Rule of Criminal Procedure 32.2(a), the United States of America gives notice to all defendants that, in the event of a conviction by any defendant of any of the offenses charged in Counts One through Fourteen of this Indictment, the United States intends to forfeit the property of that defendant as further described in this Notice of Forfeiture.

**Forfeiture Provisions:**
1. **18 U.S.C. § 1963:**
   - Any interest acquired or maintained in violation of Section 1962.
   - Any interest in, security of, claim against, or property or contractual right of any kind affording a source of influence over, any enterprise established, operated, controlled, conducted, or participated in, in violation of Section 1962.
   - Any property constituting, or derived from, any proceeds obtained directly or indirectly from racketeering activity or unlawful debt collection in violation of Section 1962.

2. **18 U.S.C. § 981(a)(1)(C) and 28 U.S.C. § 2461(c):**
   - Any property, real or personal, which constitutes or is derived from proceeds traceable to the conspiracy to commit copyright infringement, in violation of 18 U.S.C. §§ 371 and 2319.

3. **18 U.S.C. § 982(a)(1):**
   - Any property, real or personal, involved in the conspiracy to launder monetary instruments, in violation of 18 U.S.C. § 1956(h), and any property traceable to such property.

4. **18 U.S.C. § 2323:**
   - Any article, the making or trafficking of which is prohibited under 18 U.S.C. § 2319 or 17 U.S.C. § 506.
   - Any property used, or intended to be used, to commit or facilitate the commission of a violation of 18 U.S.C. § 2319 or 17 U.S.C. § 506.
   - Any property constituting or derived from any proceeds obtained directly or indirectly as a result of the commission of a violation of 18 U.S.C. § 2319 or 17 U.S.C. § 506.

5. **18 U.S.C. § 981(a)(1)(C) and 28 U.S.C. § 2461(c):**
   - Any property, real or personal, which constitutes or is derived from proceeds traceable to the wire fraud scheme, in violation of 18 U.S.C. § 1343.

**Money Judgment:**
Upon conviction of any defendant, a money judgment may be imposed on that defendant equal to the total value of the property subject to forfeiture, which is at least $175,000,000.

**Property Subject to Forfeiture:**
The United States of America gives notice to all defendants that the property to be forfeited includes, but is not limited to, the following:

1. $175,000,000 in United States dollars.
2. Various bank accounts and financial assets held in the names of the defendants and their associated entities, including accounts at Bank of New Zealand, Kiwibank, Hongkong and Shanghai Banking Corporation Limited, Citibank, Stadtsparkasse München, Commerzbank, Deutsche Bank AG, Computershare Investor Services Limited, Development Bank of Singapore, Hang Seng Bank Ltd., Westpac Bank, Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (Asia) Limited, and other financial institutions.
