**ATT&CK ID:** M1026  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Privileged Account Management

**Description:**
- **Domain Admin Accounts:** Restrict the permissions of domain admin accounts to domain controllers and a limited number of servers. Delegate other administrative functions to separate, dedicated accounts.
- **Service Accounts:** Limit service accounts to the minimal required privileges. Avoid including these accounts in privileged groups, such as Domain Administrators, unless absolutely necessary.
