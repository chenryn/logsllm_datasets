The `google.golang.org/cloud` packages have been renamed and are now available at `cloud.google.com/go`. The `google.golang.org/cloud` resources will remain accessible until September 12, after which they will no longer be available. For more information, please refer to the announcement on our API clients' mailing list, which can be found at [this link](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/google-api-go-announce/nXY-DYZGqz8).
