### InvisiMole
**描述：**
InvisiMole 恶意软件能够创建隐藏的系统目录。[1]

**参考文献：**
- Hromcova, Z. 和 Cherpanov, A. (2020, June). INVISIMOLE: THE HIDDEN PART OF THE STORY. Retrieved July 16, 2020. [PDF](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ESET_InvisiMole.pdf)

### HAWKBALL
**描述：**
HAWKBALL 恶意软件可以收集操作系统版本、架构信息以及计算机名称。[2]

**参考文献：**
- Patil, S. 和 Williams, M. (2019, June 5). Government Sector in Central Asia Targeted With New HAWKBALL Backdoor Delivered via Microsoft Office Vulnerabilities. Retrieved June 20, 2019. [链接](https://www.fireeye.com/blog/threat-research/2019/06/government-in-central-asia-targeted-with-hawkball-backdoor.html)

### RCSession
**描述：**
RCSession 恶意软件可以使用 WinSock API 进行通信，包括 WSASend 和 WSARecv。[3]

**参考文献：**
- Global Threat Center, Intelligence Team. (2020, December). APT27 Turns to Ransomware. Retrieved November 12, 2021. [PDF](https://web.archive.org/web/20210104144857/https://shared-public-reports.s3-eu-west-1.amazonaws.com/APT27+turns+to+ransomware.pdf)

### Waterbug
**描述：**
Waterbug 间谍组织在攻击政府机构时推出了一套全新的工具集。[4]

**参考文献：**
- Symantec DeepSight Adversary Intelligence Team. (2019, June 20). Waterbug: Espionage Group Rolls Out Brand-New Toolset in Attacks Against Governments. Retrieved July 8, 2019. [链接](https://www.symantec.com/blogs/threat-intelligence/waterbug-espionage-governments)

### Emotet
**描述：**
Emotet 恶意软件可以通过暴力破解本地管理员密码，然后利用该密码进行横向移动。[5]

**参考文献：**
- Smith, A. (2017, December 22). Protect your network from Emotet Trojan with Malwarebytes Endpoint Security. Retrieved January 17, 2019. [链接](https://support.malwarebytes.com/docs/DOC-2295)

### C0015 活动
**描述：**
在 C0015 活动中，威胁行为者对多台主机进行了文件列表发现，以确保锁加密成功。[6]

**参考文献：**
- DFIR Report. (2021, November 29). CONTInuing the Bazar Ransomware Story. Retrieved September 29, 2022. [链接](https://thedfirreport.com/2021/11/29/continuing-the-bazar-ransomware-story/)

NETWIRE 恶意软件使用 .NET 打包工具来逃避检测。[7]

**参考文献：**
- Lambert, T. (2020, January 29). Intro to Netwire. Retrieved January 7, 2021. [链接](https://redcanary.com/blog/netwire-remote-access-trojan-on-linux/)

Denis 恶意软件会查询注册表中的键和值。[8]

### Cleaver 组织
**描述：**
Cleaver 组织创建了包含个人资料照片、详细信息和联系人的虚假 LinkedIn 资料。[9]

**参考文献：**
- Dell SecureWorks. (2015, October 7). Suspected Iran-Based Hacker Group Creates Network of Fake LinkedIn Profiles. Retrieved January 14, 2016. [链接](http://www.secureworks.com/cyber-threat-intelligence/threats/suspected-iran-based-hacker-group-creates-network-of-fake-linkedin-profiles/)

### 条件访问策略
**描述：**
使用条件访问策略阻止来自非合规设备或组织 IP 范围之外的登录。[10]

**参考文献：**
- Microsoft. (2022, December 14). Conditional Access templates. Retrieved February 21, 2023. [链接](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/active-directory/conditional-access/concept-conditional-access-policy-common)

### 配置入侵防御设备
**描述：**
配置入侵防御设备以检测来自未经授权源的 SNMP 查询和命令。[11]

**参考文献：**
- US-CERT. (2018, April 20). Alert (TA18-106A) Russian State-Sponsored Cyber Actors Targeting Network Infrastructure Devices. Retrieved October 19, 2020. [链接](https://www.us-cert.gov/ncas/alerts/TA18-106A)

### APT18 组织
**描述：**
APT18 组织成员使用 Windows 内置的任务计划程序工具 `at` 来安排任务，在受害网络上执行。[12]

**参考文献：**
- Carvey, H. (2014, September 2). Where you AT?: Indicators of lateral movement using at.exe on Windows 7 systems. Retrieved January 25, 2016. [链接](http://www.secureworks.com/resources/blog/where-you-at-indicators-of-lateral-movement-using-at-exe-on-windows-7-systems/)
