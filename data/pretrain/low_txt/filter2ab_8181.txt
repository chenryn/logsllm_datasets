**ATT&CK ID:** S0455  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Metamorfo

**Description:**
Metamorfo is a malware that includes a function to hijack data from the clipboard. It achieves this by continuously monitoring the clipboard's contents and replacing any detected cryptocurrency wallet addresses with one controlled by the attacker. This technique enables the malware to redirect cryptocurrency transactions to the attacker's wallet, thereby facilitating financial theft.
