**Story Time: Supply Acquisition and Circuit Interrupts Around the House**

**Purpose of Circuit Interrupters:**
- **GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter):** Designed to prevent electric shock by interrupting the circuit when a ground fault is detected.
- **AFCI (Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter):** Designed to prevent fires by interrupting the circuit when an arc fault is detected.

**Code Requirements:**

**GFCI:**
- Bathrooms and other indoor wet locations
- Garages, storage areas, non-habitable areas, and unfinished basements
- Outdoor areas, including compartments accessible from outside the unit, rooftops, pools, and hot tubs
- Crawl spaces at or below grade level
- Kitchen (dishwasher, refrigerator)
- Laundry areas

**AFCI:**
- Kitchens
- Family rooms
- Dining rooms
- Living rooms
- Parlors
- Libraries
- Dens
- Bedrooms
- Sunrooms
- Recreation rooms
- Closets and hallways
- Laundry areas
- Or similar rooms
