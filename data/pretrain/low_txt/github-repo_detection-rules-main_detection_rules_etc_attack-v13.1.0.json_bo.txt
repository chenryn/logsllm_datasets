### Black Basta
**Description:**
Black Basta is a ransomware written in C++ that has been offered within the ransomware-as-a-service (RaaS) model since at least April 2022. It targets both Windows and VMWare ESXi servers. Black Basta operations include double extortion techniques, where attackers threaten to leak sensitive information if the ransom is not paid. The group has targeted multiple high-value organizations, with a significant number of victims based in the U.S. Security researchers suggest that Black Basta RaaS operators may include current or former members of the Conti group.
