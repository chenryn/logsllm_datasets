**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT28

APT28, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has employed various malicious applications to steal user OAuth access tokens. For Gmail users, they have used applications disguised as "Google Defender," "Google Email Protection," and "Google Scanner." Similarly, for Yahoo users, APT28 has targeted them with applications masquerading as "Delivery Service" and "McAfee Email Protection." [6]
