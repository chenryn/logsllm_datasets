**Title: Black Hat Asia 2022 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways**

Join members of the Black Hat Asia Review Board—Ryan Flores, Lidia Giuliano, Vandana Verma, and Stefano Zanero—in a thought-provoking discussion on the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community. Moderated by Jeff Moss, this Locknote session will provide a candid and insightful conversation on the key takeaways from the conference. The panelists will explore how these emerging trends and insights will shape future InfoSec strategies, offering valuable perspectives for both practitioners and leaders in the field.
