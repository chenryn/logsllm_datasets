**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The malware **SierraAlfa**, associated with the Lazarus Group, transmits data to one of several hard-coded command and control (C2) servers, selected at random. If the initial transmission attempt fails, the malware will automatically select a different C2 server from the list and retry the data transfer.
