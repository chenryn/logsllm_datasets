**InvisiMole (S0260)**
- **描述**: InvisiMole 可以使用指向控制面板的 .lnk 快捷方式来建立持久性。
- **引用**: Hromcova, Z. 和 Cherpanov, A. (2020, June). INVISIMOLE: THE HIDDEN PART OF THE STORY. 取自 July 16, 2020. [链接](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ESET_InvisiMole.pdf)

**Pupy (S0192)**
- **描述**: Pupy 可以通过反射式 DLL 注入迁移到另一个进程中。
- **引用**: Nicolas Verdier. (n.d.). 取自 January 29, 2018. [链接](https://github.com/n1nj4sec/pupy)

**Windigo (G0124)**
- **描述**: Windigo 使用脚本来收集由 OpenSSH 后门留在磁盘上的凭据文件。
- **引用**: Dumont, R., M. Léveillé, M., Porcher, H. (2018, December 1). THE DARK SIDE OF THE FORSSHE A landscape of OpenSSH backdoors. 取自 July 16, 2020. [链接](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/ESET-The_Dark_Side_of_the_ForSSHe.pdf)

**BITTER (G1002)**
- **描述**: BITTER 尝试通过钓鱼邮件诱使受害者打开恶意附件。
- **引用**:
  - Dela Paz, R. (2016, October 21). BITTER: a targeted attack against Pakistan. 取自 June 1, 2022. [链接](https://www.forcepoint.com/blog/x-labs/bitter-targeted-attack-against-pakistan)
  - Raghuprasad, C. (2022, May 11). Bitter APT adds Bangladesh to their targets. 取自 June 1, 2022. [链接](https://blog.talosintelligence.com/2022/05/bitter-apt-adds-bangladesh-to-their.html)

**PinchDuke (S0048)**
- **描述**: PinchDuke 收集系统配置信息。
- **引用**: F-Secure Labs. (2015, September 17). The Dukes: 7 years of Russian cyberespionage. 取自 December 10, 2015. [链接](https://www.f-secure.com/documents/996508/1030745/dukes_whitepaper.pdf)

**SVCReady (S1064)**
- **描述**: SVCReady 通过 HTTP 与其 C2 服务器通信。
- **引用**: Schlapfer, Patrick. (2022, June 6). A New Loader Gets Ready. 取自 December 13, 2022. [链接](https://threatresearch.ext.hp.com/svcready-a-new-loader-reveals-itself/)

**网络连接监控**
- **描述**: 监控由不受信任的主机发送或接收的新建网络连接。

**Bonadan (S0486)**
- **描述**: Bonadan 可以下载一个带有加密货币挖矿扩展的附加模块。
- **引用**: Dumont, R., M. Léveillé, M., Porcher, H. (2018, December 1). THE DARK SIDE OF THE FORSSHE A landscape of OpenSSH backdoors. 取自 July 16, 2020. [链接](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/ESET-The_Dark_Side_of_the_ForSSHe.pdf)

**PolyglotDuke (S0518)**
- **描述**: PolyglotDuke 可以使用 LoadLibraryW 和 CreateProcess 来加载和执行代码。
- **引用**: Faou, M., Tartare, M., Dupuy, T. (2019, October). OPERATION GHOST. 取自 September 23, 2020. [链接](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/ESET_Operation_Ghost_Dukes.pdf)

**Bisonal (S0268)**
- **描述**: Bisonal 使用 RegQueryValueExA 函数从注册表中检索代理信息。
- **引用**: Mercer, W., et al. (2020, March 5). Bisonal: 10 years of play. 取自 January 26, 2022. [链接](https://blog.talosintelligence.com/2020/03/bisonal-10-years-of-play.html)

**rundll32.exe 命令参数**
- **描述**: rundll32.exe 调用时使用的命令参数可能有助于确定正在加载的 DLL 的来源和目的。

**SUNSPOT (S0562)**
- **描述**: SUNSPOT 解密存储在 AES128-CBC 加密块中的 SUNBURST。
- **引用**: CrowdStrike Intelligence Team. (2021, January 11). SUNSPOT: An Implant in the Build Process. 取自 January 11, 2021. [链接](https://www.crowdstrike.com/blog/sunspot-malware-technical-analysis/)

**Rifdoor (S0433)**
- **描述**: Rifdoor 可以从包含宏的恶意 Excel 或 Word 文档中执行。
- **引用**: Knight, S. (2020, April 16). VMware Carbon Black TAU Threat Analysis: The Evolution of Lazarus. 取自 May 1, 2020. [链接](https://www.carbonblack.com/2020/04/16/vmware-carbon-black-tau-threat-analysis-the-evolution-of-lazarus/)

**SUNBURST Backdoor (S0559)**
- **描述**: SUNBURST 后门通过 SolarWinds 供应链攻击多个全球受害者。
- **引用**:
  - FireEye. (2020, December 13). Highly Evasive Attacker Leverages SolarWinds Supply Chain to Compromise Multiple Global Victims With SUNBURST Backdoor. 取自 January 4, 2021. [链接](https://www.fireeye.com/blog/threat-research/2020/12/evasive-attacker-leverages-solarwinds-supply-chain-compromises-with-sunburst-backdoor.html)
  - NCSC, CISA, FBI, NSA. (2021, May 7). Further TTPs associated with SVR cyber actors. 取自 July 29, 2021. [链接](https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/files/Advisory-further-TTPs-associated-with-SVR-cyber-actors.pdf)
