**ATT&CK ID:** S1065  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Woody RAT

**Description:**
Woody RAT is capable of downloading files from its command and control (C2) server. These files may include .NET DLLs, as well as the WoodySharpExecutor and WoodyPowerSession components.[483]
