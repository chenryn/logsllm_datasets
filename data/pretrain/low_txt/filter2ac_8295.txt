**ATT&CK ID:** T1021.005  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** VNC

Adversaries may leverage valid accounts to remotely control machines using Virtual Network Computing (VNC). VNC is a platform-independent desktop sharing system that utilizes the Remote Framebuffer (RFB) protocol. This protocol allows users to remotely control another computer's display by relaying screen, mouse, and keyboard inputs over the network.
