**ATT&CK ID:** S0692  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SILENTTRINITY

SILENTTRINITY includes several modules designed to bypass User Account Control (UAC). These methods include leveraging Windows Device Manager, Manage Optional Features, and an image hijack on the `.msc` file extension.[59]
