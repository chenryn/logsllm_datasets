**Javali (S0528)**
- **描述**: Javali (S0528) 使用大型混淆库来阻碍检测和分析。(引用: Securelist Brazilian Banking Malware July 2020)
- **外部引用**: 
  - GReAT. (2020, July 14). The Tetrade: Brazilian banking malware goes global. Retrieved November 9, 2020. [链接](https://securelist.com/the-tetrade-brazilian-banking-malware/97779/)

- **描述**: Trojan.Karagany (S0094) 可以监控打开窗口的标题以识别特定关键词。(引用: Secureworks Karagany July 2019)
- **外部引用**: 
  - Secureworks. (2019, July 24). Updated Karagany Malware Targets Energy Sector. Retrieved August 12, 2020. [链接](https://www.secureworks.com/research/updated-karagany-malware-targets-energy-sector)

- **外部引用**: 
  - Cybereason Nocturnus. (2022, May 4). Operation CuckooBees: Deep-Dive into Stealthy Winnti Techniques. Retrieved September 22, 2022. [链接](https://www.cybereason.com/blog/operation-cuckoobees-deep-dive-into-stealthy-winnti-techniques)

**ProLock (S0654)**
- **描述**: ProLock (S0654) 可以在受感染主机上使用 RC6 加密文件，并使用 RSA-1024 加密密钥。(引用: Group IB Ransomware September 2020)
- **外部引用**: 
  - Group IB. (2020, September). LOCK LIKE A PRO. Retrieved September 27, 2021. [链接](https://groupib.pathfactory.com/ransomware-reports/prolock_wp)

**Wingbird (S0176)**
- **描述**: Wingbird (S0176) 会丢弃一个恶意文件 (sspisrv.dll) 并附带一个 lsass.exe 的副本，用于注册一个加载 sspisrv.dll 为驱动的服务。当 lsass.exe 加载恶意驱动时，其入口点函数中的有效载荷会被执行，然后伪造的服务变得不稳定并崩溃。(引用: Microsoft SIR Vol 21, Microsoft Wingbird Nov 2017)
- **外部引用**: 
  - Anthe, C. et al. (2016, December 14). Microsoft Security Intelligence Report Volume 21. Retrieved November 27, 2017. [链接](http://download.microsoft.com/download/E/B/0/EB0F50CC-989C-4B66-B7F6-68CD3DC90DE3/Microsoft_Security_Intelligence_Report_Volume_21_English.pdf)
  - Microsoft. (2017, November 9). Backdoor:Win32/Wingbird.A!dha. Retrieved November 27, 2017. [链接](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/wdsi/threats/malware-encyclopedia-description?Name=Backdoor:Win32/Wingbird.A!dha)

**Ursnif (S0386)**
- **描述**: Ursnif (S0386) 使用合法系统文件和现有文件夹中的字符串作为其文件、文件夹和注册表项的名称。(引用: TrendMicro Ursnif Mar 2015)
- **外部引用**: 
  - Caragay, R. (2015, March 26). URSNIF: The Multifaceted Malware. Retrieved June 5, 2019. [链接](https://blog.trendmicro.com/trendlabs-security-intelligence/ursnif-the-multifaceted-malware/?_ga=2.165628854.808042651.1508120821-744063452.1505819992)
