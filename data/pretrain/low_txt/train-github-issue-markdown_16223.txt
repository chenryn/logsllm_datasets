The default alert boxes in Bootstrap do not meet the contrast ratio requirements as specified by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0. Using the contrast checker tool available at [WebAIM](http://webaim.org/resources/contrastchecker/), it can be demonstrated that both AAA and AA levels of contrast are not achieved for all types of alert boxes, including .alert, .alert-danger, .alert-error, .alert-info, and .alert-success.

I have customized the alert boxes to better suit my needs, but I wanted to bring this issue to your attention for consideration.
