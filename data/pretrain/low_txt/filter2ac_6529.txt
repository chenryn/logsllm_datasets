**ATT&CK ID:** C0025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack

In the 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack, the Sandworm Team conducted network reconnaissance to check for connectivity to internal resources. They utilized Lightweight Directory Access Protocol (LDAP) to query Active Directory, thereby gathering information about the computers listed in the directory. [3]
