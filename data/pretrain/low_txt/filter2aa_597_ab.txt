- **Behavior Analysis**: Conducting multi-platform behavioral analysis of malware.

### Why TWMAN?
- **Components**:
  - **Malware Behavioral Analysis Agent**: Collects and analyzes malware behavior.
  - **Ontology Agent**: Builds and maintains a repository of malware behavioral ontologies and rules.
- **Functionality**: TWMAN protects computers from malware, viruses, and Trojans by leveraging these components.
