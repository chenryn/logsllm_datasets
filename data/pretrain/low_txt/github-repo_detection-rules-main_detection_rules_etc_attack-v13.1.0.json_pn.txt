**Windshift（G0112）**

Windshift (G0112) 使用模仿 MS Office 文件图标的恶意可执行文件来欺骗用户。(Citation: objective-see windtail1 dec 2018) 此外，该组织还通过将文件扩展名更改为 ".scr" 来伪装成 Windows 屏保程序，从而隐藏恶意可执行文件。(Citation: BlackBerry Bahamut)

**外部参考**
- Wardle, Patrick. (2018, December 20). Middle East Cyber-Espionage analyzing WindShift's implant: OSX.WindTail (part 1). Retrieved October 3, 2019.
- The BlackBerry Research & Intelligence Team. (2020, October). BAHAMUT: Hack-for-Hire Masters of Phishing, Fake News, and Fake Apps. Retrieved February 8, 2021.

**Saint Bot (S1018)**

Saint Bot (S1018) 依赖于用户执行通过鱼叉式网络钓鱼发送的恶意附件。(Citation: Malwarebytes Saint Bot April 2021)(Citation: Palo Alto Unit 42 OutSteel SaintBot February 2022)

**外部参考**
- Hasherezade. (2021, April 6). A deep dive into Saint Bot, a new downloader. Retrieved June 9, 2022.
- Unit 42. (2022, February 25). Spear Phishing Attacks Target Organizations in Ukraine, Payloads Include the Document Stealer OutSteel and the Downloader SaintBot. Retrieved June 9, 2022.

**TeamTNT (G0139)**

TeamTNT (G0139) 在受害者的环境中部署了不同类型的容器以促进执行。(Citation: Intezer TeamTNT September 2020)(Citation: Trend Micro TeamTNT) 该组织还将加密货币挖矿软件传输到本地 IP 地址范围内的 Kubernetes 集群中。(Citation: Cisco Talos Intelligence Group)

**外部参考**
- Darin Smith. (2022, April 21). TeamTNT targeting AWS, Alibaba. Retrieved August 4, 2022.

**Cobalt Strike (S0154)**

Cobalt Strike (S0154) 可以在生成的进程中使用伪造的参数来执行信标命令。(Citation: Cobalt Strike Manual 4.3 November 2020)
