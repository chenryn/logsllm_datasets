- **Side Band Phone:** 2.4 kHz – 3 kHz
- **AM Phone:** 5 kHz – 10.2 kHz
- **FM Phone:** 8 kHz – 16 kHz
- **AM Television:** 6 MHz – 10 MHz

### The Good Stuff
- **Experimental ‘Test’ Modes allowed on all bands (Pulse and Spread Spectrum Limited)**
- **Unlimited Bandwidth on 33cm and above**
- **Power Limits:**
  - 1500 W PEP on most bands
  - 50 W PEP on 60m
  - 200 W PEP on 30m
  - 50 W PEP on 70cm in some locations
- **Unlimited ERP (Effective Radiated Power)**
- **PEP (Peak Envelope Power): Peak Power at leaving the transmitter**
- **ERP (Effective Radiated Power): Power in to the radio horizon after feed line loss and antenna gain**
- **Higher Antenna Gain = Narrower Beamwidth**

## 3. Getting Started
### Classes of Operator
- **Technician:**
  - No Morse Code requirement
  - All privileges on 6m and up
  - 200 W PEP below 6m
  - CW only on 80m, 40m, 15m
  - CW, RTTY, and Data on 10m
  - SSB Voice on 10m (28.3 – 28.5 MHz)
- **General:**
  - All Technician privileges plus most HF privileges
  - 400 kHz of bandwidth reserved for Advanced and Extra
  - Ability to administer VE Technician tests
- **Extra:**
  - All Technician and General privileges
  - All frequency privileges
  - Ability to administer all VE tests
  - Entitled to Class A and Class B call signs
