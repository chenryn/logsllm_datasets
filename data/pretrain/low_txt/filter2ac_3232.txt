**ATT&CK ID:** C0025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack

In the 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack, the Sandworm Team employed a trojanized version of Windows Notepad to establish an additional layer of persistence for the Industroyer malware. [1]
