By removing the `min-height` property from the "Shared size and type resets" section in the `forms.less` file, we can prevent input fields from appearing overly large in Internet Explorer 8. This change has been tested in the latest versions of Internet Explorer 9 and 10, as well as the most recent versions of Firefox and Chrome, and no issues have been observed.
