# Microsoft Security Best Practices Module: Network Security and Containment

Network security and containment are critical for reducing organizational risk. By implementing access controls, you can limit the ability of attackers to traverse your enterprise environment without impeding legitimate communications and interactions.

The following videos provide detailed guidance on various aspects of network security and containment. You can also download the [PowerPoint slides](/microsoft-365/downloads/security-compass-presentation.pptx) associated with these videos.
