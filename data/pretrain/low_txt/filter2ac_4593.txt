**ATT&CK ID:** S1030  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Squirrelwaffle

**Description:**
Squirrelwaffle has been observed using malicious VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) macros embedded in Microsoft Word documents and Excel spreadsheets. These macros are designed to execute an `AutoOpen` subroutine, which automatically runs when the document is opened, facilitating the initial stages of the attack.
