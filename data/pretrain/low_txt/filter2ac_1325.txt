**Title: Black Hat Asia 2022 Keynote: Fireside Chat with U.S. National Cyber Director Chris Inglis**

Join us for an insightful conversation with the inaugural U.S. National Cyber Director, Chris Inglis, and Jeff Moss. This discussion will explore the evolving relationship between governments and the private sector in the realm of cybersecurity. Director Inglis will share his optimistic vision for the future of the Internet and outline the responsibilities we all share to achieve this vision.

The session will delve into what it takes for governments to become more effective partners, the expectations from companies and individuals, and the interplay between changes in cyberspace and geopolitics. Expect a dynamic and thought-provoking dialogue on these critical issues.
