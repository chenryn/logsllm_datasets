**Title: Black Hat Asia 2020 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways from Day 1**

Join us at the conclusion of the first day of this year's virtual conference for an insightful discussion with members of the Black Hat Review Board. Ryan Flores, Lidia Giuliano, Ty Miller, and Ashley Shen will delve into the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community. This Locknote session will feature a candid conversation on the key takeaways from the first day, and how these emerging trends are likely to shape future InfoSec strategies.
