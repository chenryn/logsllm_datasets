### Appendices

**A. Measurement Validation**

To validate our findings in §3, we examined 182 publicly disclosed incidents from two cloud providers [2, 7]. These incident reports include issues that led to significant customer impact and provide a partial log of the mitigation process. Despite their limitations, these reports offer valuable insights. We found that in 35 out of the 182 incidents, multiple components were discussed as either being impacted or involved in the incident. Each of these components may have played a role in the overall incident.
