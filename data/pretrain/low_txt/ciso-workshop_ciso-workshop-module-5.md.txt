# CISO Workshop Module 5: Information Protection

This module will guide you through the process of protecting sensitive information, no matter where it travels, using automatic classification and persistent encryption across various devices. Additionally, it will cover the continuous monitoring of data across mobile devices, cloud services, and other corporate assets.
