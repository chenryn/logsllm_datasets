**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group, a well-known threat actor, has been observed downloading various files, malware, and tools from its command-and-control (C2) servers onto compromised hosts. This activity has been documented in multiple reports and analyses.[252][253][254][129][134][255][256][257][258][259]
