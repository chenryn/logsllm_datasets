In this paper, we introduce DeStroid, an automated method for decrypting obfuscated strings in Android applications. Our focus is specifically on contemporary Android malware that employs sophisticated string encryption techniques. We demonstrate that DeStroid outperforms all publicly available string deobfuscation methods.
