**ATT&CK ID:** M1028  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Operating System Configuration

**Recommendation:**

- **Session Timeout Settings:** Modify Group Policy Objects (GPOs) to define shorter session timeouts and set a maximum duration for any single session.
- **Disconnected Session Management:** Adjust GPOs to specify the maximum amount of time that a disconnected session can remain active on the Remote Desktop (RD) session host server.
