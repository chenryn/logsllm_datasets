**ATT&CK ID:** C0025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack

In the 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack, the Sandworm Team utilized UPX (Ultimate Packer for eXecutables) to compress a copy of Mimikatz. This technique allowed the attackers to obfuscate the malicious payload and evade detection by security systems. [3]
