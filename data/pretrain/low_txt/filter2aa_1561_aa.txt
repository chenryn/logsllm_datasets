### Emulation Accuracy Evolution
- **Early Emulators:** Highly inaccurate
- **bsnes:**
  - Extreme accuracy, poor usability
  - [Ars Technica Article](http://arstechnica.com/gaming/2011/08/accuracy-takes-power-one-mans-3ghz-quest-to-build-a-perfect-snes-emulator/)
  - [Web Archive](https://web.archive.org/web/20120915125144/http://byuu.org/bsnes/accuracy)
- **higan:**
  - Match actual hardware, frame for frame
  - [byuu.org/emulation/higan/](http://byuu.org/emulation/higan/)
