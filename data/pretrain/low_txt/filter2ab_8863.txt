**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group has been known to use `mshta.exe` to execute HTML Application (HTA) files that are downloaded by initial access documents. This technique allows the group to leverage the Windows HTML Application Host to run malicious scripts, often as part of their initial compromise or for further execution of their payload.
