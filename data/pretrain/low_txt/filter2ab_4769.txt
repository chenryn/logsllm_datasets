**Title: Black Hat USA 2022 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways**

To conclude Black Hat USA 2022, we invite you to join Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss and Review Board members Sheila A. Berta, Chris Eng, Natalie Silvanovich, and Matt Suiche for an insightful discussion on the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community.

During this Locknote session, the panel will engage in a candid conversation, highlighting the key takeaways from the conference and exploring how these trends will shape future InfoSec strategies. This is an excellent opportunity to gain valuable insights and perspectives from leading experts in the field.

Join us as we reflect on the major themes and developments from Black Hat USA 2022 and discuss their implications for the future of cybersecurity.
