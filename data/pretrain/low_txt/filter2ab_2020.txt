**ATT&CK ID:** M1042  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Disable or Remove Feature or Program

- **Disable Autorun if it is not necessary.** [9]
- **Restrict or disallow the use of removable media at an organizational policy level if they are not required for business operations.** [10]

This approach ensures that unnecessary features and programs, which could potentially be exploited by attackers, are disabled or removed, thereby reducing the attack surface.
