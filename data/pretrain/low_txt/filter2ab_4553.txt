**Title: Black Hat Asia 2023: Key Trends and Takeaways**

Join Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss and distinguished members of the Black Hat Asia Review Board—Sudhanshu Chauhan, Ty Miller, and Asuka Nakajima—for an insightful discussion on the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community. This panel session will feature a candid conversation on the key takeaways from the conference and how these emerging trends will shape future InfoSec strategies.

Don't miss this opportunity to gain valuable insights into the latest developments and challenges in the field, and to hear from leading experts on how to navigate the evolving landscape of cybersecurity.
