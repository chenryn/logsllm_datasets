### Activation Associations
- **Visual Cortex (both tasks)**: Visual processing, search, attention shift, and decision-making.
- **RMFG (Right Middle Frontal Gyrus) (phishing)**: Search, attention shift, and decision-making (fake decisions are harder).
- **Bilateral Insula (phishing)**: Attention, decision-making, and visual processing.
- **LMFG (Left Middle Frontal Gyrus)**: Language comprehension, visual processing, reading.
- **RMFG (phishing)**: Language comprehension, visual attention.
- **LMFG (phishing)**: Language comprehension, visual attention, and inspection (warnings show more activity than non-warnings).
