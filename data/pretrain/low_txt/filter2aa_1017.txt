## 2. Solder

### Definition of Solder
- **Solder**: A metal or metallic alloy used when melted to join metallic surfaces, typically an alloy of lead and tin.
- **Temperature Ranges:**
  - **Soldering**: > 840°F
  - **Brazing**: 840° - 1200°F
  - **Welding**: 1200 - 6000°F

### Hazardous Substances in Solder
- **Lead**
- **Mercury**
- **Cadmium**
- **Hexavalent Chromium**
- **Polybrominated Biphenyls**
- **Polybrominated Diphenyl Ether**

**RoHS (Reduction of Hazardous Substances)**: Regulations to reduce the use of hazardous substances in electronic equipment.

## 3. Flux

### Definition of Flux
- **Flux**: A substance used to promote fusion, such as rosin, applied to surfaces to be joined by soldering, brazing, or welding to clean and free them from oxide and promote their union.

### Functions of Flux
- **Cleans Surfaces to Be Joined**
- **Increases Solder Flow**
- **Conducts Heat to Some Degree**
- **Prevents Oxidation During Soldering**

### Available Forms of Flux
- **Liquid**: Rosin, No-clean Rosin, Water Soluble (Inorganic)
- **Paste**: Rosin, Acid
- **Cored Solder**: Rosin, No-clean Rosin, Water Soluble (Organic & Inorganic), Acid

### Rosin
- **Definition**: A translucent, amber-colored to almost black, brittle, friable resin obtained from pine trees or tall oil, used in making varnish.

### Readily Available Fluxes
- **Water (120°-150°F)**
- **Organic (‘331’ ORH1)**
- **Mild (‘3350’ INH1)**
- **Rosin (‘48’ ROM1)**
- **No Clean (‘245’ ROL0, ‘275’ ROL0, ‘285’ ROL0)**
- **Acid (Optional, Solvent)**
- **Neutralizer (Hot Water)**
- **Cleaning Type (Solvent, Denatured Alcohol, 50/50 Denatured Alcohol and Acetone, Rubbing Alcohol, 50/50 Rubbing Alcohol and Unscented Nail Polish Remover)**

### Flux Removal Tools
- **Toothbrush**
- **Cotton Swab (Q-Tip)**
- **Chamois Cloth**
- **Dental Pick**

### Soldering Process
1. **Clean Surface of Oxidation and Apply Flux if Necessary**
2. **Apply Heat to Work**
3. **Allow Work to Reach Temperature**
4. **Apply Solder to Work**
5. **Allow Solder Joint to Cool Down**
6. **Clean Flux Residue**
7. **Inspect Quality of Joint**
