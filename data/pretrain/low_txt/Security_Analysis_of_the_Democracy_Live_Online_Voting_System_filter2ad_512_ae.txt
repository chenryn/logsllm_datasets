### Online Ballot Delivery and Marking Technologies

Numerous platforms, such as OmniBallot and others, offer online ballot delivery and marking services. There is an urgent need for enhanced security scrutiny of these technologies to help officials evaluate the associated risks and ensure that remote voters can participate as safely as possible.
