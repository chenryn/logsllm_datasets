**ATT&CK ID:** M0930  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Segmentation

**Description:**
Implement network segmentation to categorize and isolate operational assets and their management devices based on their functional roles within the process. This approach allows for more stringent isolation of critical control and operational information, enhancing security within the control environment.
