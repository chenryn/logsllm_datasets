**Title: Botconf 2017 - Malpedia: A Collaborative Effort to Inventory the Malware Landscape**

In this paper, we present Malpedia, a collaborative platform designed for the curation of a comprehensive and well-organized corpus of malware samples. These samples are meticulously labeled and unpacked, ensuring a high level of accuracy and consistency. To demonstrate one of the key use cases for this dataset, we provide a comparative analysis of the structural characteristics of over 300 families of Windows malware.
