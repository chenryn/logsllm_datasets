**Title: FastIR Collector at BotConf 2016**

**Workshop Overview:**

The objective of this workshop is to introduce and demonstrate the use of FastIR, an open-source live forensic collector, in various investigative scenarios on Windows systems. We will cover a range of cases, including:

- Remote Access Trojans (RATs) with anti-forensic techniques
- Rootkits
- Trojans that employ DLL injection

Additionally, we will present new features developed this year, including enhancements to both the agent and server components.
