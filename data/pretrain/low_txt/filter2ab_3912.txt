**ATT&CK ID:** G0047  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Gamaredon Group

The Gamaredon Group has been known to deploy an Outlook VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) module on compromised systems. This module is used to send phishing emails, containing malicious attachments, to other employees within the same organization. [3]
