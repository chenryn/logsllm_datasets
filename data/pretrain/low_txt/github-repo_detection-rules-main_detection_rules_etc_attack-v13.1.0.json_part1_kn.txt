### HAWKBALL
**描述：**
HAWKBALL（[MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0391](https://attack.mitre.org/software/S0391)）曾使用包含方程编辑器的OLE对象来释放嵌入的shellcode。(引用来源: [FireEye HAWKBALL Jun 2019](https://www.fireeye.com/blog/threat-research/2019/06/government-in-central-asia-targeted-with-hawkball-backdoor.html))

**引用来源：**
- Patil, S. and Williams, M. (2019, June 5). 政府部门在中亚地区受到新型HAWKBALL后门攻击。通过Microsoft Office漏洞进行传播。取自：[FireEye Blog](https://www.fireeye.com/blog/threat-research/2019/06/government-in-central-asia-targeted-with-hawkball-backdoor.html)
