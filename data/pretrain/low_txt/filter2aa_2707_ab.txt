# Games and Home Security
Kinect-like technology could be utilized in home security systems, distinguishing between the movements of pets, family friends, and unfamiliar intruders. According to Jamie Shotton from Microsoft Research, the necessary algorithms are already available; the challenge lies in the social acceptability of having a camera continuously monitoring the home.
