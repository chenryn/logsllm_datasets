# Microsoft Security Best Practices Module: Information Protection and Storage

Valuable intellectual property, whether it belongs to your organization or its customers/constituents, requires appropriate security measures that reflect its value. This module provides guidance on best practices for information protection and storage.

## Video Tutorials

The following videos offer detailed insights into information protection and storage. You can also download the [PowerPoint slides](/microsoft-365/downloads/security-compass-presentation.pptx) associated with these videos.

For more information on information protection capabilities across Microsoft 365 and SQL databases, please refer to [CISO Workshop Module 5: Information Protection](/security/ciso-workshop/ciso-workshop-module-5).
