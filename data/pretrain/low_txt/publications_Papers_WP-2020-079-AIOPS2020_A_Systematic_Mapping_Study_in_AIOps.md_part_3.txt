### Distribution of Failure Management Papers by Category and Sub-Categories

**Figure 4: Published Papers in AIOps by Year and Categories from the Described Taxonomy**

In this study, we employed a pre-established methodology encompassing formulation, search, selection, and categorization techniques. This approach enabled us to collect over 1,000 contributions, which we then grouped into several categories based on our proposed taxonomy. The papers vary significantly in terms of their goals, data sources, and target components.

In the results section, we observed that the majority of the papers address failures in various forms. From a temporal perspective, there has been a consistent and growing research interest, particularly in areas such as anomaly detection and root cause analysis.
