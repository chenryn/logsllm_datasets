**ATT&CK ID:** M1026  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Privileged Account Management

Regularly audit domain and local accounts, along with their associated permission levels, to identify any scenarios that could allow an adversary to gain broad access by obtaining the credentials of a privileged account. [3] [67] These audits should also check for the presence of enabled default accounts and unauthorized new local accounts. Adhere to best practices for the design and administration of enterprise networks to minimize the use of privileged accounts across administrative tiers. [68]
