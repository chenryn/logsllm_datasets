**ATT&CK ID:** G0102  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Wizard Spider

Wizard Spider has been observed using `services.exe` to execute scripts and executables during lateral movement within a victim's network. [67][68]

### Summary
- **Threat Actor:** Wizard Spider
- **Tactic:** Lateral Movement
- **Technique:** Execution via `services.exe`
- **Purpose:** To run malicious scripts and executables on compromised systems
