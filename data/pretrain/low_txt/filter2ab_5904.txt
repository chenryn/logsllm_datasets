**ATT&CK ID:** M1043  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Credential Access Protection

On Windows 10 and Windows Server 2016, it is recommended to enable Windows Defender Credential Guard. This feature runs the Local Security Authority Subsystem Service (LSASS) in an isolated and virtualized environment, thereby preventing access by unauthorized device drivers.
