**Title: Black Hat Asia 2023 Fireside Chat: Jeff Moss and Gaurav Keerthi**

In this fireside chat, Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss will engage in a conversation with Gaurav Keerthi, the former Deputy Chief Executive of the Cyber Security Agency (CSA). The discussion will focus on the ongoing tension between regulating emerging technologies and the drive for innovation in cybersecurity. They will also explore the roles of government and the private sector in fostering innovation while protecting against security threats and addressing privacy concerns. 

Join this session to gain insights into whether government technology regulations are saving humanity or stifling innovation.
