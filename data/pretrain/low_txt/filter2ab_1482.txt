**Title: Black Hat Asia 2020: The Black Hat NOC: Greatest Hits and Holy Cows**

In this session, two veterans who have been at the helm of the Black Hat Network Operations Center (NOC) for nearly two decades will share their most fascinating experiences and insights. The discussion will go beyond just attacks and failures; it will delve into the evolution and reinvention of the NOC in response to changing industry trends and emerging threats.

We will explore which technologies and trends have proven effective, which have fallen short, and what decisions have been our best and worst. You may gain valuable ideas for your own NOC or Security Operations Center (SOC), or you might be shocked and appalled by some of our revelations. Regardless, you are guaranteed an engaging and entertaining experience.
