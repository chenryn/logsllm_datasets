**Title: BotConf 2019 - Bot with Rootkit: Update and Mine!**

In June 2019, we encountered an intriguing sample during our analysis. Upon closer inspection, we discovered that this sample downloaded a legitimate Microsoft update, KB3033929, from its own command-and-control (C&C) server and installed it on the infected machine. As we delved deeper into the sample's activities, the situation became even more fascinating.

For further details, you can download the full presentation [here](https://www.botconf.eu/wp-content/uploads/formidable/2/B2019-Eremin_Bot_with_rootkit.pdf).
