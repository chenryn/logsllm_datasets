### Treatment and Management

- **Itraconazole 200 mg BID for 1–2 years** for patients with AIDS who remain immunosuppressed.
- Lifelong maintenance therapy is recommended for these patients.
- **Alternatives to Itraconazole:** For patients unable to tolerate itraconazole, fluconazole and ketoconazole are viable alternatives.

**Monitoring:**
- Monitor urine and serum *Histoplasma* antigens several times per year.
