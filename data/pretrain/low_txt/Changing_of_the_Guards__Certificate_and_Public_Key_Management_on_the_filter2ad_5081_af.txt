### Addressing Middle Attacks on Compromised Keys

Our solution leverages current trends and takes them to an extreme, integrating short-lived three-phase certificates, a parent-child certificate relationship, and simple management rules. This approach addresses the critical issue of revocation without increasing the frequency of subject-key validations or CT log submissions.

### Future Work

Future research could involve:
- Collecting and analyzing more granular datasets.
- Comparing with alternative data sources (e.g., CT logs) to gain a comprehensive understanding of the certificate replacement landscape.
- Conducting additional analyses to explore other characteristics, such as the relationship between current features and domain popularity.
- Implementing and testing the proposed solution.
