**ATT&CK ID:** T1588  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Obtain Capabilities

Adversaries may acquire or steal capabilities to use during their targeting operations. Instead of developing these capabilities in-house, they may purchase, freely download, or steal them. These activities can include the acquisition of malware, software (including licenses), exploits, certificates, and information related to vulnerabilities. Adversaries may obtain these capabilities to support various phases of their lifecycle, enhancing their operational effectiveness.
