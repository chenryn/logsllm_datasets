**ATT&CK ID:** S0604  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Industroyer

**Description:**
Industroyer's OPC (OLE for Process Control) and IEC 61850 protocol modules are equipped with the capability to send `stVal` (status value) requests. These requests allow the malware to read the status of operational variables, providing it with critical information about the target system's state. [1]
