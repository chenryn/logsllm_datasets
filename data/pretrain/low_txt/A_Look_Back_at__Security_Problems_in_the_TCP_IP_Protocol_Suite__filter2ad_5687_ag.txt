From this perspective, the flaws in the r-utilities become evident. Another critical point to consider is the role of auxiliary data in facilitating attacks. An attacker who can establish a single TCP connection cannot accurately guess the correct sequence number. This limitation often serves as an effective mechanism for blocking or at least detecting such attacks.
