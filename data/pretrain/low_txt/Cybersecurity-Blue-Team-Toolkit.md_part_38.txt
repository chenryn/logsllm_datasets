## In Conclusion {#c15.xhtml#head-2-110}

When my husband suggested I write a book I would want to read, I decided to create the book I wished I had when starting in IT. My career has oscillated between practical work and teaching, and I am passionate about this niche. Being a technical educator in IT is not just my job; it is my passion. I aim to equip my students with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen paths.

The past six months of writing have challenged me to document my knowledge and experiences in making the world a safer place. As Ryan mentioned in the Foreword, in the field of cybersecurity, we are constantly confronted with new products, tools, and attack techniques. We are pulled in multiple directions daily, deciding what to secure and how to secure it. I hope this book provides you with the tools to be successful.

What's next? Keep learning and expanding your toolkit. Our industry will continue to evolve, and you must keep up or, even better, become a leader. Share your knowledge. Remember, as you learn more, you realize there is so much more to know. If you understand it well enough, you can explain it simply.
