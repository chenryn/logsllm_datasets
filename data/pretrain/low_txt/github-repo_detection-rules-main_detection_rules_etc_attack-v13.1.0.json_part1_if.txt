**Bumblebee**
- **描述**: Bumblebee (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S1039) 可以检查注册表中的特定键。
- **引用来源**: Salem, A. (2022, April 27). The chronicles of Bumblebee: The Hook, the Bee, and the Trickbot connection. Retrieved September 2, 2022. [链接](https://elis531989.medium.com/the-chronicles-of-bumblebee-the-hook-the-bee-and-the-trickbot-connection-686379311056)

**Mafalda**
- **描述**: Mafalda (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S1060) 可以枚举所有子键及其值的注册表键。
- **引用来源**: SentinelLabs. (2022, September 22). Metador Technical Appendix. Retrieved April 4, 2023. [链接](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1e9ZTW9b71YwFWS_18ZwDAxa-cYbV8q1wUefmKZLYVsA/edit#heading=h.lmnbtht1ikzm)

**BitPaymer**
- **描述**: BitPaymer (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0570) 通过设置运行键 HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run 来实现持久化。
- **引用来源**: Frankoff, S., Hartley, B. (2018, November 14). Big Game Hunting: The Evolution of INDRIK SPIDER From Dridex Wire Fraud to BitPaymer Targeted Ransomware. Retrieved January 6, 2021. [链接](https://www.crowdstrike.com/blog/big-game-hunting-the-evolution-of-indrik-spider-from-dridex-wire-fraud-to-bitpaymer-targeted-ransomware/)

**Sandworm Team**
- **描述**: Sandworm Team (MITRE ATT&CK ID: G0034) 使用商业工具 RemoteExec 进行无代理远程代码执行。
- **引用来源**: MSTIC. (2022, October 14). New “Prestige” ransomware impacts organizations in Ukraine and Poland. Retrieved January 19, 2023. [链接](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/security/blog/2022/10/14/new-prestige-ransomware-impacts-organizations-in-ukraine-and-poland/)

**GrimAgent**
- **描述**: GrimAgent (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0632) 可以使用 Windows 命令行 shell 执行命令，包括其自身的删除操作。
- **引用来源**: Priego, A. (2021, July). THE BROTHERS GRIM: THE REVERSING TALE OF GRIMAGENT MALWARE USED BY RYUK. Retrieved July 16, 2021. [链接](https://gibnc.group-ib.com/s/Group-IB_GrimAgent_analysis#pdfviewer)

**Doki**
- **描述**: Doki (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0600) 将文件伪装成 Linux 内核模块。
- **引用来源**: Fishbein, N., Kajiloti, M. (2020, July 28). Watch Your Containers: Doki Infecting Docker Servers in the Cloud. Retrieved March 30, 2021. [链接](https://www.intezer.com/blog/cloud-security/watch-your-containers-doki-infecting-docker-servers-in-the-cloud/)

**Proxysvc**
- **描述**: Proxysvc (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0238) 在系统上执行二进制文件，并将结果记录到临时文件中，使用以下命令：`cmd.exe /c " > %temp%\PM* .tmp 2>&1"`。
- **引用来源**: Sherstobitoff, R., Malhotra, A. (2018, April 24). Analyzing Operation GhostSecret: Attack Seeks to Steal Data Worldwide. Retrieved May 16, 2018. [链接](https://securingtomorrow.mcafee.com/mcafee-labs/analyzing-operation-ghostsecret-attack-seeks-to-steal-data-worldwide/)

**DEADEYE**
- **描述**: DEADEYE (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S1052) 对其有效载荷进行了加密。
- **引用来源**: Rufus Brown, Van Ta, Douglas Bienstock, Geoff Ackerman, John Wolfram. (2022, March 8). Does This Look Infected? A Summary of APT41 Targeting U.S. State Governments. Retrieved July 8, 2022. [链接](https://www.mandiant.com/resources/apt41-us-state-governments)

**XCSSET**
- **描述**: XCSSET (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0658) 根据机器的本地时间，在特定文件 `.report` 的初始创建时间戳后等待 43200 秒（12 小时）。等待时间结束后，XCSSET 会执行额外的模块。
- **引用来源**: Mac Threat Response, Mobile Research Team. (2020, August 13). The XCSSET Malware: Inserts Malicious Code Into Xcode Projects, Performs UXSS Backdoor Planting in Safari, and Leverages Two Zero-day Exploits. Retrieved October 5, 2021. [链接](https://documents.trendmicro.com/assets/pdf/XCSSET_Technical_Brief.pdf)

**EvilBunny**
- **描述**: EvilBunny (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0396) 使用三个不同的 API 在执行休眠操作前后测量时间，以检查并中止在沙箱环境中运行的恶意软件。
- **引用来源**: Marschalek, M. (2014, December 16). EvilBunny: Malware Instrumented By Lua. Retrieved June 28, 2019. [链接](https://web.archive.org/web/20150311013500/http://www.cyphort.com/evilbunny-malware-instrumented-lua/)

**China Chopper**
- **描述**: China Chopper (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0020) 的服务器组件可以对认证门户进行暴力破解密码猜测。
- **引用来源**: FireEye. (2018, March 16). Suspected Chinese Cyber Espionage Group (TEMP.Periscope) Targeting U.S. Engineering and Maritime Industries. Retrieved April 11, 2018. [链接](https://www.fireeye.com/blog/threat-research/2018/03/suspected-chinese-espionage-group-targeting-maritime-and-engineering-industries.html)

**Ryuk**
- **描述**: Ryuk (MITRE ATT&CK ID: S0446) 使用 C$ 网络共享进行横向移动。
- **引用来源**: Abrams, L. (2021, January 14). Ryuk Ransomware Uses Wake-on-Lan To Encrypt Offline Devices. Retrieved February 11, 2021. [链接](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/ryuk-ransomware-uses-wake-on-lan-to-encrypt-offline-devices/)
