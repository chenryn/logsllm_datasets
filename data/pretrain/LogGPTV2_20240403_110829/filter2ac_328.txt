**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

**Description:**
APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed deploying various tools after gaining lateral movement within a network using administrative accounts. This tactic allows the threat actor to extend their reach and maintain persistence within the compromised environment.
