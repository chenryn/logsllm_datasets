**ATT&CK ID:** S0045  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** ADVSTORESHELL

**Description:**
ADVSTORESHELL has been observed using `rundll32.exe` in a registry value to establish persistence. This technique allows the malware to execute automatically when the system starts, ensuring continued access to the compromised system.
