**ATT&CK ID:** T1562  
**Technique Name:** Impair Defenses

Adversaries may maliciously modify components within a victim's environment to hinder or disable defensive mechanisms. This technique encompasses the impairment of both preventative defenses, such as firewalls and antivirus software, and detection capabilities that defenders use to audit activity and identify malicious behavior. The modifications can affect both native defenses and additional security tools installed by users and administrators.
