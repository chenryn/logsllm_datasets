**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group employs several malware families that gather detailed information about the victim's system. This includes the type and version of the operating system, the computer name, and CPU information. Additionally, a variant similar to Destover, which is also used by the Lazarus Group, collects data on available disk space and transmits this information to its command and control (C2) server.[201][202][203][204][205][206]
