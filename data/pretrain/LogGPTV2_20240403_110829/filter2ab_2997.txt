**ATT&CK ID:** S0487  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Kessel

Kessel is a technique that can split the data to be exfiltrated into smaller chunks, which are then embedded in the subdomains of DNS queries. This method allows for the covert transmission of data by leveraging the DNS protocol. [7]
