**ATT&CK ID:** S0463  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** INSOMNIA

**Description:**
INSOMNIA employs malicious JavaScript and iframes to exploit vulnerabilities in WebKit on iOS 12 devices. This technique leverages these web-based elements to gain unauthorized access or execute arbitrary code, thereby compromising the security of the affected devices.
