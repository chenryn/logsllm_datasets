**ATT&CK ID:** M1031  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Intrusion Prevention

Network intrusion prevention systems (NIPS) and other security solutions designed to scan and block malicious downloads can be effectively used to prevent and mitigate unauthorized or malicious network activity. These systems monitor network traffic in real-time, identifying and blocking any suspicious or known malicious activities, thereby enhancing the overall security posture of the network.
