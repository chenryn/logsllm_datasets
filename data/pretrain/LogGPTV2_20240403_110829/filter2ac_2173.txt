**Title: Black Hat Europe 22 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways from Day 2**

To conclude Black Hat Europe 2022, join Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss and Review Board members Jiska Classen, Federico Maggi, Leigh-Anne Galloway, and Anant Shrivastava for an insightful discussion on the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community. This closing session, known as the Locknote, will feature a candid conversation on the key takeaways from the conference and how these trends will shape future InfoSec strategies.

During this session, the panelists will delve into the critical insights and emerging themes that have emerged over the course of the event. They will also explore the implications of these findings for the broader InfoSec landscape, providing valuable perspectives for both practitioners and leaders in the field.
