**ATT&CK ID:** T1591  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Gather Victim Organization Information

Adversaries may collect information about the targeted organization to facilitate their attack planning. This information can encompass a wide range of details, such as the names and functions of various divisions or departments, specifics about business operations, and the roles and responsibilities of key personnel. Such data can be leveraged to tailor phishing campaigns, social engineering efforts, or other tactics that exploit the internal structure and operational context of the victim organization.
