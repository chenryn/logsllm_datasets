**ATT&CK ID:** S0359  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Nltest

**Description:**
The `nltest` command can be utilized to enumerate the parent domain of a local machine by using the `/parentdomain` switch. This technique is often employed in reconnaissance activities to gather information about the target network's domain structure.
