Ã˜ Manually extracting SRs needs significant human efforts and is error-prone:
Ã˜ HTTP RFC specifications are lengthy (RFC 7230 includes 89 pages in total) 
Ã˜ Traditional regular templates or keyword-based approaches do not work well
Ã˜ RFC documents are described in natural language rather than formal 
language, in which the sentences are complex and flexible in expression.
Ã˜ The same semantics can be expressed in multiple forms, including 
synonym substitution and grammatical variations (e.g., passive tense)
