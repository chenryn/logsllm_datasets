**ATT&CK ID:** M1040  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Behavior Prevention on Endpoint

**Description:**
On Windows 10, enable Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) rules to prevent the execution of potentially obfuscated scripts. This measure helps mitigate the risk of malicious activities by blocking suspicious behaviors at the endpoint.
