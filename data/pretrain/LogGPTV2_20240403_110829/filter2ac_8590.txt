**ATT&CK ID:** G0128  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** ZIRCONIUM

ZIRCONIUM has employed a tool to steal credentials from installed web browsers, including Microsoft Internet Explorer and Google Chrome.[97]

---

### Summary
ZIRCONIUM utilizes a credential-stealing tool that targets web browsers such as Microsoft Internet Explorer and Google Chrome. This technique allows the group to access and exfiltrate sensitive information, such as usernames and passwords, stored within these browsers. [97]
