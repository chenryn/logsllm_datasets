## Command Editor

To access the command editor, click the **v** button located above the **Editor** pane. This will open the editor, where you can view and copy the automatically generated commands based on your inputs.

:::image type="content" source="media/data-explorer-ingestion-wizard-command-editor/edit-view.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the ingestion wizard edit view.":::

This feature allows you to easily manage and utilize the commands created by the system, streamlining your workflow and ensuring accuracy in your data ingestion process.
