**ATT&CK ID:** S0196  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PUNCHBUGGY

**Description:**
PUNCHBUGGY is a technique that involves mimicking filenames from the `%SYSTEM%\System32` directory to conceal malicious DLLs in the `%WINDIR%` and/or `%TEMP%` directories. This method helps attackers evade detection by blending in with legitimate system files.
