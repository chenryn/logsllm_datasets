**ATT&CK ID:** S0658  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** XCSSET

XCSSET employs AES-CBC encryption on files located in the `~/Documents`, `~/Downloads`, and `~/Desktop` directories, using a fixed key. The encrypted files are then renamed to have a `.enc` extension. Only files with sizes less than 500MB are targeted for encryption. [94]
