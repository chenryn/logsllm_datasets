**ATT&CK ID:** G0047  
**Technique Name:** Gamaredon Group

The Gamaredon Group has employed various tactics to deliver and execute malicious code. Specifically, they have embedded malicious macros in document templates that, when triggered, execute VBScript. Additionally, the group has distributed Microsoft Outlook VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) projects containing embedded macros. These methods enable the group to compromise systems and gain unauthorized access.
