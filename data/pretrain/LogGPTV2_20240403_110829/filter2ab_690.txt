**ATT&CK Technique Name:** [C0015 - Network Service Scanning](https://attack.mitre.org/techniques/C0015/)

During the execution of this technique (C0015), the threat actors employed several commands and tools to discover remote systems. Specifically, they utilized the `net view /all /domain` and `ping` commands to identify active hosts on the network. Additionally, they leveraged the `Invoke-ShareFinder` script from the PowerView PowerShell toolkit to enumerate file shares. [23]
