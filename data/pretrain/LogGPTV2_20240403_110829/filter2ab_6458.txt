**ATT&CK ID:** G0016  
**Technique Name:** APT29

**Description:**
APT29, a well-known threat actor, has employed Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) event subscriptions as a method for maintaining persistence on compromised systems. This technique allows the adversary to execute malicious code in response to specific system events, ensuring long-term access and control.
