**ATT&CK ID:** S1053  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** AvosLocker

AvosLocker is a ransomware strain that encrypts files and network resources using the AES-256 encryption algorithm. After encryption, it appends one of the following file extensions to the affected files: `.avos`, `.avos2`, or `.AvosLinux`.[11][12][13][14]
