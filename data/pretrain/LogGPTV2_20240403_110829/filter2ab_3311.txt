**ATT&CK ID:** G0092  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** TA505

**Description:**
TA505 has been observed using base64-encoded PowerShell commands to execute malicious activities. This technique helps in obfuscating the code, making it more difficult for security tools to detect and analyze the commands.
