User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 2.7, Allowlist Authorized Scripts?
Assistant: An organization should Use technical controls, such as digital signatures and version control, to ensure that only authorized scripts, such as specific .ps1, .py, etc., files, are allowed to execute. Block unauthorized scripts from executing. Reassess bi-annually, or more frequently.CIS Control 2.7 has the asset type Applications, and has the security function Protect.
