**ATT&CK ID:** M1042  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Disable or Remove Feature or Program

**Description:**
If Local Link Multicast Name Resolution (LLMNR) and NetBIOS are not required in your environment, it is recommended to disable them. This can be done through the local computer security settings or via Group Policy.
