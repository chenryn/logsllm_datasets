ATT&CK ID:G0010
ATT&CK Technique Name:Turla
Turla surveys a system upon check-in to discover network configuration details using the arp -a, nbtstat -n, net config, ipconfig /all, and route commands, as well as NBTscan.[83][238][239] Turla RPC backdoors have also retrieved registered RPC interface information from process memory.[240]
