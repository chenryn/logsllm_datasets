**ATT&CK ID:** G0094  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Kimsuky

Kimsuky, a known threat actor, has employed various tactics to compromise victims' browser security. Specifically, Kimsuky has utilized browser extensions, including those for Google Chrome, to steal passwords and cookies from victims' browsers. Additionally, Kimsuky has leveraged Nirsoft's WebBrowserPassView tool to extract and dump passwords obtained from compromised systems. [38][39][40][41]
