**ATT&CK ID:** M1011  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** User Guidance

**Guidance for Users:**

1. **Avoid Public Charging Stations and Computers:** Users should be advised against using public charging stations or computers to charge their devices. Instead, they should use chargers obtained from a trusted and reliable source.
   
2. **Trust Prompt Warnings:** Users should be instructed not to click on any device prompts asking them to trust an attached computer, unless it is absolutely necessary and the computer is known to be secure.

By following these guidelines, users can help protect their devices from potential security risks associated with untrusted charging sources and unauthorized connections.
