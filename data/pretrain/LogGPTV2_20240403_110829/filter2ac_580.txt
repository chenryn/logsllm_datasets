**ATT&CK ID:** S0036  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** FLASHFLOOD

**Description:**
FLASHFLOOD is a technique used to search for and collect files with specific or customized file extensions from the local system. The tool scans the following directories:

- My Recent Documents
- Desktop
- Temporary Internet Files
- TEMP

Additionally, FLASHFLOOD gathers information stored in the Windows Address Book.
