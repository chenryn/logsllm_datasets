**ATT&CK ID:** S0269  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** QUADAGENT

**Description:**
QUADAGENT modifies a registry key under the HKCU (HKEY_CURRENT_USER) hive to store a session identifier that is unique to the compromised system. Additionally, it stores a pre-shared key used for encrypting and decrypting command and control (C2) communications.
