**Title: Integration of Cyber Insurance into a Risk Management Program at Black Hat USA 2019**

**Session Overview:**
This session will delve into the current landscape of data breaches and explore the integration of cyber insurance into a comprehensive risk management program. Many information security professionals and incident responders are often unfamiliar with the intricacies of the cyber insurance process, particularly in the context of a data breach. This lack of understanding can significantly impact the effectiveness of the incident response process.

The discussion will cover key aspects of cyber insurance, including how it fits into an organization's overall risk management strategy, the requirements that must be met during a data breach, and the steps necessary to ensure compliance and maximize coverage. By the end of the session, attendees will have a clearer understanding of how to leverage cyber insurance as a critical component of their risk management framework.
