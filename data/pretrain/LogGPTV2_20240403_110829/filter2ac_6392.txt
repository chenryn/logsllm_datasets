**ATT&CK ID:** T1590.006  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Security Appliances

Adversaries may collect information about the victim's network security appliances to aid in their targeting efforts. This information can encompass a wide range of details, including the presence and specifics of deployed firewalls, content filters, and proxies or bastion hosts. Additionally, adversaries may seek information about network-based intrusion detection systems (NIDS) and other defensive cybersecurity appliances. Understanding the configuration and capabilities of these security measures can help attackers tailor their tactics to evade detection and successfully compromise the target network.
