**ATT&CK ID:** T1593  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Search Open Websites/Domains

Adversaries may search freely available websites and domains to gather information about potential victims. This information can be used for targeting purposes. Victim-related data may be found on various online platforms, including:

- **Social Media:** Profiles and posts that reveal personal and professional details.
- **News Sites:** Articles and reports that provide insights into the victim's activities and affiliations.
- **Business Operation Websites:** Sites that host information about hiring, requested contracts, and awarded contracts.

By collecting and analyzing this publicly available information, adversaries can build a detailed profile of their targets, which can be leveraged in subsequent attacks.
