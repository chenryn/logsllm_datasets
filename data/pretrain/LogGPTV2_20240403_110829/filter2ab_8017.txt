**ATT&CK ID:** G0006  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT1

APT1, a well-known threat group, has been observed creating email accounts for various malicious purposes. These email accounts are used in social engineering campaigns, phishing attacks, and for registering domains. [1]
