**ATT&CK ID:** M1031  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Intrusion Prevention

Network intrusion prevention systems (NIPS) and email security solutions designed to scan and remove malicious attachments or links can be effectively deployed to block and mitigate such activities. These systems work by monitoring network traffic in real-time, identifying potential threats, and taking proactive measures to prevent them from compromising the network. Additionally, email security solutions can help in filtering out suspicious emails and attachments, reducing the risk of phishing attacks and malware infections.
