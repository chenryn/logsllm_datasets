## Steps to Reproduce
What I am trying to do is to let the user pick an image or take one with the
camera. After that I want him to see it in a new screen and let him write a
description or something. However, the screen seems a bit off after the
image_picker screen is closing, and the new one is pushing in from the bottom.
My hypothesis is that flutter tries to calculate the top position for new
pushed screen based on the free space it has on screen. When the image_picker
screen is closing, it might be barely visible and when the new screen is
coming in, it still takes the closing image_picker screen into account and
sets the top position wrong.
This is what it looks like:

text](https://github.com/btastic/image_picker_repro/blob/master/2018-01-25_20h23_22.png?raw=true)
