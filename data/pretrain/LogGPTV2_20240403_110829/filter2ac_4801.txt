**ATT&CK ID:** T1565.002  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Transmitted Data Manipulation

Adversaries may manipulate data while it is being transmitted to storage or other systems, in order to influence external outcomes or conceal their activities. This technique poses a significant threat to the integrity of the data. By altering transmitted data, adversaries can attempt to disrupt business processes, distort organizational understanding, and influence decision-making.
