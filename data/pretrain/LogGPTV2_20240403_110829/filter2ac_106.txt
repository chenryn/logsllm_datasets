**ATT&CK ID:** S0533  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SLOTHFULMEDIA

**Description:**
SLOTHFULMEDIA is a technique that involves collecting system information from a compromised machine. Specifically, it gathers details such as the system name, operating system version, network adapter information, memory usage, and disk information.[337]
