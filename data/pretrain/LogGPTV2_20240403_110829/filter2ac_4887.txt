**ATT&CK ID:** S0413  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** MailSniper

**Description:**
MailSniper is a tool that can be used to perform password spraying attacks against Microsoft Exchange and Office 365 environments. This technique involves attempting to access multiple user accounts with a small set of commonly used passwords, thereby increasing the likelihood of success without triggering account lockout policies.
