**ATT&CK ID:** S0125  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Remsec

**Description:**
Remsec includes a plugin designed to drop and execute vulnerable versions of the Outpost Sandbox or avast! Virtualization drivers. This technique is used to escalate privileges to kernel mode, thereby providing the attacker with deeper access to the system.[30]

---

This version clarifies the purpose of the plugin and the specific drivers it targets, while also maintaining a professional and clear tone.
