**ATT&CK ID:** M1018  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** User Account Management

**Description:**
To enhance security, it is essential to apply the principle of least privilege to Identity and Access Management (IAM) policies. This ensures that users and systems have only the minimum levels of access necessary to perform their required functions, thereby reducing the potential attack surface and mitigating the risk of unauthorized access or actions.
