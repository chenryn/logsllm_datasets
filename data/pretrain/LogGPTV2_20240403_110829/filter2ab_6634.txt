**ATT&CK ID:** G0010  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Turla

**Description:**
Turla, a well-known threat actor, has been observed utilizing the virtual private server (VPS) infrastructure of compromised Iranian threat actors.[1] This tactic allows Turla to obfuscate their activities and potentially conduct operations with reduced risk of detection.
