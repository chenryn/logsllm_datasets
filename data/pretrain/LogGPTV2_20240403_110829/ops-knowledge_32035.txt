User: What is container orchestration technology? Please list at least two commonly used container orchestration software.
Assistant: Container orchestration technology is a tool for managing containerized applications. It can automate the deployment, expansion, and management of applications, and provides functions such as automatic load balancing, service discovery, and service registration. Commonly used container orchestration software includes: 1. Kubernetes: An open source container orchestration platform launched by Google, which provides flexible container deployment and management functions, and supports automatic expansion, load balancing, service discovery and other functions. 2. Docker Swarm: The container orchestration platform launched by Docker supports the automatic deployment and management of Docker containers, and can achieve high availability and automatic expansion of containers.
