**ATT&CK ID:** S0326  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** RedDrop

RedDrop employs advertisements or other links embedded within websites to entice users to download malicious applications. This process leverages a complex content distribution network (CDN) and a series of network redirects. Additionally, RedDrop downloads further components, such as APKs and JAR files, from various command-and-control (C2) servers.
