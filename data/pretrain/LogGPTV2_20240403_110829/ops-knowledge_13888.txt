User: Answer the following questions What are the common ways to fix bugs?
Assistant: 1. Patching: Fix vulnerabilities by updating software or system patches. 2. Restrict access: Reduce the harmfulness of vulnerabilities by restricting access to functions, files, databases, etc. affected by the vulnerability. 3. Strengthen authentication and authorization: Prevent illegal users from accessing the system and limit their permissions by strengthening user authentication and authorization mechanisms. 4. Data verification and filtering: By verifying and filtering the data entered by users, it prevents vulnerabilities caused by malicious input. 5. Enhanced log monitoring and analysis: discover and deal with possible vulnerabilities in a timely manner by enhancing log monitoring and analysis. 6. Security audit and test: Through security audit and test of the system, loopholes are discovered and repaired, and hidden dangers are eliminated in time.
