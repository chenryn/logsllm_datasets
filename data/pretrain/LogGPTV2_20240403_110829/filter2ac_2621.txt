**ATT&CK ID:** G0016  
**APT Group Name:** APT29  
**Technique Used:** Kerberos Ticket Attacks for Lateral Movement

**Description:**
APT29, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed using Kerberos ticket attacks as a method for lateral movement within compromised networks. This technique allows the group to authenticate and access other systems by exploiting or misusing Kerberos tickets.
