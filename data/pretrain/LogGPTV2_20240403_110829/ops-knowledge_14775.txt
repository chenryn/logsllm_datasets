User: How to perform data transmission and communication between multiple computers through the TCP/IP protocol?
Assistant: Through the TCP/IP protocol, data transmission and communication between multiple computers can be carried out through the following steps: 1. Confirm the IP address and port number of the computer. 2. Confirm the data type and format to be transmitted. 3. Establish a TCP connection: After the client initiates a connection request, the server responds and establishes a connection. 4. Data transfer: transfer data through the connection. 5. Close the connection: After the data transmission is completed, choose to close the connection according to the situation. In practical applications, such as Web browsers, FTP clients, Telnet clients, etc., are based on the TCP/IP protocol for data transmission and communication.
