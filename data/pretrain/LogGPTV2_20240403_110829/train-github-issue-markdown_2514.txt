#### How to reproduce the bug
  1. Create dashboard with that structure:  
Three charts in three tabs, with three dashboard filters, each filter for own
tab.  
Scope of filter 1 is for tab 1, scope of filter 2 is for tab 2 and scope of
filter 3 is for tab 3.  
Save this dashboard by name "bug_report_20221220"
or (bestway)
upload dashboard from file: dashboard_export_20221220T112259.zip  
Attention! used dataset "birth_names" from Examples
  2. Go to "Dashboards" menu item of Superset.
  3. Click to dashboard by name "bug_report_20221220"
  4. You will see that charts in tabs don't display until you unfold out of scope filters.
Attention! You may need to open the dashboard "bug_report_20221220" twice to
reproduce unexpected behavior.
### Expected results
Сharts in tabs will display without you unfold out of scope filters.
### Actual results
Сharts in tabs don't display until you unfold out of scope filters.
