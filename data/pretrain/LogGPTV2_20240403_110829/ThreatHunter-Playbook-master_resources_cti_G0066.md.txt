# G0066: Elderwood

## Description
Elderwood, identified as [G0066](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0066) by MITRE, is a suspected Chinese cyber espionage group. It was reportedly responsible for the 2009 Google intrusion known as Operation Aurora. (Citation: Security Affairs Elderwood Sept 2012) The group has targeted a wide range of organizations, including defense organizations, supply chain manufacturers, human rights and nongovernmental organizations (NGOs), and IT service providers. (Citations: Symantec Elderwood Sept 2012, CSM Elderwood Sept 2012)
