**ATT&CK ID:** S0262  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** QuasarRAT

QuasarRAT is capable of enumerating the Wide Area Network (WAN) IP address by making requests to external services such as `ip-api[.]com`, `freegeoip[.]net`, or `api[.]ipify[.]org`. These requests are typically observed with a user-agent string of `Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.3; rv:48.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/48.0`.[191]
