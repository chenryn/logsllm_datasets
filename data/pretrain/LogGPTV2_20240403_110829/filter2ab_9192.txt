**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT28

**Description:**
APT28, a well-known threat actor, has been observed conducting credential phishing campaigns. These campaigns typically involve the use of embedded links that direct victims to attacker-controlled domains. This tactic is designed to harvest sensitive information, such as login credentials, from unsuspecting users.
