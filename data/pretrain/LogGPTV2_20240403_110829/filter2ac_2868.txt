**ATT&CK ID:** M0930  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Segmentation

To enhance security, deny direct remote access to internal systems by implementing network proxies, gateways, and firewalls. Consider using a jump server or a host in the Demilitarized Zone (DMZ) for more granular access control. Additionally, leverage the DMZ or corporate resources to manage vendor access. [7]
