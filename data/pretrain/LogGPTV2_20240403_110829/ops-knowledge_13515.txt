User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 1.2, Address Unauthorized Assets?
Assistant: An organization should Ensure that a process exists to address unauthorized assets on a weekly basis. The enterprise may choose to remove the asset from the network, deny the asset from connecting remotely to the network, or quarantine the asset.CIS Control 1.2 has the asset type Devices, and has the security function Respond.
