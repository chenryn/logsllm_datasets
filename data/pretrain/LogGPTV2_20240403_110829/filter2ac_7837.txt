**ATT&CK ID:** G0035  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Dragonfly

**Description:**
Dragonfly has employed a technique involving the injection of SMB (Server Message Block) URLs into malicious Word documents used in spear-phishing attacks. This method is designed to trigger Forced Authentication, thereby enabling the attackers to capture user credentials. [14]
