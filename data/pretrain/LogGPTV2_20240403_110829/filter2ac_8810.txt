**ATT&CK ID:** G0122  
**Technique Name:** Silent Librarian

**Description:**
Silent Librarian, also known as Cobalt Dickens, has been observed gathering email addresses from targeted organizations through open Internet searches. These collected email addresses are subsequently used in phishing campaigns to deceive and exploit the targeted individuals. [20]
