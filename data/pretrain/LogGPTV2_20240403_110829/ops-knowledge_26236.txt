User: How to schedule periodic restarts of system services using Crontab?
Assistant: 1. Open the terminal and enter the command sudo crontab -e to enter the Crontab editing interface. 2. Add the following command on the last line: @reboot /sbin/reboot. 3. Save and exit the editing interface. 4. Reboot the system, the service will restart periodically. Explanation of the command: @reboot means to execute the command when the system restarts; /sbin/reboot means to restart the system.
