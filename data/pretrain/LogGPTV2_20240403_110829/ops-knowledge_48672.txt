User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 7.5, Perform Automated Vulnerability Scans of Internal Enterprise Assets?
Assistant: An organization should Perform automated vulnerability scans of internal enterprise assets on a quarterly, or more frequent, basis. Conduct both authenticated and unauthenticated scans, using a SCAP-compliant vulnerability scanning tool.CIS Control 7.5 has the asset type Applications, and has the security function Identify.
