User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 15.2, Establish and Maintain a Service Provider Management Policy?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and maintain a service provider management policy. Ensure the policy addresses the classification, inventory, assessment, monitoring, and decommissioning of service providers. Review and update the policy annually, or when significant enterprise changes occur that could impact this Safeguard.CIS Control 15.2 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Identify.
