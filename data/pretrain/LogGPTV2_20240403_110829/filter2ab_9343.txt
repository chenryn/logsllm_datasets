**ATT&CK ID:** S0013  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PlugX

**Description:**
PlugX can be installed as a service to achieve persistence on a compromised system. Additionally, it includes a module that allows attackers to modify service configurations, as well as start, control, and delete services. [89][90][91][92][93]
