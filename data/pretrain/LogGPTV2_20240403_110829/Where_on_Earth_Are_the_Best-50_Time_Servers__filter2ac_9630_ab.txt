#### Performance Summary

- **Strata**: Indicates the hierarchical level of the server.
- **List S1Down**: Indicates the number of times the server was down.
- **Class Size**: Indicates the size of the class.
- **Errtime (ms)**: Indicates the error time in milliseconds.
- **Leap**: Indicates the leap second status.
- **Perf**: Indicates the performance of the server.
- **Avail. (%)**: Indicates the availability percentage of the server.

Servers with bold entries in the columns indicate that they failed to meet the "Best-50" criteria in one or more categories. The performance metrics help in identifying the most reliable and accurate time servers globally.
