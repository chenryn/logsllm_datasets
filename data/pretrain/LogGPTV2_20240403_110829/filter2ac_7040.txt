**ATT&CK ID:** G0096  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT41

APT41, a well-known threat actor, has been observed using the WMIEXEC utility to execute `whoami` commands on remote machines. This technique allows the group to gather information about the user context under which processes are running on the targeted systems.[17]
