gating the effect of the pattern length. They should be considered in
the upcoming studies. Moreover, it should also be studied whether
such a guide affects the strength of the patterns in terms of security,
and whether the gesture-based pattern lock system can survive
with such guided lock patterns from the perspectives of behavioral
authentication.
