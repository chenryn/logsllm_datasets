**ATT&CK ID:** M1055  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Do Not Mitigate

**Environmental Keying** should generally not be mitigated using preventative controls, as this approach may inadvertently protect unintended targets from being compromised. If an adversary targets Environmental Keying, the focus should be on preventing their tools from executing earlier in the attack chain and on detecting and responding to any subsequent malicious behavior if a compromise does occur.
