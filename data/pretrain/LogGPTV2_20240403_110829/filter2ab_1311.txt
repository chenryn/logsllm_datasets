**Title: Black Hat Europe NOC Report 2022**

The Black Hat NOC team is back with another year of insightful and often surprising statistics. We will be sharing the data that continues to both perplex and entertain us, year after year. In this report, we will detail the tools and techniques we employ to set up, stabilize, and secure the network. Additionally, we will highlight the changes and improvements we have implemented over the past year to enhance our operations.

As always, we will also share some of the more humorous and noteworthy network activities. These insights provide a unique perspective on how security professionals behave on an open WiFi network, offering valuable lessons for all attendees.
