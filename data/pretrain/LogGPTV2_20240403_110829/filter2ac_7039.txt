**ATT&CK ID:** G0045  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** menuPass

**Description:**
menuPass has been known to stage data on remote Managed Service Provider (MSP) systems or other victim networks prior to exfiltration. This technique allows the threat actor to prepare and consolidate the targeted information before transferring it out of the network.
