**ATT&CK ID:** T1592.001  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Gather Victim Host Information - Hardware

Adversaries may collect information about the victim's host hardware to inform their targeting strategies. This information can encompass a wide range of details, including the types and versions of hardware components on specific hosts. Additionally, adversaries may look for the presence of specialized components that could indicate enhanced defensive measures, such as smart card or biometric readers, dedicated encryption hardware, and other security-related devices. Understanding the hardware landscape helps adversaries tailor their attacks more effectively and potentially bypass or exploit specific defenses.
