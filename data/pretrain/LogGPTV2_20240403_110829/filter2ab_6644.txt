**ATT&CK ID:** S0421  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** GolfSpy

GolfSpy is a malicious tool capable of collecting various types of data from a compromised device. Specifically, it can gather the following information:
- Local user accounts
- Images stored on the device
- Bookmarks and browsing history from the default web browser
- Files stored on the SD card

Additionally, GolfSpy has the ability to list and enumerate files of different types, including images, audio, video, and other file formats, that are stored on the device. It can also copy any arbitrary files from the device to an external location.
