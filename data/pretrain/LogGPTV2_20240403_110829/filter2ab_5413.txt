**ATT&CK ID:** M0926  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Privileged Account Management

**Description:**
Implement the principle of least privilege for service accounts. This ensures that service accounts have only the permissions necessary to perform their specific functions, thereby reducing the potential impact of a compromise.
