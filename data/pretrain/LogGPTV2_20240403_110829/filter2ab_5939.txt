**ATT&CK ID:** M1006  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Use Recent OS Version

In Android 10, several changes were implemented to prevent malicious applications from completely hiding their icons in the launcher. These modifications enhance the security and transparency of the operating system, making it more difficult for malicious apps to operate undetected.
