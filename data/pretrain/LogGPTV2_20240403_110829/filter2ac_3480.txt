**ATT&CK ID:** S0386  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Ursnif

**Description:**
Ursnif, a well-known malware, has been observed using the `reg` command to query the Windows Registry for information on installed programs. This technique allows the malware to gather intelligence about the victim's system, which can be used for further exploitation or to tailor its malicious activities. References [97] and [98] provide additional details on this behavior.
