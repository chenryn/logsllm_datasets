cess pattern revealing) protocols, as practitioners will choose
ORAM over both less secure and less performant solutions.
We found our framework to be a powerful tool for analyz-
ing the protocols, and we hope developers of new protocols
will contribute implementations and evaluate them.
An important future work is to understand better the
meaning of the diﬀerent leakage proﬁles and their implica-
tions. Furthermore, another direction is to try to improve
the performance of the most secure schemes (e.g. [15]).
