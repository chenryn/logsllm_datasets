**ATT&CK ID:** T1591.003  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Identify Business Tempo

Adversaries may gather information about a victim's business tempo to inform and refine their targeting strategies. This information can encompass various details, such as the organization’s operational hours and days of the week. Additionally, it may reveal specific times and dates of purchases and shipments related to the victim’s hardware and software resources. Understanding these patterns allows adversaries to identify optimal windows for conducting malicious activities, thereby increasing the likelihood of successful infiltration and minimizing the risk of detection.
