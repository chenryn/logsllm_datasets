**ATT&CK ID:** G0131  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Tonto Team

Tonto Team has exploited several Microsoft vulnerabilities to execute their delivered malicious payloads. The specific vulnerabilities include:

- CVE-2018-0798
- CVE-2018-8174
- CVE-2018-0802
- CVE-2017-11882
- CVE-2019-9489
- CVE-2020-8468

These exploits have been documented in various sources [84][85][86][87].
