**Title: Botconf2022 RTM: Sinkholing the Botnet**

In this presentation, we discuss the discovery of a critical flaw in the Command and Control (C&C) calculation algorithm of the RTM botnet. By exploiting this logical weakness, we were able to sinkhole the botnet, which provided us with a comprehensive list of compromised machines and the capability to disrupt and shut down the entire botnet.
