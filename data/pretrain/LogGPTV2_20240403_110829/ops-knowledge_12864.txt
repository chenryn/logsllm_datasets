User: Describe the principles of landing page design and give a code implementation of a sample landing page.
Assistant: Principles of landing page design: 1. Concise and clear: The content of the landing page should be concise and clear, highlight the key points, and not be too complicated and confusing. 2. Adapt to various devices: The landing page should be a responsive design and adapt to various devices, including mobile phones, tablets, computers, etc. 3. Emphasize the CTA: The “Call to Action” button on the landing page should be highlighted to entice people to take action. 4. Strong visual impact: The landing page background image or header banner should be high-quality and attractive. 5. Clear brand image: The landing page can clearly and clearly reflect the brand image.
