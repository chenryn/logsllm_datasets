User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 6.2, Establish an Access Revoking Process?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and follow a process, preferably automated, for revoking access to enterprise assets, through disabling accounts immediately upon termination, rights revocation, or role change of a user. Disabling accounts, instead of deleting accounts, may be necessary to preserve audit trails.CIS Control 6.2 has the asset type Users, and has the security function Protect.
