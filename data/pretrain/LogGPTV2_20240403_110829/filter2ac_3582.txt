**ATT&CK ID:** G0092  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** TA505

**Description:**
TA505 is known for employing various lures to entice users into clicking on links or opening attachments in emails. These lures often involve disguising malware as legitimate files, such as Microsoft Word documents, PDFs, and .lnk (shortcut) files. [93][94][95][96][97][98][99][100]

This approach leverages social engineering techniques to increase the likelihood that unsuspecting users will interact with the malicious content, thereby facilitating the initial infection phase of an attack.
