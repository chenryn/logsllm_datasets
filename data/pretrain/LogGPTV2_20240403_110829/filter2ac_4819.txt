**ATT&CK ID:** M1040  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Behavior Prevention on Endpoint

**Description:**
On Windows 10 and later versions, enable Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) rules to block the execution of potentially obfuscated scripts. This measure helps mitigate the risk of malicious activities by preventing suspicious or obfuscated code from running on endpoints.
