**ATT&CK ID:** G0037  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** FIN6

FIN6 has been known to use Metasploit's PsExec NTDSGRAB module to obtain a copy of the victim's Active Directory database. This technique allows the group to exfiltrate sensitive information, including user credentials and other critical data stored within the Active Directory.
