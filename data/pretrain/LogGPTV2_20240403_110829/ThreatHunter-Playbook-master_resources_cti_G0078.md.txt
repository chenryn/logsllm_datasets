# G0078: Gorgon Group
## Description
The [Gorgon Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0078) is a threat actor with suspected ties to Pakistan. This group has been involved in a combination of criminal and targeted attacks, targeting government organizations in the United Kingdom, Spain, Russia, and the United States. (Citation: Unit 42 Gorgon Group Aug 2018)

## Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs)
Below is a detailed breakdown of the tactics, techniques, and procedures used by the Gorgon Group, along with the relevant data sources for detection.
