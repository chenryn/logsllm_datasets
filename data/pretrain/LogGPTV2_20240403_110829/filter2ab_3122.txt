**ATT&CK ID:** G0126  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Higaisa

**Description:**
Higaisa, a known malicious tool, has been observed to deploy and schedule the execution of `officeupdate.exe` via the Windows Task Scheduler. This method allows the malware to maintain persistence on the affected system. For further details, refer to references [80] and [81].
