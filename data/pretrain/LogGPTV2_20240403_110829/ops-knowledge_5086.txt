User: What are active and passive safety?
Assistant: Active safety refers to measures to prevent accidents or dangers, mainly reflected in the design of products. For example, airbags, windshield wipers, and anti-skid braking systems in cars are all active safety measures. Passive safety refers to measures to protect the safety of people in the event of an accident or danger. Passive safety is mainly reflected in the manufacture and use of products. For example, the material and structure of the car body, seat belts, frame, etc. are all passive safety measures.
