User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 4.3, Configure Automatic Session Locking on Enterprise Assets?
Assistant: An organization should Configure automatic session locking on enterprise assets after a defined period of inactivity. For general purpose operating systems, the period must not exceed 15 minutes. For mobile end-user devices, the period must not exceed 2 minutes.CIS Control 4.3 has the asset type Users, and has the security function Protect.
