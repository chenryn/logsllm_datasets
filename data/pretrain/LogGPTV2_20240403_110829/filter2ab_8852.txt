ATT&CK ID:M1048
ATT&CK Technique Name:Application Isolation and Sandboxing
Browser sandboxes can be used to mitigate some of the impact of exploitation, but sandbox escapes may still exist. [94] [95]Other types of virtualization and application microsegmentation may also mitigate the impact of client-side exploitation. Risks of additional exploits and weaknesses in those systems may still exist. [95]
