**ATT&CK ID:** M1015  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Active Directory Configuration

**Description:**
Manage the access control list (ACL) for "Replicating Directory Changes" and other permissions related to domain controller replication. This includes ensuring that only authorized users and systems have the necessary permissions to replicate directory changes, thereby reducing the risk of unauthorized access and potential security breaches.
