**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed using JavaScript for both drive-by downloads and command-and-control (C2) communications. This technique allows the group to deliver malicious payloads and maintain persistent control over compromised systems.
