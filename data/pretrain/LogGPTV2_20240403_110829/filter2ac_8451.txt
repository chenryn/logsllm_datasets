**Title: BotConf 2014 - Ad Fraud Botnets 101**

In recent years, ad fraud botnets have emerged as a significant threat to the online advertising industry, with their financial impact on advertisers receiving increasing attention in the media. In this presentation, we will provide an overview of the online advertising ecosystem and explain how advertisers inadvertently contribute to the proliferation of online fraud. We will also introduce the most common types of ad fraud currently being perpetrated.

Additionally, we will delve into the technical intricacies of ad fraud botnet operations, and discuss the measures that ad networks and exchanges can implement to protect advertisers from these threats. To illustrate our points, we will examine several prominent ad fraud botnet families.

This talk aims to equip attendees with a comprehensive understanding of the ad fraud landscape and the strategies needed to mitigate its risks.
