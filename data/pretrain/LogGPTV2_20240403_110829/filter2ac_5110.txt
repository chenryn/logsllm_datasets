**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed using the `netstat -anpo tcp` command to enumerate and display active TCP connections on a compromised machine. This technique allows the threat actor to gather information about network connections, which can be used for further exploitation or to maintain persistence. [11]
