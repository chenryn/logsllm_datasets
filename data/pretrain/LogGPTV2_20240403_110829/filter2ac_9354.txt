**Title: Black Hat Asia 2020 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways from Day 2**

As the second day of this year's virtual conference comes to a close, we invite you to join members of the Black Hat Review Board—Mika Devonshire, Seungjoo Kim, Asuka Nakajima, and Neil Wyler—for an insightful and candid discussion. The panel will delve into the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community, highlighting key takeaways from the day's sessions. This Locknote session will also explore how these emerging trends and insights will shape future InfoSec strategies, providing valuable perspectives for both practitioners and leaders in the field.
