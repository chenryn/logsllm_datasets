**ATT&CK ID:** S0655  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** BusyGasper

**Description:**
BusyGasper is a malicious tool that can perform several actions on a compromised device. Specifically, it can:

- Send an SMS message after the device boots, containing logs.
- Send messages to numbers specified by the adversary, with custom content.
- Delete all SMS messages on the device.
