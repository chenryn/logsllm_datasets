**ATT&CK ID:** S0604  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Industroyer

The Industroyer SIPROTEC DoS (Denial of Service) module exploits the CVE-2015-5374 vulnerability to render Siemens SIPROTEC devices unresponsive. Although this vulnerability does not directly cause the device to restart or shut down, it necessitates a manual restart of the affected device in order to restore normal operations. [1]
