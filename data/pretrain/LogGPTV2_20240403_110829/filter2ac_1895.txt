**ATT&CK ID:** G0027  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Threat Group-3390

Threat Group-3390 actors employ the `at` command to schedule tasks that execute self-extracting RAR archives. These archives are designed to install either HTTPBrowser or PlugX malware on other systems within the network. [8]
