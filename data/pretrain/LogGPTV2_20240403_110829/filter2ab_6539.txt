**Title: Black Hat US-19: Ghidra - From Classified NSA Tool to Open Source**

This year marked a significant milestone for the National Security Agency (NSA) with the release of our groundbreaking software reverse engineering (SRE) framework, Ghidra, to the open source community. The journey to this release was both lengthy and challenging. At Black Hat, we are excited to offer attendees the opportunity to hear from two of our leading experts. They will discuss the development of Ghidra, its capabilities, and the intricate process of making it publicly available.

Additionally, we will share insights into the unique experiences of NSA researchers as they engage with the dynamic and ever-evolving nature of an open source tool. We will also delve into the unexpected social media attention that Ghidra has garnered, providing a comprehensive view of its impact and the challenges and opportunities that come with it.
