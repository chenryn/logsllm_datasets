User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 3.11, Encrypt Sensitive Data at Rest?
Assistant: An organization should Encrypt sensitive data at rest on servers, applications, and databases containing sensitive data. Storage-layer encryption, also known as server-side encryption, meets the minimum requirement of this Safeguard. Additional encryption methods may include application-layer encryption, also known as client-side encryption, where access to the data storage device(s) does not permit access to the plain-text data. CIS Control 3.11 has the asset type Data, and has the security function Protect.
