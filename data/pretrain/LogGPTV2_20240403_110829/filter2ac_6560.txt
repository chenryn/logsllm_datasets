**ATT&CK ID:** G0022  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT3

APT3, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, employs a downloader that establishes SOCKS5 connections for its initial command and control (C2) communications. This method allows the malware to route traffic through a proxy, enhancing its stealth and making it more difficult to detect and block.
