**ATT&CK ID:** G0071  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Orangeworm

Orangeworm has been observed to propagate its backdoor across open network shares, specifically targeting the following directories: `ADMIN$`, `C$\\WINDOWS`, `D$\\WINDOWS`, and `E$\\WINDOWS`.[32]
