**ATT&CK ID:** M1032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Multi-Factor Authentication

**Recommendation:**
Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) for both user and privileged accounts. Many embedded network devices support TACACS+ and RADIUS, which can be used to enforce MFA. Additionally, follow the vendor's best practices for hardening access control.
