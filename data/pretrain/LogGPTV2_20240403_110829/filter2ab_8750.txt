**ATT&CK ID:** S0442  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** VBShower

**Description:**
VBShower is a technique that involves attempting to retrieve a VBScript (VBS) from command and control (C2) servers via HTTP. [359]

This technique is typically used by malicious actors to download and execute scripts on a compromised system, enabling further exploitation or data exfiltration.
