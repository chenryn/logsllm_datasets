**ATT&CK ID:** G0069  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** MuddyWater

MuddyWater, a known threat actor, has frequently employed tactics to entice users into enabling macros and executing malicious Microsoft Word documents. These documents are typically delivered through spearphishing emails, as documented in multiple reports.[150][151][152][153][154][155][156][157][158][159]
