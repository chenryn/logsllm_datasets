**ATT&CK ID:** S0145  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** POWERSOURCE

**Description:**
POWERSOURCE establishes persistence by creating a Registry Run key. The specific path for this key varies based on whether the victim's account has user or administrative privileges.[200]
