**ATT&CK ID:** M0922  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Restrict File and Directory Permissions

**Description:**
Implement file system access controls to safeguard system and application folders. This involves setting appropriate permissions to restrict unauthorized access, modification, or deletion of critical files and directories. By enforcing strict access controls, you can significantly reduce the risk of data breaches and ensure the integrity and confidentiality of your system and applications.
