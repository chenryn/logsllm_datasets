**ATT&CK ID:** S0488  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** CrackMapExec

**Description:**
CrackMapExec is a powerful tool that can be used to discover active IP addresses and their corresponding machine names within a targeted network. [30]

This tool is particularly useful for reconnaissance and enumeration in penetration testing and red team exercises, as it provides detailed information about the devices present on the network.
