**ATT&CK ID:** S0049  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** GeminiDuke

**Description:**
GeminiDuke is a technique used to gather information on programs and services that are configured to automatically start when the victim's system boots. This information can be leveraged to understand the environment and potentially identify additional targets for further exploitation.
