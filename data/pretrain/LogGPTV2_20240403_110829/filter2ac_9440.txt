**ATT&CK ID:** C0009  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Oldsmar Water Treatment Plant Intrusion

In the Oldsmar Water Treatment Plant intrusion, the threat actors exploited the operator's Human-Machine Interface (HMI) via the graphical user interface. This method allowed the attackers to make changes directly on the operator's screen, leading to their immediate detection by the plant operators. [1]
