**ATT&CK ID:** S0318  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** XLoader for Android

**Description:**
XLoader for Android is known to retrieve its command and control (C2) server addresses from encoded usernames on social media platforms, including Twitter, Instagram, and Tumblr.[5]
