**ATT&CK ID:** S0526  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** KGH_SPY

**Description:**
KGH_SPY is capable of setting the `HKCU\Environment\UserInitMprLogonScript` registry key to execute logon scripts. This technique allows the malware to run specific scripts during the user's logon process, thereby maintaining persistence and potentially executing additional malicious activities.
