**ATT&CK ID:** G0029  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Scarlet Mimic

**Description:**
Scarlet Mimic has employed the left-to-right override (LRO) character in the filenames of self-extracting RAR archives used in spear-phishing attacks. This technique manipulates the visual display of the filename, potentially deceiving the target into executing a malicious file.
