**ATT&CK ID:** T1592.004  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Client Configurations

Adversaries may collect information about the victim's client configurations to aid in their targeting efforts. This information can include a variety of details and settings, such as:

- **Operating System and Version:** The specific operating system and its version.
- **Virtualization:** Whether the system is running on a virtual machine or physical hardware.
- **Architecture:** The system architecture, such as 32-bit or 64-bit.
- **Language:** The language settings of the system.
- **Time Zone:** The time zone configured on the system.

Gathering this data allows adversaries to tailor their attacks more effectively, increasing the likelihood of successful exploitation.
