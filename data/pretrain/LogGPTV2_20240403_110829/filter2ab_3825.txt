**ATT&CK ID:** C0024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SolarWinds Compromise

In the SolarWinds Compromise, APT29 (also known as Nobelium) achieved initial network access to several victims through a trojanized update of the SolarWinds Orion software. This malicious update allowed the threat actor to gain a foothold in the targeted networks, facilitating further malicious activities.
