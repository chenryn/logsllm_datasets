title:botconf2022 Identifying malware campaigns on a budget
Malware campaigns plague enterprises, entrepreneurs, and individuals. Platforms and tools have been deployed to gain insight into the ongoing situation. Unfortunately, many of these platforms are rather pricey, which is a problem for me, as a student.This talk will explain several concepts that will provide insight into campaigns, whilst keeping the total cost below $50. Analysts and students alike can use and expand upon these techniques in their own research.
