**ATT&CK ID:** S0262  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** QuasarRAT

If the QuasarRAT client process does not have administrator privileges, it will create a registry key in `HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run` to achieve persistence. [215][216]
