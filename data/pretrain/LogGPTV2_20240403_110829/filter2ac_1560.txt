**ATT&CK ID:** S0428  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PoetRAT

**Description:**
PoetRAT is a remote access trojan (RAT) that has the capability to capture screenshots from the infected system. This feature allows the attacker to gather visual information about the user's activities and potentially sensitive data displayed on the screen.
