**ATT&CK ID:** M1040  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Behavior Prevention on Endpoint

**Recommendation:**
On Windows 10, enable Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) rules to secure the Local Security Authority Subsystem Service (LSASS) and prevent credential theft. [21]
