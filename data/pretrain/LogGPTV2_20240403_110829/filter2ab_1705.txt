**Title: Black Hat Asia 2022 Keynote: Fireside Chat with U.S. National Cyber Director Chris Inglis**

Join the inaugural U.S. National Cyber Director, Chris Inglis, and Jeff Moss for an engaging conversation on the evolving relationship between governments and the private sector. Director Inglis will share his optimistic vision for the future of the internet and discuss the responsibilities we all share to achieve this vision.

The discussion will cover:
- What it takes for governments to be more effective partners in cybersecurity.
- The roles and responsibilities of companies and individuals in enhancing cyber resilience.
- How changes in cyberspace influence geopolitics, and how geopolitical shifts, in turn, affect the digital landscape.

Expect a lively and insightful dialogue that delves into the critical issues shaping our digital future.
