**ATT&CK ID:** M1016  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Vulnerability Scanning

**Description:**
Regularly conduct vulnerability scans on all externally facing systems. Establish and maintain procedures to promptly patch these systems when critical vulnerabilities are identified, either through scanning or public disclosure. This proactive approach helps to minimize the risk of exploitation and ensures that your systems remain secure.
