**ATT&CK ID:** G0064  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT33

APT33 is known for conducting targeted spear phishing campaigns, primarily against U.S. government agencies and private sector companies. In these campaigns, APT33 sends emails containing links to HTML application files, which are embedded with malicious code. [4] [5]

This approach allows the group to gain initial access to their targets' systems, facilitating further malicious activities.
