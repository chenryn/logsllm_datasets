**ATT&CK ID:** S0161  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** XAgentOSX

XAgentOSX is equipped with keylogging functionality that monitors active application windows and logs the keystrokes. It can handle special characters and, by default, buffers up to 50 characters before transmitting them over the command and control (C2) infrastructure.[181]
