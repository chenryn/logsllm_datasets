**ATT&CK ID:** S0154  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Cobalt Strike

Cobalt Strike employs a custom command and control (C2) protocol that can be encapsulated within HTTP, HTTPS, or DNS. Additionally, it supports peer-to-peer communication over Windows named pipes, which is encapsulated within the SMB protocol. All of these protocols utilize their standard assigned ports.
