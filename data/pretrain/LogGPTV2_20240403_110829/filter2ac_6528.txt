**Title: Black Hat Asia 2023 Fireside Chat: Jeff Moss and Gaurav Keerthi**

In this fireside chat, Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss engages in a conversation with Gaurav Keerthi, Former Deputy Chief Executive of the Cyber Security Agency (CSA). The discussion centers on the delicate balance between regulating emerging technologies and fostering innovation in cybersecurity. They explore the roles of both government and the private sector in promoting technological advancement while safeguarding against security threats and addressing privacy concerns. Attend this session to gain insights into whether government technology regulations ultimately protect humanity or stifle innovation.
