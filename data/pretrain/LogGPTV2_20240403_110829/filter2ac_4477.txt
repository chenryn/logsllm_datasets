**ATT&CK ID:** M1047  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Audit

**Description:**
Conduct an audit to identify common User Account Control (UAC) bypass vulnerabilities on Windows systems. This will help you understand the current risk posture and address any identified issues as appropriate. [5]
