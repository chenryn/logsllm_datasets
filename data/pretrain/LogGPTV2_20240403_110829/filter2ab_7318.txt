**ATT&CK ID:** T1606.001  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Web Cookies

Adversaries may forge web cookies to gain unauthorized access to web applications or internet services. Many web applications and services, whether hosted in cloud-based SaaS environments or on-premises servers, rely on session cookies for user authentication and authorization. By forging these cookies, attackers can impersonate legitimate users and potentially bypass security controls.
