**ATT&CK ID:** S0507  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** eSurv

The Android version of eSurv is distributed in a three-stage process:
1. **Dropper:** The initial component that initiates the infection.
2. **Second-Stage Payload:** This follows the dropper and prepares the device for the final payload.
3. **Third-Stage Payload (Exodus):** The final and most sophisticated payload, known as Exodus, is delivered in this stage.
