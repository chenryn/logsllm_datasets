**ATT&CK ID:** M1040  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Behavior Prevention on Endpoint

To enhance endpoint security on Windows 10, enable Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) rules to prevent applications from writing signed, vulnerable drivers to the system. [5] Additionally, on both Windows 10 and Windows 11, enable the Microsoft Vulnerable Driver Blocklist to further harden the system against third-party-developed drivers. [6]
