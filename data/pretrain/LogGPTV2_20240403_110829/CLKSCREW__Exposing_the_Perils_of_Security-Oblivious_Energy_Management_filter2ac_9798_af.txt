#### A.2 FLIPENDIANNESS 实现
```plaintext
算法2: 反转内存缓冲区的字节序
1: procedure FLIPENDIANNESS(src)
2:  dst ← {0}
3:  for i ∈ {0 .. len(src)/4− 1} do
4:      d ← 0
5:      for j ∈ {0 .. 2} do
6:          d ← (src[i*4 + j] | d) << 8
7:      end for
8:      d ← src[i*4 + 3] | d
9:      k ← len(src) − i*4 − 4
10:     dst[k .. k + 3] ← d
11: end for
12: return dst
13: end procedure
```
