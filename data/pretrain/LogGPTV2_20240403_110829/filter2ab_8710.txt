**ATT&CK ID:** S0686  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** QuietSieve

**Description:**
QuietSieve is a technique that captures screenshots every five minutes and saves them to the user's local Application Data folder. The specific directories used for storing these screenshots are `Temp\SymbolSourceSymbols\icons` or `Temp\ModeAuto\icons`.[132]
