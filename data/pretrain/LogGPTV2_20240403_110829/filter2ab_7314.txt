**ATT&CK ID:** S0496  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** REvil

REvil employs a combination of JavaScript, WScript, and PowerShell scripts to execute its malicious activities. The initial infection vector typically involves a malicious JavaScript attachment that contains an obfuscated PowerShell script. When the attachment is executed, the obfuscated PowerShell script is triggered, ultimately leading to the deployment of the REvil ransomware. [5]
