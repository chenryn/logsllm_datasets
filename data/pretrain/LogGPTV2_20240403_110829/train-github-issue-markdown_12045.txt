### Version 0.122.0

1. Enable the "Show Invisibles" option in Atom.
2. Close Atom.
3. Open your terminal and navigate to your working directory.
4. Type `atom --dev` to open Atom in developer mode.
5. Notice that the "Show Invisibles" option is not active.

This sequence of steps should help you verify whether the "Show Invisibles" feature is functioning as expected in developer mode. If it does not work, further troubleshooting may be required.
