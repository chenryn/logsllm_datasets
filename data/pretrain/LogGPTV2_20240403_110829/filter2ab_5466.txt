**ATT&CK ID:** G0016  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT29

APT29, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed using a diverse set of tools in their operations. These tools include Mimikatz for credential dumping, SDelete for secure file deletion, Tor and meek for anonymizing network traffic, and Cobalt Strike as a comprehensive penetration testing and post-exploitation framework.[10][11]
