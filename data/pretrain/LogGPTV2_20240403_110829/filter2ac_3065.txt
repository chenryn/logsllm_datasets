**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed using spearphishing tactics to deliver malicious droppers. These attacks typically involve luring targeted users into executing the attached malware, thereby compromising their systems. This technique has been documented in multiple reports and incidents.[20][21][22][23][24]
