**Title: Black Hat USA 2022: The 8th Annual NOC Report**

The Black Hat NOC (Network Operations Center) team is back with another year of insightful and often surprising statistics. We will be sharing the data that keeps us both intrigued and entertained, year after year. In this report, we will detail the tools and techniques we use to set up, stabilize, and secure the network. We will also discuss the improvements and changes we have implemented over the past year to enhance our operations.

Additionally, we will highlight some of the more humorous and unusual network activities observed during the event. These insights provide a unique perspective on how security professionals behave on an open WiFi network, offering valuable lessons for all attendees.
