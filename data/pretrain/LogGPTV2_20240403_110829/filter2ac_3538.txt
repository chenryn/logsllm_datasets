**ATT&CK ID:** M1050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Exploit Protection

Microsoft's Enhanced Mitigation Experience Toolkit (EMET) includes an Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) feature that can be utilized to block methods of using `rundll32.exe` to bypass application control. This feature helps in mitigating potential security risks by restricting the execution of potentially malicious code.

For more detailed information on how to configure and use ASR rules, refer to Microsoft's official documentation.
