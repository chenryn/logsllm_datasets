**ATT&CK ID:** M1027  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Password Policies

- **Use Strong Passphrases for Private Keys:** Ensure that private keys are protected with strong passphrases to make them resistant to cracking attempts.
- **Avoid Storing Credentials in the Registry:** Do not store passwords or other sensitive credentials within the Windows Registry.
- **Prohibit Password Storage in Files:** Establish and enforce an organizational policy that explicitly prohibits the storage of passwords in files, ensuring that sensitive information is not easily accessible.
