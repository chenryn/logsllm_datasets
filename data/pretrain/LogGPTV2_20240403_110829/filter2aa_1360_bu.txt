### Access Level and Data Breach Services

#### Leaked Source (leakcdsource.ru)
- **Overview**: This service provides a partial view of passwords associated with email addresses.
- **Data Set**: 8 billion credentials, including a "Combo List" which can be acquired later.
- **Password Masking**: The service uses a masking protocol to block portions of each password.
- **Example**:
  - `PI:EMAIL:55Ji*******`
  - `PI:EMAIL:Big6****`

#### Leak Peek (leakpeek.com)
- **Query Options**: Allows searching by email address, username, password, keyword, and domain.
- **Usage**: Useful for verifying the presence of an email in a breach.
- **Example URL**:
  - `https://check.cybernews.com/chk/?lang=en_US&e=PI:EMAIL`

#### We Leak Info (weleakinfo.ro)
- **Background**: A clone of the original site, shut down by federal law enforcement. Requires a free account for access.
- **Benefits**: Provides details about the breach source.
- **Example Results**:
  - `WarFrame.com`: 1 result, hacked on 2016-04-09
  - `NeoPets.com`: 4 results, hacked on 2013-10-08

#### Cybernews (cybernews.com/personal-data-leak-check)
- **Service Overview**: Displays services associated with a target email due to stolen and publicly released data.
- **Premium Services**: Dehashed and Spycloud offer full access to unredacted passwords for a fee.
- **Free Alternatives**: Some sites provide redacted and unredacted passwords without fees.

#### Breach Directory (breachdirectory.org)
- **Example**:
  - `dc3245ecdcf2e40b!40el21a014cc37f70239f41 : Ex71ayer`

#### PSBDMP (psbdmp.ws)
- **Functionality**: Monitors Pastebin for posts containing email addresses and/or passwords.
- **API Usage**: Requires a paid API key for results. Free trial available with Google account.
- **Example Query**:
  - `site:psbdmp.ws "PI:EMAIL"`
  - `https://pastebin.com/WjwCkNL4`

#### IntelligenceX (intelx.io)
- **Search Capabilities**: Free and partial results available, or create a free trial for full access.
- **Limitations**: Limited to the number of burner "trial" email addresses.
- **Example**:
  - `Email Addresses: test@email.com`

#### Avast Hack Check (avast.com/hackcheck)
- **Service Overview**: Identifies email addresses in known data breaches and leaks.
- **Privacy Concerns**: Adds email to marketing campaigns and notifies the account owner.
- **Alternative**: Use "Friends Check" at `https://www.avast.com/hackcheck/friends-check` for verification without notifications.

#### Citoday Leaks (breach.myosint.com)
- **Data Set**: Over 23,000 data breaches, containing hundreds of millions of exposed email addresses and passwords.
- **Uniqueness**: Presents results not found in other services.

#### Scylla (scylla.so)
- **Current Status**: Service is offline but expected to return.
- **Query Format**:
  - `https://scylla.sh/search?q=email:tcst@tesLcom`
  - `https://scylla.sh/search?q=password:passwordl23`
  - `https://scylla.sh/search?q=name:bazzcll`

#### Hunter (hunter.io/email-verifier)
- **Features**: Verifies email addresses and provides links to associated social networks.
- **Example**:
  - `email:PI:EMAIL`

#### OCCRP (data.occrp.org)
- **Description**: Global archive of research material for investigative reporting.
- **Email Search**: Displays documents associated with the email and allows filtering by email address.

#### XLEK (xlek.com)
- **Usage**: Find email addresses using Google search.
- **Example Query**:
  - `site:xlek.com "PI:EMAIL"`

#### Thatâ€™s Them (thatsthem.com)
- **Results**: Includes name, city, and telephone number associated with the email.
- **Paid Options**: Not recommended due to cost.

#### Search People Free (searchpeoplefree.com/cyberbackgroundchecks.com)
- **People Search**: Displays email addresses and related information.
- **Example**:
  - `PI:EMAIL`
  - `PI:EMAIL`

#### Many Contacts (manycontacts.com/cn/mail-check)
- **Social Network Links**: Provides links to LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Foursquare, Instagram, and Flickr accounts.
- **Example**:
  - `email:PI:EMAIL`

#### Search My Bio (searchmy.bio)
- **Instagram Search**: Indexes email addresses in Instagram biographies.
- **Example**:
  - `@gmail.com`

#### Public Email Records (publicemailrecords.com)
- **Data Source**: Believed to be from the River City Media data leak.
- **Results**: Include bill name and address.

#### ProtonMail (protonmail.com)
- **Account Creation Date**: Determine the creation date of a ProtonMail account.
- **URLs**:
  - `https://api.protonmail.ch/pks/lookup?op=get&search=PI:EMAIL`
  - `https://api.protonmail.ch/pks/lookup?op=index&search=PI:EMAIL`
- **Epoch Unix Time Stamp**: Convert the timestamp to a readable date at `https://www.unixtimestamp.com/index.php`.

#### Domain Connections
- **ScamSearch (scamsearch.io)**: Identify email addresses associated with domains.
- **People Data Labs (peopledatalabs.com)**: Enrich personal data using API.
- **Whoxy (whoxy.com/reverse-whois)**: Full name, mailing address, and telephone number.
- **Whoisology (whoisology.com)**: Full name, mailing address, and domain details.
- **AnalyzeID (analyzeid.com)**: Amazon and AdSense affiliate IDs, and owned domain names.

### Conclusion
These services provide various levels of access to breached data and can be used for passive intelligence gathering. Always ensure compliance with legal and ethical guidelines when using these tools.
