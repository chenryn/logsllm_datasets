# G0060: BRONZE BUTLER

## Description
BRONZE BUTLER is a cyber espionage group, likely of Chinese origin, that has been active since at least 2008. The group primarily targets Japanese organizations, with a focus on government, biotechnology, electronics manufacturing, and industrial chemistry sectors. (Citation: Trend Micro Daserf Nov 2017) (Citation: Secureworks BRONZE BUTLER Oct 2017)
