User:  What is containerization technology? What are the application scenarios of containerization technology?
Assistant: Containerization is the packaging of an application and its dependencies in a single container for portability, deployment and management in multiple environments. Commonly used container technologies include Docker and Kubernetes. Containerization technology has many application scenarios, such as: 1. Rapid deployment and upgrade of applications 2. Resource isolation and security 3. Resource management in multi-tenant environments 4. Elastic computing in cloud computing environments 5. Cross-platform operating systems Support 6. Consistency of development and testing environments 7. High performance computing and big data processing 8. Deployment of microservice architecture In short, containerization technology can help enterprises improve the portability, flexibility and scalability of applications, thereby Better meet the needs of different business scenarios.
