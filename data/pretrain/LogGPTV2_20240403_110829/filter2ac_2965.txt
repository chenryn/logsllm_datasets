**ATT&CK ID:** M1017  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** User Training

**Description:**
Holding the Shift key while logging in can prevent applications from opening automatically. This technique is often used to troubleshoot issues or to start the system in a clean state, without the usual startup programs running.
