**ATT&CK ID:** M1035  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Limit Access to Resources Over the Network

- **Restrict Use of Unsecured Protocols:** Ensure that all network communications use encryption and authentication mechanisms. Avoid using protocols that do not provide these security features.
- **Limit Access to Administrative and Management Interfaces:** Restrict access to administrative and management interfaces from untrusted network sources. Only allow trusted, authenticated connections to these critical systems.

By implementing these measures, you can significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access and potential security breaches.
