**ATT&CK ID:** S0690  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Green Lambert

**Description:**
Green Lambert is capable of extracting proxy information from a compromised system by accessing the system's environment variables. This technique allows the malware to gather network configuration details, which can be used for further malicious activities or to bypass certain security measures.
