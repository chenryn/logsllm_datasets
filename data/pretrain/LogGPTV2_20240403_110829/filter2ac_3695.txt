**ATT&CK ID:** S1011  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Tarrask

**Description:**
Tarrask is a technique that involves deleting the Security Descriptor (SD) registry subkey to effectively "hide" scheduled tasks. By removing the SD, the task becomes invisible to standard system tools and management interfaces, making it more difficult to detect and manage.
