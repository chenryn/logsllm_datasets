**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

**Description:**
APT32, a known threat actor, has been observed employing spearphishing tactics. These attacks typically involve sending emails that contain malicious links, designed to compromise the recipient's system or gain unauthorized access to sensitive information. References to this activity can be found in multiple sources.[15][16][17][18][19]
