**ATT&CK ID:** G0026  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT18

APT18, also known as Gadolinium, establishes persistence on a compromised system by modifying the `HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run` registry key. This technique allows the malware to automatically execute every time the user logs in, ensuring continued access and control over the target system. [13][14]
