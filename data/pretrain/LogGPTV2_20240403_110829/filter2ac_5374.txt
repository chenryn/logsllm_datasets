**ATT&CK ID:** S0196  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PUNCHBUGGY

**Description:**
PUNCHBUGGY is a malicious tool capable of downloading additional files and payloads to compromised hosts. This functionality allows attackers to extend their capabilities and maintain persistence on the target system. References for this technique can be found in sources [360] and [361].
