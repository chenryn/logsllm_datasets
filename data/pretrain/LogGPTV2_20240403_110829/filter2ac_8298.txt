**ATT&CK ID:** C0015  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** [C0015 - Additional Tools and Files Downloaded](https://attack.mitre.org/techniques/C0015/)  

**Description:**
During this phase, the threat actors downloaded additional tools and files onto the compromised network. These actions are often aimed at furthering their objectives, such as establishing persistence, exfiltrating data, or conducting other malicious activities.
