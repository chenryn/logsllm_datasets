User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 14.5, Train Workforce Members on Causes of Unintentional Data Exposure?
Assistant: An organization should Train workforce members to be aware of causes for unintentional data exposure. Example topics include mis-delivery of sensitive data, losing a portable end-user device, or publishing data to unintended audiences.CIS Control 14.5 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Protect.
