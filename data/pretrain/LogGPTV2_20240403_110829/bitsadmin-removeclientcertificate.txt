### bitsadmin removeclientcertificate

**Description:**
Removes the client certificate from a specified job.

**Syntax:**
```plaintext
bitsadmin /removeclientcertificate <job>
```

**Parameters:**

- **job**: The display name or GUID of the job from which the client certificate will be removed.

**Examples:**

To remove the client certificate from the job named `myDownloadJob`:
```plaintext
bitsadmin /removeclientcertificate myDownloadJob
```
