**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed employing spearphishing tactics. In these attacks, the group sends emails containing a malicious executable that is disguised as a legitimate document or spreadsheet. This method is designed to trick recipients into opening the attachment, thereby compromising their systems. [25][26][27][28][29][30]
