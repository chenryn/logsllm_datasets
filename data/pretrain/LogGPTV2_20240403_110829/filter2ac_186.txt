**ATT&CK ID:** T1491  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Defacement

Adversaries may alter the visual content displayed within or outside an enterprise network, thereby compromising the integrity of the original material. The primary motivations for defacement include:

- **Delivering a Message:** To communicate specific information or propaganda.
- **Intimidation:** To instill fear or create a sense of vulnerability.
- **Claiming Credit:** To take (potentially false) responsibility for an intrusion.

In some cases, defacement may involve the use of disturbing or offensive imagery to cause discomfort to users or to pressure them into complying with accompanying demands or messages.
