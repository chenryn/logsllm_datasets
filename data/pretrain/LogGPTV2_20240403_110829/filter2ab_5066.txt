**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

**Description:**
APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed using COM scriptlets to download Cobalt Strike beacons. This technique allows the threat actor to execute malicious code and establish a foothold within the target environment.
