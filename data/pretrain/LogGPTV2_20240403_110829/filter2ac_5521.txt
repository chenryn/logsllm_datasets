**ATT&CK ID:** S0697  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** HermeticWiper

**Description:**
HermeticWiper is a malicious tool designed to cause extensive damage to the file system and boot sectors of targeted systems. It has the capability to corrupt disk partitions, destroy the Master Boot Record (MBR), and overwrite the Master File Table (MFT) on all available physical drives. These actions render the affected systems inoperable and result in data loss.
