**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group, a notorious threat actor, employs various malware families that collect and transmit a list of running processes on the victim's system to their command-and-control (C2) server. Additionally, a variant similar to Destover, which is also utilized by the Lazarus Group, gathers information on process execution times. These activities are documented in multiple sources.[152][153][154][155][71][156]
