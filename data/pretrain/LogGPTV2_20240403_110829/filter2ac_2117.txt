**ATT&CK ID:** G0045  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** menuPass

**Description:**
menuPass is known to execute commands through command-line interfaces and reverse shells. The group has employed a modified version of the penetration testing script `wmiexec.vbs` to execute these commands.[211][212][213][214] Additionally, menuPass has used malicious macros embedded in Office documents to execute files.[215][214]
