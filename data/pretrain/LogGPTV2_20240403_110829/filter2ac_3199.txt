**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT32

APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed using the Outlook Credential Dumper tool to extract credentials stored in the Windows registry. This technique allows the group to gain access to sensitive information, such as email account details, which can be used for further malicious activities.
