**ATT&CK ID:** G0102  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Wizard Spider

**Description:**
Wizard Spider has employed spearphishing tactics, distributing malicious attachments to deliver malware. These attachments typically include Microsoft Office documents with embedded macros or PDF files containing malicious links. The ultimate goal is to download and install one of the following malware variants: Emotet, Bokbot, TrickBot, or Bazar.[242][243]
