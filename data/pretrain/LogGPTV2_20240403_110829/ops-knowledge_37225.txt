User: If you are an operation and maintenance expert, please answer the following questions: What is ITSS?
Assistant: ITSS (Information Technology Service Standards, ITSS for short) is a set of systematic and comprehensive information technology service standard library, which comprehensively regulates IT service products and their components, and is used to guide the implementation of standardized and reliable IT services. ITSS mainly includes three categories of technical standards, management standards and service standards, which are instructive, operable and self-certifying. The main goal of ITSS is to provide a set of comprehensive, complete and operable information technology service standards to provide guidance and support for the organization's information construction and IT service management.
