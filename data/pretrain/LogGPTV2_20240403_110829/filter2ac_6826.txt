**ATT&CK ID:** C0014  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Operation Wocao

In Operation Wocao, threat actors employed several tools and techniques to discover and enumerate remote systems. Specifically, they utilized `nbtscan` and `ping` to identify active hosts on the network. Additionally, they executed `dsquery subnet` on a domain controller to retrieve a list of all subnets within the Active Directory environment. [67]
