### Operation Sharpshooter
**Aliases:** Operation Sharpshooter  
**Created:** 2022-09-26T21:18:34.075Z  
**Description:**  
Operation Sharpshooter was a global cyber espionage campaign targeting nuclear, defense, government, energy, and financial companies, with many of the targets located in Germany, Turkey, the United Kingdom, and the United States. Security researchers noted that the campaign shared many similarities with previous operations conducted by the Lazarus Group, including the use of fake job recruitment lures and shared malware code.

### Moses Staff
**Aliases:** Moses Staff  
**Created:** 2022-08-11T22:47:27.686Z  
**Description:**  
Moses Staff is a suspected Iranian threat group that has primarily targeted Israeli companies since at least September 2021. The group openly states their motivation is to cause damage by leaking stolen sensitive data and encrypting the victim's networks without a ransom demand. They are politically motivated and have also targeted government, finance, travel, energy, manufacturing, and utility companies in Italy, India, Germany, Chile, Turkey, the UAE, and the US.

**External References:**
- [MITRE ATT&CK](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G1009)

### C0010
**Aliases:** C0010  
**Created:** 2022-09-21T22:16:42.003Z  
**Description:**  
C0010 is a cyber espionage campaign conducted by UNC3890, targeting Israeli shipping, government, aviation, energy, and healthcare organizations. Security researchers assess that UNC3890 conducts operations in support of Iranian interests, noting limited technical connections to Iran, including PDB strings and Farsi language artifacts. The campaign began by at least late 2020 and was ongoing as of mid-2022.

### Machete
**Aliases:** Machete, APT-C-43, El Machete  
**Created:** 2019-09-13T12:37:10.394Z  
**Description:**  
Machete is a suspected Spanish-speaking cyber espionage group active since at least 2010, primarily focusing on Latin America, with a particular emphasis on Venezuela. ### Nomadic Octopus
**Aliases:** Nomadic Octopus, DustSquad  
**Created:** 2021-08-24T17:04:27.002Z  
**Description:**  
Nomadic Octopus is a Russian-speaking cyber espionage threat group that has primarily targeted Central Asia, including local governments, diplomatic missions, and individuals, since at least 2014. The group conducts campaigns involving Android and Windows malware, mainly using the Delphi programming language, and builds custom variants.
