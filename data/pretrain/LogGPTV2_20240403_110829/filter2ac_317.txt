**ATT&CK ID:** T1005  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Data from Local System

Adversaries may search local system sources, such as file systems, configuration files, and local databases, to identify and collect files of interest and sensitive data before exfiltrating it. This technique involves exploring various storage locations on the compromised system to gather valuable information that can be used for further malicious activities or exfiltrated to an external location.
