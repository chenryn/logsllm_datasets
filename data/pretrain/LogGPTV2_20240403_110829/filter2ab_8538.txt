**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group has been observed compressing exfiltrated data using RAR. Additionally, they have employed the RomeoDelta malware to archive specified directories in .zip format, encrypt the resulting .zip file, and subsequently upload it to a command-and-control (C2) server. [29][30][31]
