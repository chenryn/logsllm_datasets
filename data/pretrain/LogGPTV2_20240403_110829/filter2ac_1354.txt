**ATT&CK ID:** S0449  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Maze

Maze malware is designed to disable various dynamic analysis and security tools, such as IDA Pro debugger, x32dbg, and OllyDbg.[56] Additionally, it has been observed to disable the Real-Time Monitoring feature of Windows Defender and attempts to disable other endpoint protection services.[57]
