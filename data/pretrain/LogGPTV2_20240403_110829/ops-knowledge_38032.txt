User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 6.8, Define and Maintain Role-Based Access Control?
Assistant: An organization should Define and maintain role-based access control, through determining and documenting the access rights necessary for each role within the enterprise to successfully carry out its assigned duties. Perform access control reviews of enterprise assets to validate that all privileges are authorized, on a recurring schedule at a minimum annually, or more frequently.CIS Control 6.8 has the asset type Data, and has the security function Protect.
