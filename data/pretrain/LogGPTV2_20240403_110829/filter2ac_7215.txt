**ATT&CK ID:** S0677  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** AADInternals

**Description:**
AADInternals is a tool that can be utilized to generate SAML tokens by leveraging the token signing certificate from Active Directory Federated Services (AD FS). This capability allows for the creation of seemingly legitimate SAML assertions, which can be used in various malicious activities, such as unauthorized access or identity impersonation.
