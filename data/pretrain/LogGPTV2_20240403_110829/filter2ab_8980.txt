**ATT&CK ID:** S1013  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** ZxxZ

**Description:**
The technique known as ZxxZ involves the creation of a snapshot of running processes by utilizing the `CreateToolhelp32Snapshot` function. This method, referenced in [284], allows for the enumeration and monitoring of active processes on a system.
