**ATT&CK ID:** M1011  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** User Guidance

**Description:**
Insecure or malicious configuration settings are generally not installed without the user's consent. To mitigate risks, users should be advised to avoid installing unexpected configuration settings, such as:

- CA (Certificate Authority) certificates
- iOS Configuration Profiles
- Mobile Device Management (MDM) server provisioning

By following these guidelines, users can help protect their devices from potential security threats.
