# G0017: DragonOK

## Description
DragonOK is a threat group that has been known to target Japanese organizations through phishing emails. Due to overlapping Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs), including the use of similar custom tools, it is believed that DragonOK has either a direct or indirect relationship with another threat group, Moafee. (Citation: Operation Quantum Entanglement) The group is known to employ a variety of malware, such as Sysget/HelloBridge, PlugX, PoisonIvy, FormerFirstRat, NFlog, and NewCT. (Citation: New DragonOK)
