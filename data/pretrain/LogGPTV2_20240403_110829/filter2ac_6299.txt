**ATT&CK ID:** G0068  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PLATINUM

**Description:**
PLATINUM, a threat group, has been known to utilize the Intel® Active Management Technology (AMT) Serial-over-LAN (SOL) channel for command and control (C2) communications. [52]

This method allows the group to maintain persistent and stealthy access to targeted systems, leveraging the built-in management capabilities of AMT.
