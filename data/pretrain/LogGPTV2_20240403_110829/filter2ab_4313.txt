**Title: Black Hat Asia 2023 NOC Report**

The Black Hat NOC team is back with another year of insightful and often surprising statistics. We are excited to share the data that continues to both challenge and amuse us annually. In this report, we will detail the tools and techniques we employ to set up, stabilize, and secure the network. Additionally, we will highlight the changes and improvements we have implemented over the past year to enhance our operations.

We will also delve into some of the more humorous and intriguing network activities, providing a unique perspective on how security professionals behave on an open WiFi network. Join us as we explore these fascinating insights and learn from the experiences of the Black Hat Asia 2023 event.
