**ATT&CK ID:** G0067  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT37

APT37 has exploited vulnerabilities in several software applications to execute malicious code. Specifically, the group has utilized the following exploits:

- **Flash Player:**
  - CVE-2016-4117
  - CVE-2018-4878
- **Microsoft Word:**
  - CVE-2017-0199
- **Internet Explorer:**
  - CVE-2020-1380
  - CVE-2020-26411
- **Microsoft Edge:**
  - CVE-2021-26411

These exploits have been leveraged by APT37 for initial access and execution of their payloads. [18][19][20][21]
