**ATT&CK ID:** S0057  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Tasklist

**Description:**
The `tasklist` command can be utilized to enumerate the processes that are currently running on a system. This information can be valuable for an adversary seeking to understand the environment and identify potential targets or security mechanisms in place. [253]
