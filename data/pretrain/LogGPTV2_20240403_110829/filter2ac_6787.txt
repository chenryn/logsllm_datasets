**ATT&CK ID:** S0086  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** ZLib

**Description:**
ZLib is a compression library that can be used by malware to compress and decompress data. In the context of malicious activities, ZLib has been observed to facilitate the exfiltration of screenshots from compromised systems. [187]
