**ATT&CK ID:** S0044  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** JHUHUGIT

JHUHUGIT is a malicious tool that extracts specific information from the victim's system. It retrieves a build identifier and hard drive details from the Windows registry key `HKLM\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\Disk\Enum`. Additionally, another variant of JHUHUGIT gathers the volume serial number and the name of the storage device.[177][178]
