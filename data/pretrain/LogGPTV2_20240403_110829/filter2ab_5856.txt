**ATT&CK ID:** S0479  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** DEFENSOR ID

**Description:**
DEFENSOR ID can exploit the accessibility service to perform actions on behalf of the user. This includes launching applications specified by the attacker, which can be used to steal data. [6]
