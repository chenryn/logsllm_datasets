User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 18.2, Perform Periodic External Penetration Tests?
Assistant: An organization should Perform periodic external penetration tests based on program requirements, no less than annually. External penetration testing must include enterprise and environmental reconnaissance to detect exploitable information. Penetration testing requires specialized skills and experience and must be conducted through a qualified party. The testing may be clear box or opaque box.CIS Control 18.2 has the asset type Network, and has the security function Identify.
