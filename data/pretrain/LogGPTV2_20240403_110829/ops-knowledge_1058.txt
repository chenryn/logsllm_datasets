User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 4.5, Implement and Manage a Firewall on End-User Devices?
Assistant: An organization should Implement and manage a host-based firewall or port-filtering tool on end-user devices, with a default-deny rule that drops all traffic except those services and ports that are explicitly allowed.CIS Control 4.5 has the asset type Devices, and has the security function Protect.
