### Offline Command

The `offline` command is used to transition an online disk or volume to an offline state.

#### Syntax
- `offline disk`
- `offline volume`

#### Parameters

| Parameter     | Description                                                  |
|---------------|--------------------------------------------------------------|
| `offline disk` | Transitions the currently focused, online disk to the offline state. |
| `offline volume` | Transitions the currently focused, online volume to the offline state. |

This command allows you to manage the availability of disks and volumes by taking them offline, which can be useful for maintenance or other administrative tasks.
