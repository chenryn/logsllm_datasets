**ATT&CK ID:** S0520  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** BLINDINGCAN

**Description:**
BLINDINGCAN has been packaged using the UPX (Ultimate Packer for eXecutables) packer. This technique is often used to compress and obfuscate malware, making it more difficult to detect and analyze by security tools.
