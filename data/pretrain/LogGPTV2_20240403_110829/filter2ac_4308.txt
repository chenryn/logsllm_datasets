**ATT&CK ID:** S0420  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Dvmap

**Description:**
Dvmap is a malicious technique that enables the downloading of code and binaries from a command-and-control (C2) server. Once downloaded, these components can be executed on the device with root-level privileges, allowing for extensive control and potential harm to the system. [11]
