**ATT&CK ID:** M1041  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Encrypt Sensitive Information

**Recommendation:**
Configure SNMPv3 to use the highest level of security, which is `authPriv` (Authentication and Privacy). This ensures that both authentication and encryption are applied, thereby protecting sensitive information from unauthorized access and eavesdropping.
