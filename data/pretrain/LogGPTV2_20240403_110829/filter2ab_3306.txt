**ATT&CK ID:** G0035  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Dragonfly

**Description:**
Dragonfly has exploited a Windows Netlogon vulnerability (CVE-2020-1472) to gain access to Windows Active Directory servers. This vulnerability, also known as "Zerologon," allows an attacker to impersonate any computer on the network and gain domain administrator privileges, enabling them to take control of the entire Active Directory environment.
