**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT28

APT28, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed using the `ntdsutil.exe` utility to export the Active Directory database. This technique is employed to gain access to credentials stored within the database, thereby facilitating further malicious activities.
