**ATT&CK ID:** S0330  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Zeus Panda

**Description:**
Zeus Panda, a variant of the Zeus malware, is known to check for running processes on the victim's machine. This behavior is typically observed as part of its reconnaissance and evasion tactics.[281]
