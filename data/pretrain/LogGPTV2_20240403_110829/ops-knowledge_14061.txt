User: How to set up email signature through Exchange Online?
Assistant: To set up an email signature through Exchange Online, please follow the steps below: 1. Log in to your Exchange Online administrator account. 2. In the console, click Email Flow. 3. Under Email Flow, find the Rules tab and click it. 4. Click Add New Rule. 5. Enter a name for the rule and select the conditions to apply the rule to. 6. Select Add Action, then select Add Email Signature. 7. Create a signature. You can use a template or a custom signature. 8. Commit the rule and activate it. After completing the above steps, Exchange Online will add email signatures for all users in your organization.
