**ATT&CK ID:** S0674  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** CharmPower

**Description:**
CharmPower is a malicious tool that can exfiltrate victim data via FTP. The credentials for the FTP server are hardcoded within the script, facilitating the transfer of stolen information.[12]
