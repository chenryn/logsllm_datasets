**ATT&CK ID:** S1061  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** AbstractEmu

**Description:**
AbstractEmu is a malicious technique that can intercept SMS messages containing two-factor authentication (2FA) codes. This capability allows attackers to bypass 2FA security measures and gain unauthorized access to user accounts.
