**ATT&CK ID:** G0003  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Cleaver

Cleaver is known for developing customized tools and payloads that support a wide range of malicious activities. These include:

- **ARP Poisoning:** Manipulating the Address Resolution Protocol to intercept or redirect network traffic.
- **Encryption:** Implementing encryption to protect their communications and data.
- **Credential Dumping:** Extracting authentication credentials from compromised systems.
- **ASP.NET Shells and Web Backdoors:** Deploying backdoors and shells in web applications, particularly those built on the ASP.NET framework.
- **Process Enumeration:** Listing running processes on a target system to identify potential targets or security mechanisms.
- **WMI Querying:** Using Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) to gather information about the system and its environment.
- **HTTP and SMB Communications:** Establishing and maintaining communication channels over HTTP and Server Message Block (SMB) protocols.
- **Network Interface Sniffing:** Capturing and analyzing network traffic to extract sensitive information.
- **Keystroke Logging:** Recording keystrokes to capture passwords, personal information, and other valuable data.

These capabilities highlight Cleaver's sophisticated approach to cyber espionage and targeted attacks. [11]
