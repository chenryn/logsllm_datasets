**ATT&CK ID:** S0402  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** OSX/Shlayer

**Description:**
OSX/Shlayer is known to gather specific system information, including the `IOPlatformUUID`, the session `UID`, and the operating system version. This information is obtained by executing the command `sw_vers -productVersion`.
