**ATT&CK ID:** G0121  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Sidewinder

**Description:**
Sidewinder has been observed sending emails with malicious attachments that, when opened, redirect victims to credential harvesting websites. These tactics are designed to steal sensitive information from the targeted individuals.
