**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group's malware, known as "IndiaIndia," collects and transmits information about the configuration of the first network interface card to its command-and-control (C2) server. This information includes the IP address, gateways, subnet mask, DHCP settings, and the availability of WINS.[127][128]
