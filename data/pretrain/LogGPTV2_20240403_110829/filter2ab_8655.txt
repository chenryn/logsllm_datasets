**ATT&CK ID:** S0125  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Remsec

**Description:**
Remsec is a sophisticated malware that includes a network loader designed to receive and execute modules from remote attackers on the local victim's system. Additionally, it is capable of uploading and downloading files over both HTTP and HTTPS protocols. This functionality allows the malware to maintain persistence and exfiltrate data from the compromised system.
