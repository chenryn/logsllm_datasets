User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 17.4, Establish and Maintain an Incident Response Process?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and maintain an incident response process that addresses roles and responsibilities, compliance requirements, and a communication plan. Review annually, or when significant enterprise changes occur that could impact this Safeguard.CIS Control 17.4 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Respond.
