**ATT&CK ID:** S0515  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** WellMail

**Description:**
WellMail is a technique used to exfiltrate files from a compromised system. This method allows attackers to transfer sensitive data from the victim's machine to an external location, facilitating unauthorized access and potential data breaches. [191]
