**ATT&CK ID:** M1042  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Disable or Remove Feature or Program

**Description:**
If a remote service, such as SSH, RDP, or any other similar service, is not required for the operation of your system, it should be disabled or removed. This action helps to reduce the attack surface and minimize the risk of unauthorized access.

**Recommendation:**
- **Identify Unnecessary Services:** Review the services running on your system and identify those that are not essential.
- **Disable or Remove:** For each unnecessary service, follow the appropriate procedures to disable or remove it. Ensure that this process is documented and tested to avoid disrupting critical operations.

By taking these steps, you can enhance the security posture of your system and protect it from potential threats.
