**ATT&CK ID:** C0017  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Exfiltration Over DNS

In the context of C0017, APT41 exfiltrated victim data by encoding and prepending it as subdomains to a domain controlled by the attackers. This technique leverages DNS lookups to covertly transmit the data. [9]
