**ATT&CK ID:** S0584  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** AppleJeus

AppleJeus employs XOR encryption to obfuscate collected system information before transmitting it to a command and control (C2) server. Additionally, the malware utilizes the open-source ADVObfuscation library to further obfuscate its components. [15]
