**ATT&CK ID:** C0022  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Operation Dream Job

During Operation Dream Job, the Lazarus Group deployed malware that was specifically designed to avoid execution on systems with Windows language preferences set to Korean, Japanese, or Chinese. [18]
