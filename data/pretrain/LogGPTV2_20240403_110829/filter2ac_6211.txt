**ATT&CK ID:** M1042  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Disable or Remove Feature or Program

**Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Disable Autorun if Unnecessary:** If the autorun feature is not required for your operations, it should be disabled to prevent potential security risks.
2. **Restrict Removable Media at an Organizational Policy Level:** Implement organizational policies that disallow or restrict the use of removable media, such as USB drives, if they are not essential for business operations.

By implementing these measures, you can reduce the risk of unauthorized access and potential security breaches.
