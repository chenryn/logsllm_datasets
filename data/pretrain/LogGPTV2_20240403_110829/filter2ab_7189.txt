**ATT&CK ID:** M1006  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Use Recent OS Version

**Description:**
Starting with Android 7, several changes were introduced to make the abuse of device administrator permissions more difficult. These enhancements aim to improve security and reduce the risk of unauthorized access and control over the device.
