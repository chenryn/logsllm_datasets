Recent years have seen rapid development in cellular networks, both in increas-

demands. This growth has made the quality of network management an even
greater challenge and puts limits on the analysis methods that can be applied.

In our proposed alarm RCA system, we create an influence graph to model

view the problem as an influence maximization problem [10], i.e., we want to
discover the alarms with the largest influence. When a failure transpires, our
system can automatically perform RCA based on the sub-graph containing the
involved alarms and output the top-K most probable root cause alarms.
