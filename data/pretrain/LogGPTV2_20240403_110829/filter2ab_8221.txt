**ATT&CK ID:** S0125  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Remsec

**Description:**
Remsec is a sophisticated malware capable of enumerating the contents of folders on a victim's system. Additionally, it searches for custom network encryption software installed on the compromised machine. This functionality allows the attackers to gather detailed information about the target environment and identify potential security measures in place.
