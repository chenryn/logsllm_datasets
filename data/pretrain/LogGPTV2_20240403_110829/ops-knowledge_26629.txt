User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 14.8, Train Workforce on the Dangers of Connecting to and Transmitting Enterprise Data Over Insecure Networks?
Assistant: An organization should Train workforce members on the dangers of connecting to, and transmitting data over, insecure networks for enterprise activities. If the enterprise has remote workers, training must include guidance to ensure that all users securely configure their home network infrastructure.CIS Control 14.8 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Protect.
