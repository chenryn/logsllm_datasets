User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 17.5, Assign Key Roles and Responsibilities?
Assistant: An organization should Assign key roles and responsibilities for incident response, including staff from legal, IT, information security, facilities, public relations, human resources, incident responders, and analysts, as applicable. Review annually, or when significant enterprise changes occur that could impact this Safeguard.CIS Control 17.5 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Respond.
