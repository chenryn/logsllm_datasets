ATT&CK ID:G0092
ATT&CK Technique Name:TA505
TA505 has used lures to get users to enable content in malicious attachments and execute malicious files contained in archives. For example, TA505 makes their malware look like legitimate Microsoft Word documents, .pdf and/or .lnk files. [226][227][228][229][230][231][232][233][234]
