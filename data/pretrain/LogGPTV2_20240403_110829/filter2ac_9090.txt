**ATT&CK ID:** G0092  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** TA505

**Description:**

TA505 is a threat actor known for employing various lures to deceive users into enabling content in malicious attachments and executing malicious files contained within archives. The group often disguises their malware as legitimate Microsoft Word documents, PDFs, and/or .lnk (shortcut) files. This tactic increases the likelihood that unsuspecting users will open and interact with the malicious content, thereby initiating the infection process.
