**ATT&CK ID:** G0034  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Sandworm Team

The Sandworm Team has employed the Telegram Bot API, a feature of the Telegram Messenger, to send and receive commands to its Python-based backdoor. Additionally, they have leveraged legitimate M.E.Doc software update check requests to transmit and receive commands, and have hosted malicious payloads on the domain putdrive.com.[45][46]
