**ATT&CK ID:** S0673  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** DarkWatchman

**Description:**
DarkWatchman is capable of performing several malicious activities, including the uninstallation of malicious components from the Windows Registry, stopping specific processes, and clearing the browser history.[4]
