**ATT&CK ID:** G0010  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Turla

Turla, a well-known threat actor, has employed various obfuscation techniques to conceal its PowerShell commands and payloads. These methods include the use of encryption, such as salted 3DES via PowerSploit's `Out-EncryptedScript.ps1` script, the generation of random variable names, and base64 encoding.[85]
