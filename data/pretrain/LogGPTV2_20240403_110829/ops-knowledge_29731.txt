User: How to set up a VPN connection?
Assistant: Here are the steps to set up a VPN connection: 1. Open the "Settings" menu and go to the "Network & Internet" option. 2. Click the "VPN" tab. 3. Click "Add VPN Connection". 4. Select the VPN service provider you want to use in the "VPN Provider" field. 5. Fill in the necessary information such as "Connection Name", "Server Name or Address", "VPN Type", "User Name" and "Password". 6. Check the "Remember my username and password" option so that you don't need to re-enter it next time you connect. 7. Click the "Save" button to save the settings. 8. Tap the "Connect" button to connect to the VPN service. Note that if you are using a Windows operating system, you may need administrator privileges to set up a VPN connection. Additionally, a VPN service provider may offer to use a dedicated app to make it easier for you to set up your connection.
