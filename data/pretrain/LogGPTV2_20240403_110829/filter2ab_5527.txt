**ATT&CK ID:** G0016  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT29

**Description:**
APT29, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed using the legitimate mailing service Constant Contact to distribute phishing emails. This tactic allows the group to bypass some email security measures and increase the likelihood of their malicious messages reaching their intended targets.
