**ATT&CK ID:** S0190  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** BITSAdmin

**Description:**
BITSAdmin is a command-line utility that can be used to create and manage Background Intelligent Transfer Service (BITS) jobs. These jobs enable the upload and/or download of files in the background, even when the user is not logged in. This functionality can be leveraged by attackers to transfer malicious files or exfiltrate data from a compromised system.
