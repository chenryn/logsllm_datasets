# G0033: Poseidon Group
## Description
The [Poseidon Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0033) is a Portuguese-speaking threat group that has been active since at least 2005. This group is known for using exfiltrated information from victims to extort them, often blackmailing companies into hiring the [Poseidon Group](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0033) as a security firm. (Citation: Kaspersky Poseidon Group)
