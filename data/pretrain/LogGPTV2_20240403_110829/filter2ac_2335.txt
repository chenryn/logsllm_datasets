**Title: Botconf 2016 - MISP: The Threat Sharing Platform - A Developer's Perspective on Extensions and Collaboration**

MISP is rapidly becoming a cornerstone open-source tool for indicator and threat sharing within the information security community. Recent versions of MISP have significantly enhanced its modularity, offering a variety of ways to utilize and extend the platform. This workshop is designed to guide developers and contributors in mastering the MISP platform and customizing it to meet their specific needs. Additionally, the session aims to gather valuable feedback from current MISP users to further refine and improve the software.
