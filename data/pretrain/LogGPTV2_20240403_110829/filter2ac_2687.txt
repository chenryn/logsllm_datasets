**ATT&CK ID:** S0545  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** TERRACOTTA

**Description:**
TERRACOTTA verifies whether its call stack has been altered, which is a common indicator of an analysis environment. If the call stack has been modified, TERRACOTTA refrains from decrypting its obfuscated strings, thereby evading detection and analysis.
