**ATT&CK ID:** M1049  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Antivirus/Antimalware

**Recommendations:**
- Implement heuristic-based malware detection to identify and mitigate unknown or evolving threats.
- Regularly update virus definitions to ensure the antivirus/antimalware solution can detect the latest known threats.
- Develop and deploy custom signatures based on observed malware to enhance the detection and prevention capabilities of your security systems.
