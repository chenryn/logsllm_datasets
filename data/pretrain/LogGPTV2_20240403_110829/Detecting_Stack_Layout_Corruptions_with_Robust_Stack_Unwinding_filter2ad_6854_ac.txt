reason is the pthread library; large programs using multiple threads get additional
type A errors due to this library.
While most of programs triggered non-trivial number of type A failures, in
all cases, no violation of stack invariants is detected causing zero false positives
of our approach.

marking suites. The Nbench and SPEC 2006 benchmarks are executed using
the standard setting. Performance numbers from diﬀerent types of benchmarks
