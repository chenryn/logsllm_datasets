the core of VMware security best practices. Started more than a decade ago as the VMware vSphere Security Hardening
Guide, it has long served as guidance for vsphere Administrators looking to protect their infrastructure.

SCG's recommendations are likely to be safe to implement, but others may interfere with operations of those solutions.
