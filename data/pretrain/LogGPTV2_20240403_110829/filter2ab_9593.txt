**ATT&CK ID:** S0194  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PowerSploit

PowerSploit is a comprehensive suite of PowerShell modules designed for penetration testing and red team operations. Among its features, PowerSploit includes a set of Privesc-PowerUp modules that can be used to discover and manipulate service binaries, paths, and configuration files. These capabilities enable attackers to escalate privileges by modifying or replacing critical system components. [95][96]
