**Title: Black Hat Europe 2021 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways**

Join members of the Black Hat Europe Review Board for an insightful discussion on the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community. In this Locknote, the panel will candidly explore the key takeaways from the conference and analyze how these emerging trends will shape future InfoSec strategies.

This session will provide a comprehensive overview of the critical insights and actionable recommendations that emerged from Black Hat Europe 2021, ensuring that attendees are well-prepared to address the evolving challenges in the field of cybersecurity.
