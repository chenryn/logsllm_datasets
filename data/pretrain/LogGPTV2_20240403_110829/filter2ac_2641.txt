**ATT&CK ID:** M1028  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Operating System Configuration

To enhance system security, follow the vendor's device hardening best practices. This includes:

- Disabling unnecessary and unused features and services.
- Avoiding the use of default configurations and passwords.
- Implementing logging and auditing to facilitate detection and monitoring.

By adhering to these guidelines, you can significantly reduce the attack surface and improve the overall security posture of your operating system.
