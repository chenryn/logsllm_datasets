**ATT&CK ID:** C0024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SolarWinds Compromise

In the SolarWinds compromise, APT29 (also known as Nobelium) gained unauthorized access to target systems by exploiting compromised accounts at cloud solution partners. Additionally, the threat actor leveraged compromised digital certificates issued by Mimecast to authenticate to Mimecast customer systems, thereby extending their reach and maintaining persistence.[14][15]
