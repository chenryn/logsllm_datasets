**ATT&CK ID:** S0501  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PipeMon

**Description:**
The initial execution of PipeMon is triggered by a call to the `CreateProcess` function, with the decryption password passed as an argument. Subsequently, PipeMon employs the `LoadLibrary` function to load its installer.[134]
