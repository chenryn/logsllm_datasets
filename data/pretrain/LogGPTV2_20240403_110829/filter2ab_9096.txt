**ATT&CK ID:** S0154  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Cobalt Strike

**Description:**
Cobalt Strike is capable of spawning processes with alternate parent process IDs (PPIDs). This technique can be used to evade detection by making the spawned processes appear as if they are children of legitimate system processes, rather than those associated with malicious activity.
