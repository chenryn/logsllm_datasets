# Contribution Guidelines

We appreciate your interest in contributing to our project. To ensure a smooth and efficient process, please adhere to the following guidelines when submitting a pull request:

1. **Compliance with the Awesome Manifesto**: Before you begin, familiarize yourself with [the awesome manifesto](https://github.com/sindresorhus/awesome/blob/master/awesome.md) and make sure your list aligns with its principles.

2. **Check for Duplicates**: Thoroughly search through existing suggestions to avoid creating duplicates. Your contribution should add new and valuable content.

3. **Quality and Usefulness**: Ensure that your list is useful and contains sufficient content. Each item should have a clear and concise description, providing value to the reader.

4. **Backlinking**: Include a link back to this list from yours. This helps users discover more related lists and enhances the overall value of the project.

5. **Individual Pull Requests**: Submit an individual pull request for each suggestion. This makes it easier to review and merge your contributions.

6. **Title Capitalization**: Titles should follow standard capitalization rules. Refer to [this guide](http://grammar.yourdictionary.com/capitalization/rules-for-capitalization-in-titles.html) for proper formatting.

7. **Formatting**: Use the following format for your entries: `[List Name](link)`.

8. **Categorization**: Add new links to the bottom of the relevant category. If you believe a new category is necessary or if you have suggestions for improving the current categorization, feel free to include them.

9. **Spelling and Grammar**: Double-check your submission for spelling and grammatical errors to maintain the high quality of the project.

10. **Trailing Whitespace**: Ensure your text editor is configured to remove trailing whitespace.

11. **Descriptive Titles**: Both the pull request and the commit should have a clear and descriptive title that summarizes the changes made.

Thank you for your contributions!
