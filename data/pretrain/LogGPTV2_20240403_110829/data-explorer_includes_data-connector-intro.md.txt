Azure Data Explorer is a high-performance, highly scalable service designed for the exploration and analysis of log and telemetry data. It supports efficient data ingestion from a variety of sources, including Event Hubs, IoT Hubs, and blob storage containers. This capability ensures that data can be loaded into the system in near real-time, enabling timely and insightful analysis.
