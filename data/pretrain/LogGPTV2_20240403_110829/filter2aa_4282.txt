# MITRE CTF - Cyber Challenge 2019

This document provides writeups for various challenges from the 2019 MITRE CTF competition, which can be found at [MITRE STEM CTF](https://mitrestemctf.org/).

**Note:** The challenges were taken offline immediately after the CTF concluded. As a result, these writeups are based on the data that was available offline and may lack some of the finer details.
