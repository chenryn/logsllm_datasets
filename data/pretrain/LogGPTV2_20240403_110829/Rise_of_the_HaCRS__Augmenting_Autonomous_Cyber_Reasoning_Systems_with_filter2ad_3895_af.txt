pertise, along with justifications for their classification. These binaries were filtered for receiving mostly printable input, but some of them
(marked with *) decoded that into raw binary input, making them suboptimal for human interaction. Others (marked with +) received their
inputs in protocols which were automatically translated by HaCRS’ interaction assistance layer to be easily human-interactive. We expect
humans to do best on binaries with a high semantic complexity, and unskilled humans to do best with binaries requiring a low technical
expertise.
