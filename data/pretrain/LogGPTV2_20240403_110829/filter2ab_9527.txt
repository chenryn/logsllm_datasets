**ATT&CK ID:** G1006  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Earth Lusca

Earth Lusca has been observed sending spear-phishing emails that contain malicious links. When the recipient clicks on these links, they are directed to a decoy document. Opening this document triggers the execution of a malicious loader. [27]
