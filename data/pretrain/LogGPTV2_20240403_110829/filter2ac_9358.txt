**ATT&CK ID:** S0689  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** WhisperGate

**Description:**
WhisperGate is a malicious technique that overwrites specific sectors on the hard drive of a victim's host at periodic intervals. This method can cause significant damage to the system and render it inoperable.
