**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT28

APT28, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed using the `WindowStyle` parameter to hide PowerShell windows. This technique allows the group to execute commands without the user being aware of the activity, thereby evading detection. [5] [6]
