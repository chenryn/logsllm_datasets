**ATT&CK ID:** G0094  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Kimsuky

Kimsuky, a known threat actor, has utilized Visual Basic to download malicious payloads.[73][74][75][76] Additionally, the group has employed malicious VBA macros embedded in maldocs, which are often disguised as forms. These macros are triggered when a victim enters any content into the form, thereby initiating the malicious activity.[76]
