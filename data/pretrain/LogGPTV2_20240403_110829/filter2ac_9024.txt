ATT&CK ID:S1045
ATT&CK Technique Name:INCONTROLLER
INCONTROLLER can perform a UDP multicast scan of UDP port 27127 to identify Schneider PLCs that use that port for the NetManage protocol.[3][4]INCONTROLLER can use the FINS (Factory Interface Network Service) protocol to scan for and obtain MAC address associated with Omron devices.[5][4]INCONTROLLER has the ability to perform scans for TCP port 4840 to identify devices running OPC UA servers.[4]
