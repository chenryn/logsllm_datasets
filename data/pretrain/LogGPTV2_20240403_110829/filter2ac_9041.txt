**ATT&CK ID:** S0454  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Cadelspy

**Description:**
Cadelspy is a malware that has the capability to log keystrokes on a compromised host. This feature allows it to capture sensitive information, such as passwords and personal data, entered by the user.
