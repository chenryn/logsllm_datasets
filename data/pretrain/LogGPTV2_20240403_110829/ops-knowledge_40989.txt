User: According to the following description, analyze the possible network topology and give a reasonable solution. An enterprise has multiple office locations, which need to be connected to each other, access the Internet, and ensure security. Telephony, video, and data transmission is required between office locations.
Assistant: Possible network topologies are star, bus, ring and mesh. It is recommended to use a mesh topology to connect various office locations, and each office location is directly connected to other office locations to form a network. On this basis, set a central router as the core of the entire network, converge the traffic of all office locations to this router, and access the Internet. Use virtual private network (VPN) technology for security protection, realize encrypted transmission of data, and ensure data confidentiality. At the same time, IP telephone and video conferencing systems can be used to realize simultaneous transmission of telephone, video and data.
