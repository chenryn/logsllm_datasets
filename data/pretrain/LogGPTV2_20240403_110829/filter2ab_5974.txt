**ATT&CK ID:** G0080  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Cobalt Group

The Cobalt Group, a sophisticated threat actor, has been known to employ a diverse set of tools in their operations. These include Mimikatz, PsExec, Cobalt Strike, and SDelete.[37] These tools are used for various purposes, such as credential theft, remote execution, and data manipulation, enabling the group to carry out complex and targeted cyber attacks.
