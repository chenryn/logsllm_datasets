**ATT&CK ID:** M1026  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Privileged Account Management

To enhance security, avoid adding user or administrative domain accounts to local administrator groups across multiple systems unless they are strictly controlled. This practice is often equivalent to having a local administrator account with the same password on all systems, which can significantly increase the risk of unauthorized access and lateral movement.

Adhere to best practices for the design and administration of an enterprise network to minimize the use of privileged accounts across different administrative tiers. Implementing these measures will help to reduce the potential attack surface and mitigate the risk of privilege escalation.
