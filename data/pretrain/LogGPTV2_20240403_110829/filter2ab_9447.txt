**ATT&CK ID:** M1024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Restrict Registry Permissions

**Recommendation:**
To enhance security and prevent unauthorized modifications to critical server parameters in the Windows Registry, it is recommended to use Group Policy. Specifically, configure Group Policy to restrict changes to service-related and other essential registry settings. This measure will help mitigate potential risks associated with unauthorized alterations.
