**ATT&CK ID:** G0102  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Wizard Spider

**Description:**
Wizard Spider, a notorious cybercriminal group, has employed spearphishing tactics to distribute malware. Specifically, they have lured victims into opening email attachments that contain malicious macros. These macros, when executed, download and install one of several types of malware, including Emotet, Bokbot, TrickBot, or Bazar.[255][256]
