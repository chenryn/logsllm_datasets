**ATT&CK ID:** G0045  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** menuPass

**Description:**
menuPass, also known as APT19, is a threat group that stages data for exfiltration by compressing it into multi-part archives. These archives are often saved in the Recycle Bin to evade detection and facilitate later exfiltration.[57]
