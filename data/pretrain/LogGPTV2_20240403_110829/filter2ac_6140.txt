**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**Technique Name:** APT32

**Description:**
APT32, also known as OceanLotus, has been observed using scheduled tasks as a method to maintain persistence on compromised systems. This technique allows the threat actor to execute malicious code at specific intervals, ensuring continued access and control over the victim's environment. References to this behavior can be found in multiple sources [11][12][13][14].
