**ATT&CK ID:** G0059  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Magic Hound

The Magic Hound malware has been observed using a PowerShell command to determine the victim system's architecture, specifically checking if it is an x64 machine. Additionally, other variants of this malware have been known to gather information such as the operating system version, UUID, and the computer or host name, which is then transmitted to the command-and-control (C2) server.[218][219][220]
