**ATT&CK ID:** S0538  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Crutch

**Description:**
Crutch is a tool that continuously monitors removable drives and automatically copies files of interest to the attacker. [11]

This technique allows adversaries to exfiltrate sensitive data from removable media, such as USB drives, by automatically detecting when such devices are connected and copying specified files to a predetermined location.
