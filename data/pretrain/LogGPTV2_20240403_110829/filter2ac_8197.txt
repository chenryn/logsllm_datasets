**Title: Black Hat Europe 2018 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways**

As the conference draws to a close, we invite you to join Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss and distinguished members of the Black Hat Review Board for an insightful and candid discussion. This Locknote session will delve into the most pressing issues currently facing the information security (InfoSec) community. Our panelists will share key takeaways from Black Hat Europe 2018, providing valuable insights into emerging trends and their potential impact on future InfoSec strategies.

Don't miss this opportunity to gain a deeper understanding of the critical challenges and opportunities that lie ahead in the field of cybersecurity.
