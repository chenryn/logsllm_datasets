**ATT&CK ID:** S0310  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** ANDROIDOS_ANSERVER.A

**Description:**
ANDROIDOS_ANSERVER.A employs encrypted content embedded within a blog site as part of its command and control (C2) infrastructure. Specifically, the encrypted content contains URLs that direct the malware to additional servers, which are used for further C2 activities. [1]
