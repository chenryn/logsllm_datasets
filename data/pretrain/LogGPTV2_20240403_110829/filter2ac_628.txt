**ATT&CK ID:** G0021  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Molerats

**Description:**
Molerats, a known threat actor, has been observed using executables to download malicious files from various sources. [297][298]

This approach allows the group to distribute and execute their payloads more effectively, often evading detection by security systems. The use of multiple sources for downloading these files also helps in maintaining operational resilience and redundancy.
