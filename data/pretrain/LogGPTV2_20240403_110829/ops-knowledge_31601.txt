User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 3.7, Establish and Maintain a Data Classification Scheme?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and maintain an overall data classification scheme for the enterprise. Enterprises may use labels, such as “Sensitive,” “Confidential,” and “Public,” and classify their data according to those labels. Review and update the classification scheme annually, or when significant enterprise changes occur that could impact this Safeguard.CIS Control 3.7 has the asset type Data, and has the security function Identify.
