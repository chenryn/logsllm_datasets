Substitution Cipher – Puzzle
1.  S + M = MO, so M = 1
3.  S + 1 = 10, so S = 9
4.  N + R = 1E, E + 1 = N, so R = 8
5.  Since E+1 = N, and N + 8 > 11,
E can be 3, 4, or 5.
6.  If E=3 or 4, reach dead end; thus 
E=5
7.  If E=5, then N=6
8.  Only remaining values are D=7, Y=2 
2.  S + 1 = 1O, so O = 0
