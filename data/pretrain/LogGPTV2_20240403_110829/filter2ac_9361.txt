**ATT&CK ID:** S0493  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** GoldenSpy

**Description:**
GoldenSpy utilizes specific network ports for various types of traffic. It employs HTTP over ports 9005 and 9006 for general network communication, port 9002 for command and control (C2) requests, port 33666 as a WebSocket, and port 8090 for file downloads. [15]
