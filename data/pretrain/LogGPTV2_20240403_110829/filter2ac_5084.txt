**ATT&CK ID:** G0006  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT1

APT1 employs two utilities, GETMAIL and MAPIGET, to exfiltrate email data. Specifically, MAPIGET is used to steal emails that are still stored on Exchange servers but have not yet been archived.
