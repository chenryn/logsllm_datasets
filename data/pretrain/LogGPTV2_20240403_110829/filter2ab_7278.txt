**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**Technique Name:** APT28

APT28, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed engaging in the practice of clearing event logs to evade detection. Specifically, they have used the following commands to clear system and security event logs:

- `wevtutil cl System`
- `wevtutil cl Security`

These actions are part of their broader strategy to maintain stealth and persistence within targeted networks.
