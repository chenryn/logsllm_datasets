**ATT&CK ID:** S0036  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** FLASHFLOOD

**Description:**
FLASHFLOOD is a technique used to search for files with specific or customizable file extensions on both the local system and removable media. This method allows attackers to identify and potentially exfiltrate valuable data from the compromised environment.
