**ATT&CK ID:** S0697  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** HermeticWiper

**Description:**
HermeticWiper is capable of disabling crash dumps by setting the `HKLM:\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\CrashControl\CrashDumpEnabled` Registry key to 0. This action prevents the system from generating memory dump files during a crash, which can hinder forensic analysis and incident response efforts.
