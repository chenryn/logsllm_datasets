**ATT&CK ID:** S0357  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Impacket

The SecretsDump and Mimikatz modules within Impacket can be used to perform credential dumping, enabling attackers to obtain account and password information. [13]

In this version, the text is more structured and clearly explains the function of the specified modules. The use of "enabling attackers" provides context for the action, making it more explicit and professional.
