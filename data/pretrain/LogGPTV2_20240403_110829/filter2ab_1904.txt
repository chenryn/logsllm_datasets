**ATT&CK ID:** G0035  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Dragonfly

**Description:**
Dragonfly has created accounts that are disguised as legitimate backup, service, and email administration accounts. These deceptive accounts are designed to blend in with the target environment, making them more difficult to detect and investigate. [13]
