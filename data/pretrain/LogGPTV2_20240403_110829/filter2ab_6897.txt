**ATT&CK ID:** M1030  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Segmentation

To effectively implement network segmentation, it is essential to adhere to best practices for configuring network firewalls. This includes allowing only the necessary ports and traffic to enter and exit the network, thereby minimizing the attack surface and enhancing overall security. [10]
