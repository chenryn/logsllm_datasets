**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group employs timestomping techniques in several of its malware families. This includes:
- Modifying the last write timestamp of a specified Registry key to a random date.
- Copying the timestamp from legitimate .exe files (such as `calc.exe` or `mspait.exe`) to the dropped files.
