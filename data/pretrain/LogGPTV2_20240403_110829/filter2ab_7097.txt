**ATT&CK ID:** S0337  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** BadPatch

**Description:**
BadPatch leverages Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) to enumerate and identify installed security products within the victim's environment. This technique enables attackers to gather information about the security measures in place, which can be used to tailor their attack strategies accordingly.
