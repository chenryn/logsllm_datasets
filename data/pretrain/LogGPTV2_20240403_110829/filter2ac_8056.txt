**ATT&CK ID:** G0045  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** menuPass

**Description:**
menuPass has exploited valid accounts, including those shared between Managed Service Providers (MSPs) and their clients, to move laterally between the two environments. This technique has been documented in multiple sources.[44][45][46][47]
