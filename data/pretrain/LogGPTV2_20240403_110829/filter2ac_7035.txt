**ATT&CK ID: G0080**
**ATT&CK Technique Name: Cobalt Group**

The Cobalt Group has been known to utilize public platforms, such as GitHub and SendSpace, for uploading files, which are subsequently downloaded onto the victim's computers. [114][1] Additionally, the group's JavaScript backdoor is capable of downloading files. [115]
