**ATT&CK ID:** S0053  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SeaDuke

**Description:**
SeaDuke can achieve persistence by placing a `.lnk` file in the Startup directory. This method ensures that the malware is executed every time the system starts, thereby maintaining its presence on the infected machine.
