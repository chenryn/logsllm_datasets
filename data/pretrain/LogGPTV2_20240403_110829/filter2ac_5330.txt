**ATT&CK ID:** M1032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Multi-Factor Authentication

**Recommendation:**
Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) for both user and privileged accounts. Many embedded network devices support TACACS+ and RADIUS protocols for MFA. Adhere to the vendor's best practices for strengthening access control measures. [11]
