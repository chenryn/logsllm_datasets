### Human Subjects and Ethical Considerations

For the analysis of cryptojacking usage in the wild, this paper uses NetFlow statistics from a Tier 1 network operator. This data access was approved by the institutional review board. The research team did not have direct access to the NetFlow data containing source and destination IP addresses, which are considered personally identifiable information. Instead, the team provided a list of IP addresses of cryptomining proxies and mining pools to the data owner, who then provided the corresponding flow records with the connection’s source IP protected by a salted hash.
