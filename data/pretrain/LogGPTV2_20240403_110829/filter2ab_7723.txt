**ATT&CK ID:** G0046  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** FIN7

FIN7, a well-known threat group, has been observed creating scheduled tasks as a method to establish persistence on compromised systems. This technique allows the malware to execute at regular intervals or during system startup, ensuring continued access and control. References for this behavior can be found in sources [59], [60], [61], and [62].
