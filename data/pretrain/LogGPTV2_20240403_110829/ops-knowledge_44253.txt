User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 5.5, Establish and Maintain an Inventory of Service Accounts?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and maintain an inventory of service accounts. The inventory, at a minimum, must contain department owner, review date, and purpose. Perform service account reviews to validate that all active accounts are authorized, on a recurring schedule at a minimum quarterly, or more frequently.CIS Control 5.5 has the asset type Users, and has the security function Identify.
