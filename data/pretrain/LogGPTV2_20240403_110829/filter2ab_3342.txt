**ATT&CK ID:** M1018  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** User Account Management

**Recommendations:**
- **Regular Review and Cleanup:** Conduct periodic reviews of user accounts to identify and remove those that are inactive or unnecessary. This helps to minimize the potential attack surface.
- **Restrict Account Creation:** Limit the ability for users to create additional accounts, ensuring that only authorized personnel can perform this action. This reduces the risk of unauthorized account proliferation.
