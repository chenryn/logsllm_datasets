**ATT&CK ID:** G0022  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT3

APT3, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, employs a downloader that utilizes the Windows command `cmd.exe /C whoami` to gather information about the current user. Additionally, this group leverages a tool to execute commands on remote computers, enhancing their capability to control and manipulate target systems.[19][20]
