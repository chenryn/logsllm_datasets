**ATT&CK ID:** S0650  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** QakBot

**Description:**
QakBot is a sophisticated malware that possesses the capability to enumerate windows on a compromised host. This functionality allows it to gather information about active windows, which can be used for further malicious activities.
