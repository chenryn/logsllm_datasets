> On x86 architecture, this mode is generally referred to as Ring -2.
>
> **Answer: System Management Mode (SMM)**

This question refers to the System Management Mode (SMM), a special-purpose operating mode in x86 processors. SMM is often called "Ring -2" because it operates at a privilege level below the standard Ring 0, which is typically reserved for the operating system kernel.
