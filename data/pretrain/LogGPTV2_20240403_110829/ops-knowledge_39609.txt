User:  Analyze the possible fault causes of the following phenomena, and give solutions: The disk space is full, and the disk space is not released after executing rm.
Assistant: Possible cause of failure: the node is not released and the service is still running. Solution: 1. Open the terminal. 2. Use the command ps -ef | grep "corresponding process name" to find the PID of the corresponding process. 3. Use the command kill -9 PID to kill the corresponding process. 4. Execute the rm command to release space.
