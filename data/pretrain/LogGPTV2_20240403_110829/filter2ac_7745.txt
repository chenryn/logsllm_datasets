**ATT&CK ID:** M1006  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Use Recent OS Version

**Description:**
Starting with Android 8, the operating system introduced additional restrictions on the implicit intents that an application can register for. These limitations enhance security by reducing the potential attack surface associated with intent handling.
