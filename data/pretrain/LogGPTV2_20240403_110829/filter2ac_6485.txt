**Title: Black Hat Europe 2018 Locknote: Conclusions and Key Takeaways**

As this year's conference draws to a close, we invite you to join Black Hat Founder Jeff Moss and distinguished members of the Black Hat Review Board for an insightful discussion on the most pressing issues facing the information security (InfoSec) community. During this Locknote session, they will candidly address the key takeaways from Black Hat Europe 2018 and explore how these trends will shape future InfoSec strategies.

This conversation will provide valuable insights into the current state of cybersecurity and offer actionable guidance for professionals in the field. Don't miss this opportunity to gain a deeper understanding of the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.
