**ATT&CK ID:** M0816  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Mitigation Limited or Not Effective

**Description:**
This technique may not be effectively mitigated. Consider implementing controls for the assets and processes that lead to the use of this technique.

**Recommendations:**
- Evaluate and enhance existing security controls to reduce the likelihood of this technique being employed.
- Conduct regular risk assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in your assets and processes.
- Implement monitoring and detection mechanisms to quickly identify and respond to any attempts to use this technique.
