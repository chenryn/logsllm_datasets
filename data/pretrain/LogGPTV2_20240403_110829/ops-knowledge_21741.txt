Assistant: When you're not inside the example dir, you need to specify the path for jetty.home. It may also be a good idea to set the jetty.logs option as well:

java -Djetty.home=example -Djetty.logs=/tmp -Dsolr.solr.home=example/solr -jar example/start.jar
