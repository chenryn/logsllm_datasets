### Purpose of the Test

The purpose of this test was to determine the performance of the 8-VSB receivers under various interference conditions. Due to laboratory limitations, only the case of filtered DTV interference was evaluated. However, tests were conducted using both adjacent channel and taboo DTV channel interference. In some cases, the combination of these interferences was also examined.
