User: What tests are required in the ITSS certification evaluation process in the field of operation and maintenance? What is the method of testing?
Assistant: During the ITSS certification assessment process, some of the following tests may be required: Document review: assess whether the operation and maintenance management system documents, procedures, records, etc. meet the requirements, whether they are complete, and whether they are effective. On-site inspection: evaluate whether the operation and maintenance management system is effectively implemented and meets the requirements, including on-site inspection, interviews, observations, etc. Test verification: evaluate the performance and functions of the operation and maintenance management system, including functional testing, performance testing, compatibility testing, security testing and other methods. Evaluation report: evaluate the effect and results of the operation and maintenance management system, including internal audit, self-assessment, third-party evaluation, etc.
