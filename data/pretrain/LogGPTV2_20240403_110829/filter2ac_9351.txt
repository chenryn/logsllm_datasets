**Optimized Version:**

**ATT&CK ID:** M1040  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Behavior Prevention on Endpoint

To enhance security on Windows 10, it is recommended to enable Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) rules. These rules can help prevent Office applications from:

1. Creating child processes.
2. Writing potentially malicious executable content to the disk.
