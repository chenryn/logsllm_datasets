# G0012: Darkhotel
## Description
[Darkhotel](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0012) is a threat group that has been active since at least 2004. The group primarily targets hotel and business center Wi-Fi and physical connections, as well as peer-to-peer and file-sharing networks. They have also employed spearphishing tactics. (Citation: Kaspersky Darkhotel)
