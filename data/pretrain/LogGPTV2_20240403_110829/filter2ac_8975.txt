**ATT&CK ID:** M1037  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Filter Network Traffic

To effectively mitigate the risks associated with remote access tools, it is crucial to properly configure firewalls, application firewalls, and proxies. These configurations should restrict outgoing traffic to only those sites and services that are necessary for legitimate business operations. This approach helps in minimizing the potential for unauthorized data exfiltration and other malicious activities.
