**Device:** Nexus 4  
**Browser:** Chrome 32

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open the URL `http://getbootstrap.com/2.3.2/` in Chrome on an Android device.
2. Click the menu button located at the top right of the page to open the navigation menu. At this point, all links should display correctly.
3. Close the menu by clicking the menu button again.
4. Reopen the menu for the second time. You will notice that all the links have disappeared.

**Expected Behavior:**
- The navigation links should remain visible every time the menu is opened.

**Actual Behavior:**
- The links disappear when the menu is opened for the second time.
