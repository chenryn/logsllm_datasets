User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 4.7, Manage Default Accounts on Enterprise Assets and Software?
Assistant: An organization should Manage default accounts on enterprise assets and software, such as root, administrator, and other pre-configured vendor accounts. Example implementations can include: disabling default accounts or making them unusable.CIS Control 4.7 has the asset type Users, and has the security function Protect.
