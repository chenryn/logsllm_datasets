**ATT&CK ID:** G0027  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Threat Group-3390

Threat Group-3390 has been observed to use a tool that creates new Registry keys under the following paths:
- `HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Classes\`
- `HKLM\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Services\`
