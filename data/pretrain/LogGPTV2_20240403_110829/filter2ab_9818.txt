**ATT&CK ID:** S0263  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** TYPEFRAME

**Description:**
TYPEFRAME is capable of installing encrypted configuration data in the Windows Registry. Specifically, it targets the following registry keys:
- `HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\ShellCompatibility\Applications\laxhost.dll`
- `HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\PrintConfigs`
