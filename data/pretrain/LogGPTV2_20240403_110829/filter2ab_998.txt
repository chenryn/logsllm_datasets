**Title: Black Hat Asia 2023 NOC Report**

The Black Hat NOC (Network Operations Center) team is back with another year of insightful and often surprising statistics. In this report, we will share the data that continues to both puzzle and entertain us. We will provide an in-depth look at the tools and techniques we employ to set up, stabilize, and secure the network. Additionally, we will highlight the changes and improvements we have implemented over the past year to enhance our operations.

We will also delve into some of the more humorous and intriguing network activities observed during the event. These insights offer a unique perspective on how security professionals behave on an open WiFi network, providing valuable lessons for the broader cybersecurity community.

Join us as we explore the challenges, innovations, and unexpected moments that make each Black Hat Asia conference a unique and educational experience.
