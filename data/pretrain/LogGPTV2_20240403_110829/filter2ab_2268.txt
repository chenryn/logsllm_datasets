**ATT&CK ID:** M1054  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Software Configuration

**Recommendations:**

- **Automated Relaunching of Forwarding Mechanisms:** Consider configuring forwarding mechanisms to automatically relaunch at regular intervals, such as on a time-based schedule or upon user logon. This ensures that these mechanisms remain active and up-to-date.

- **Change Management for Firewall Rules and System Configurations:** Implement a robust change management process for firewall rules and other related system configurations. This includes documenting all changes, conducting regular reviews, and ensuring that updates are tested and approved before deployment. Proper change management helps maintain the integrity and security of your systems.

By following these recommendations, you can enhance the resilience and security of your software configurations.
