**ATT&CK ID:** S0401  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Exaramel for Linux

**Description:**
Exaramel for Linux is a malicious tool that can execute commands with elevated privileges by leveraging a specific binary with setuid functionality. This allows the malware to perform actions with the permissions of the file's owner, typically root, thereby bypassing standard user permission restrictions. [4]
