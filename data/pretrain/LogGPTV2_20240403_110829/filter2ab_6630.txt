**ATT&CK ID:** S0264  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** OopsIE

**Description:**
OopsIE is a malware tool capable of downloading files from its command-and-control (C2) server to the victim's machine. This functionality allows the adversary to deliver additional malicious payloads or tools, enhancing their control over the compromised system.
