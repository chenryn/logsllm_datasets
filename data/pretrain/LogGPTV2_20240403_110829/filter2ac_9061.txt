**ATT&CK ID:** M1042  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Disable or Remove Feature or Program

**Recommendation:**
To enhance system security, it is recommended to disable the "AlwaysInstallElevated" policy. This will prevent the elevated execution of Windows Installer packages, thereby reducing the risk of unauthorized changes to the system.
