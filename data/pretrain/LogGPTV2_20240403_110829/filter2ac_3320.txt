**ATT&CK ID:** S0251  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Zebrocy

**Description:**
Zebrocy achieves persistence by adding a logon script to the Windows Registry. Specifically, it modifies the `UserInitMprLogonScript` value under the `HKCU\Environment` key. This technique ensures that the malicious script is executed every time the user logs on to the system.
