**ATT&CK ID:** S0332  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Remcos

**Description:**
Remcos is a remote access trojan (RAT) that can be used to search for and manipulate files on an infected machine. [247] 

This capability allows the attacker to locate and potentially exfiltrate, modify, or delete specific files, thereby compromising the security and integrity of the victim's data.
