**ATT&CK ID:** S0575  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Conti

**Description:**
Conti ransomware is capable of deleting Windows Volume Shadow Copies by utilizing the `vssadmin` command. This action can hinder data recovery efforts and increase the impact of a successful attack.
