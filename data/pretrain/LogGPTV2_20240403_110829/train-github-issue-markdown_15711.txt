# Environment
- **Windows Build Number:** 2004 (19041.329)
- **PowerToys Version:** 19
- **PowerToy Module (if applicable):** N/A

# Steps to Reproduce
1. Press the Space Bar in most applications, including Adobe Premiere Pro, Microsoft Edge (Chromium), and Google Chrome.
2. Observe that the PowerToys search is triggered, even when all PowerToys modules are disabled.

# Expected Behavior
- The Space Bar should function as intended within the application, without triggering the PowerToys search.

# Actual Behavior
- The PowerToys search appears, blocking the Space Bar input from the intended application, even when all PowerToys modules are disabled.
