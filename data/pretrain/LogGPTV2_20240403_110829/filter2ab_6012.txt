**ATT&CK ID:** S0357  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Impacket

The `SecretsDump` and `Mimikatz` modules within Impacket can be used to perform credential dumping, allowing attackers to extract account and password information from the NTDS.dit file.[15]
