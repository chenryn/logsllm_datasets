**ATT&CK ID:** S0501  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PipeMon

**Description:**
PipeMon can achieve persistence by registering a malicious DLL as an alternative Print Processor. This DLL is loaded when the print spooler service starts, allowing the malware to maintain a presence on the system.
