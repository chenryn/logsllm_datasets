**ATT&CK ID:** S1012  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** PowerLess

**Description:**
PowerLess is a technique that involves writing and executing PowerShell code without invoking the `powershell.exe` process. This method can be used to evade detection by security tools that monitor or block the use of `powershell.exe`.
