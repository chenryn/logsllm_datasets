**ATT&CK ID:** G0122  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Silent Librarian

**Description:**
Silent Librarian, a threat actor, has been observed using freely available and publicly accessible tools such as SingleFile and HTTrack to replicate the login pages of targeted organizations. These tools enable the group to create convincing phishing sites, thereby facilitating credential theft.
