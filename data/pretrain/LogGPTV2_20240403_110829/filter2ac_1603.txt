**ATT&CK ID:** S0645  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Wevtutil

**Description:**
Wevtutil is a command-line utility that can be used to clear system and security event logs from a Windows system. This tool is often utilized by attackers to erase evidence of their activities, making it more difficult for forensic analysts to detect and investigate malicious actions.
