**ATT&CK ID:** T1560.001  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Archive via Utility

Adversaries may utilize various utilities to compress and/or encrypt data before exfiltrating it. These utilities often provide features for compressing, encrypting, or otherwise packaging the data into a format that is more convenient and secure for transport. This process can help adversaries reduce the size of the data and protect it from detection during exfiltration.
