### Summary of the New Feature/Enhancement

We are introducing the capability to customize and add shortcuts in the Shortcut Guide. This enhancement will allow users to:

1. **Personalize Shortcut Visibility:** Choose which shortcuts to display in the Shortcut Guide, tailoring the interface to their specific needs.
2. **Add Custom Shortcuts:** Create and include custom shortcuts, such as `Win + .`, to the guide. This will eliminate the need to frequently search for these shortcuts online.

This feature will provide a more personalized and efficient user experience, ensuring that essential shortcuts are always at your fingertips.
