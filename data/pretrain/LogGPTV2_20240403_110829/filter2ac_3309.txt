**ATT&CK ID:** S0581  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** IronNetInjector

IronNetInjector is a tool that can obfuscate variable names, encrypt strings, and encode payloads using base64. Additionally, it supports Rijndael encryption for further payload protection.[170]

This version clarifies the capabilities of IronNetInjector and presents the information in a more structured and professional manner.
