**ATT&CK ID:** C0025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack

In the 2016 Ukraine Electric Power Attack, the Sandworm Team employed a method to achieve persistence for the Industroyer malware. Specifically, they configured an arbitrary system service to load at system startup. Additionally, they modified the `ImagePath` registry value of a legitimate Windows service, replacing it with a new backdoor binary. [6]
