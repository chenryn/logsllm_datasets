**ATT&CK ID:** G0050  
**Technique Name:** APT32

APT32, a well-known threat actor, has created websites that contain a variety of articles and content scraped from the internet to give the appearance of legitimacy. However, some of these pages are embedded with malicious JavaScript. This code is designed to profile potential victims or deliver malware through fake software update prompts.
