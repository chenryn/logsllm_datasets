**ATT&CK ID:** S0249  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Gold Dragon

**Description:**
Gold Dragon is a technique that involves collecting the username of the endpoint victim. This username is then used as a basis for downloading additional components from the command-and-control (C2) server.[74]
