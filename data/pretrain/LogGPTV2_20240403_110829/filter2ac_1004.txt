**ATT&CK ID:** G0045  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** menuPass

**Description:**
menuPass, a known threat actor, has employed various tactics in spearphishing campaigns. Specifically, they have distributed malicious Office documents via email and executables disguised as documents to deceive targets. [141][142][143][144]
