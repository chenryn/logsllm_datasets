**ATT&CK ID:** S0481  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Ragnar Locker

Ragnar Locker employs VirtualBox along with a streamlined Windows XP virtual machine to execute its operations. By specifying a shared folder in the configuration, Ragnar Locker is able to encrypt files on the host operating system, including those located on any mapped drives. [2]
