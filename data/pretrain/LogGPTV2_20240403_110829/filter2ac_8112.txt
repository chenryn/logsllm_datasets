**ATT&CK ID:** M0930  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Segmentation

**Description:**
Implement network segmentation to divide operational assets and their management devices based on their functional roles within the process. This approach allows for more stringent isolation of critical control and operational information within the control environment, enhancing overall security and reducing the potential impact of a breach.
