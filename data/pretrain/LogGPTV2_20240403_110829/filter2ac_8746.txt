**ATT&CK ID:** S0223  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** POWERSTATS

**Description:**
POWERSTATS is a malicious tool that can delete all files on the C:\, D:\, E:\, and F:\ drives by using PowerShell `Remove-Item` commands. [182]
