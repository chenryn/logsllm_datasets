### Keyboard Shortcuts for Adobe Lightroom

#### General Shortcuts
- **`Ctrl` `Alt` `Shift` `E`**: Export with previous settings
- **`Ctrl` `Alt` `Shift` `,`**: Open plug-in manager
- **`Ctrl` `P`**: Print selected photo
- **`Ctrl` `Shift` `P`**: Open Page Setup dialog box
