**ATT&CK ID:** M1032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

**Description:**
Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) for both user and privileged accounts. This adds an additional layer of security by requiring more than one form of verification. Most embedded network devices support protocols such as TACACS+ and RADIUS for MFA. 

**Best Practices:**
- Follow the vendor's recommended best practices for hardening access control.
- Ensure that MFA is enabled and configured correctly according to the device's specifications.
