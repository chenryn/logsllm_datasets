**ATT&CK ID:** G0121  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Sidewinder

**Description:**
Sidewinder has been observed adding paths to executables in the Windows Registry to establish persistence. This technique allows the malware to execute automatically when the system starts, ensuring long-term access to the compromised system. References for this behavior can be found in [241], [242], and [243].
