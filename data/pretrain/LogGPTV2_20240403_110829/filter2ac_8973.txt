**ATT&CK ID:** M1017  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** User Training

**Recommendation:**
Regularly close all browser sessions, especially when they are no longer needed. This practice helps to minimize the risk of unauthorized access and ensures that sensitive information is not inadvertently exposed.
