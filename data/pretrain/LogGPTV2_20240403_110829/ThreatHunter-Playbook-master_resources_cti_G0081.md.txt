# G0081: Tropic Trooper

## Description
[Tropic Trooper](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0081) is an unaffiliated threat group that has conducted targeted campaigns against organizations in Taiwan, the Philippines, and Hong Kong. The group primarily targets government, healthcare, transportation, and high-tech industries. Tropic Trooper has been active since 2011, as documented in reports by TrendMicro (Citation: TrendMicro Tropic Trooper Mar 2018) and Unit 42 (Citation: Unit 42 Tropic Trooper Nov 2016).
