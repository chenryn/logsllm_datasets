**ATT&CK ID:** G0127  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** TA551

**Description:**
TA551 is known for distributing spearphishing emails that contain password-protected ZIP files. These attachments are often used to deliver malicious payloads to the victims. References for this technique can be found in sources [222], [223], and [224].
