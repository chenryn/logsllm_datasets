**ATT&CK ID:** S0601  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Hildegard

**Description:**
Hildegard is a technique that involves packing ELF (Executable and Linkable Format) files into other binaries. This method can be used to obfuscate the presence of malicious code and evade detection by security tools.[48]
