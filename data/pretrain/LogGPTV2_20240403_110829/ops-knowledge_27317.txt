User: The database instances used by each business line exceed the usage limit, and capacity planning and expansion operations are required to ensure the normal operation of the business.
Assistant: Capacity planning and capacity expansion operations are effective means to solve the database instance exceeding the usage limit. First, you need to analyze the current usage of the database to understand the structure, size, load, etc. Then, based on business needs and development conditions, carry out capacity planning to determine the capacity and computing power that need to be expanded. Then, choose an appropriate expansion method according to the actual situation, such as horizontal expansion, vertical expansion, etc., and implement the expansion. Finally, application testing is carried out to ensure that the performance and stability of the expanded database meet business requirements, so as to ensure the normal operation of the business.
