# G0095: Machete

## Description
Machete (G0095) is a threat actor that has been active since at least 2010, primarily targeting high-profile government entities in Latin American countries. (Citation: Cylance Machete Mar 2017) (Citation: Securelist Machete Aug 2014) (Citation: ESET Machete July 2019)
