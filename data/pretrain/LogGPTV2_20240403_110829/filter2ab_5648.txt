**ATT&CK ID: G0034**  
**ATT&CK Technique Name: Sandworm Team**

The Sandworm Team has employed various techniques to obfuscate and secure their Python-based backdoor. Specifically, they have utilized ROT13 encoding, AES encryption, and compression with the zlib library.[76]
