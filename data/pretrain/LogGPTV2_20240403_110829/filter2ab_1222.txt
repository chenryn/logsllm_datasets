**ATT&CK ID:** G0096  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT41

APT41, a sophisticated threat actor, has been known to modify legitimate Windows services in order to install malware backdoors. Specifically, APT41 created a service called "StorSyncSvc" to establish persistence for Cobalt Strike, a popular penetration testing tool that is often used maliciously by attackers. This tactic allows the group to maintain long-term access to compromised systems.
