**ATT&CK ID:** G0087  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT39

**Description:**
APT39 has been observed creating scheduled tasks as a method for maintaining persistence on compromised systems. This technique allows the threat actor to execute malicious code at specific intervals or upon system startup, ensuring continued access and control over the target environment.
