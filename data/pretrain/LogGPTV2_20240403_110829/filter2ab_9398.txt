**ATT&CK ID:** S0089  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** BlackEnergy

BlackEnergy has been observed to execute a plug-in on a compromised system, enabling it to spread through the local network. This propagation is achieved by leveraging PsExec and accessing administrative shares.[11]
