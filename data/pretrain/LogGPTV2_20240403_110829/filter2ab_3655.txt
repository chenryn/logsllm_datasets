**ATT&CK ID:** G0134  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Transparent Tribe

**Description:**
Transparent Tribe, also known as APT36, has been observed compromising domains for use in targeted malicious campaigns. These compromised domains are often used to host and distribute malware, as well as to facilitate command and control (C2) communications. [17]
