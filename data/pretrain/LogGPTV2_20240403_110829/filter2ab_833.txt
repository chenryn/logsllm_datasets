**ATT&CK ID:** S0659  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Diavol

After encrypting the victim's files, Diavol captures the desktop background, changes the background color to black, and replaces the desktop wallpaper with a newly created bitmap image. This image displays the following text: "All your files are encrypted! For more information, see 'README-FOR-DECRYPT.txt'."
