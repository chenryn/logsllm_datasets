**ATT&CK ID:** S0334  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** DarkComet

**Description:**
DarkComet is a remote access trojan (RAT) that can covertly activate and use the victim's system microphone to eavesdrop on conversations. This capability allows attackers to gather sensitive information from audio recordings without the victim's knowledge. References for this technique can be found in [7] and [8].
