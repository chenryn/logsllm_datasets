### Malware Descriptions

#### Machete
**Overview:**
Machete is a Python-based backdoor targeting Windows machines, first observed in 2010. It is used for cyber espionage and has been attributed to the threat group Machete.

**References:**
- ESET. (2019, July). MACHETE JUST GOT SHARPER: Venezuelan government institutions under attack. [Link](https://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/ESET_Machete.pdf)
- Kaspersky Global Research and Analysis Team. (2014, August 20). El Machete. [Link](https://securelist.com/el-machete/66108/)
- 360. (2020, September 25). APT-C-43 steals Venezuelan military secrets to provide intelligence support for the reactionaries — HpReact campaign. [Link](https://blog.360totalsecurity.com/en/apt-c-43-steals-venezuelan-military-secrets-to-provide-intelligence-support-for-the-reactionaries-hpreact-campaign/)

#### PowerLess
**Overview:**
PowerLess is a PowerShell-based modular backdoor used by the Magic Hound group since at least 2022. It targets Windows platforms.

**References:**
- Cybereason Nocturnus. (2022, February 1). PowerLess Trojan: Iranian APT Phosphorus Adds New PowerShell Backdoor for Espionage. [Link](https://www.cybereason.com/blog/research/powerless-trojan-iranian-apt-phosphorus-adds-new-powershell-backdoor-for-espionage)

---

#### Action RAT
**Overview:**
Action RAT is a remote access tool written in Delphi, used by the SideCopy group since at least December 2021. It has been deployed against Indian and Afghani government personnel.

**References:**
- Threat Intelligence Team. (2021, December 2). SideCopy APT: Connecting lures victims, payloads to infrastructure. [Link](https://www.malwarebytes.com/blog/news/2021/12/sidecopy-apt-connecting-lures-to-victims-payloads-to-infrastructure)

---

#### Avenger
**Overview:**
Avenger is a downloader used by BRONZE BUTLER since at least 2019. It targets Windows systems.

**References:**
- Chen, J. et al. (2019, November). Operation ENDTRADE: TICK’s Multi-Stage Backdoors for Attacking Industries and Stealing Classified Data. [Link](https://documents.trendmicro.com/assets/pdf/Operation-ENDTRADE-TICK-s-Multi-Stage-Backdoors-for-Attacking-Industries-and-Stealing-Classified-Data.pdf)

---

#### Prikormka
**Overview:**
Prikormka is a malware family used in the Operation Groundbait campaign, primarily observed in Ukraine since 2008.

**References:**
- Cherepanov, A. (2016, May 17). Operation Groundbait: Analysis of a surveillance toolkit. [Link](http://www.welivesecurity.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/Operation-Groundbait.pdf)

---

#### PingPull
**Overview:**
PingPull is a Visual C++-based remote access trojan (RAT) used by GALLIUM since at least June 2022. It targets telecommunications companies, financial institutions, and government entities in multiple countries, including Afghanistan, Australia, Belgium, Cambodia, Malaysia, Mozambique, the Philippines, Russia, and Vietnam.

**References:**
- Unit 42. (2022, June 13). GALLIUM Expands Targeting Across Telecommunications, Government and Finance Sectors With New PingPull Tool. [Link](https://unit42.paloaltonetworks.com/pingpull-gallium/)

---

#### WellMess
**Overview:**
WellMess is a lightweight malware family with variants written in .NET and Golang, used by APT29 since at least 2018. It targets Windows systems.

**References:**
- CISA. (2020, July 16). MAR-10296782-2.v1 — WELLMESS. [Link](https://us-cert.cisa.gov/ncas/analysis-reports/ar20-198b)
- PWC. (2020, July 16). How WellMess malware has been used to target COVID-19 vaccines. [Link](https://www.pwc.co.uk/issues/cyber-security-services/insights/cleaning-up-after-wellmess.html)
- National Cyber Security Centre. (2020, July 16). Advisory: APT29 targets COVID-19 vaccine development. [Link](https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/files/Advisory-APT29-targets-COVID-19-vaccine-development-V1-1.pdf)

#### Dacls
**Overview:**
Dacls is a multi-platform remote access tool used by Lazarus Group since at least December 2019. It targets macOS, Linux, and Windows systems.

**References:**
- Mabutas, G. (2020, May 11). New MacOS Dacls RAT Backdoor Shows Lazarus’ Multi-Platform Attack Capability. [Link](https://blog.trendmicro.com/trendlabs-security-intelligence/new-macos-dacls-rat-backdoor-show-lazarus-multi-platform-attack-capability/)
- Stokes, P. (2020, July 27). Four Distinct Families of Lazarus Malware Target Apple’s macOS Platform. [Link](https://www.sentinelone.com/blog/four-distinct-families-of-lazarus-malware-target-apples-macos-platform/)

---

#### DropBook
**Overview:**
DropBook is a Python-based backdoor compiled with PyInstaller, used in Middle East espionage campaigns.

**References:**

#### Woody RAT
**Overview:**
Woody RAT is a remote access trojan (RAT) used since at least August 2021, primarily targeting Russian organizations.

**References:**
- MalwareBytes Threat Intelligence Team. (2022, August 3). Woody RAT: A new feature-rich malware spotted in the wild. [Link](https://www.malwarebytes.com/blog/threat-intelligence/2022/08/woody-rat-a-new-feature-rich-malware-spotted-in-the-wild)

#### Mafalda
**Overview:**
Mafalda is a flexible interactive implant used by Metador. The name may be inspired by an Argentinian cartoon character popular for political commentary since the 1960s.

**References:**
- Ehrlich, A., et al. (2022, September). THE MYSTERY OF METADOR | AN UNATTRIBUTED THREAT HIDING IN TELCOS, ISPS, AND UNIVERSITIES. [Link](https://assets.sentinelone.com/sentinellabs22/metador#page=1)

---

#### KARAE
**Overview:**
KARAE is a backdoor typically used by APT37 as first-stage malware.

**References:**
- FireEye. (2018, February 20). APT37 (Reaper): The Overlooked North Korean Actor. [Link](https://www2.fireeye.com/rs/848-DID-242/images/rpt_APT37.pdf)

#### Squirrelwaffle
**Overview:**
Squirrelwaffle is a loader first seen in September 2021, used in spam email campaigns to deliver additional malware such as Cobalt Strike and QakBot.

**References:**
- Kumar, A., Stone-Gross, B. (2021, September 28). Squirrelwaffle: New Loader Delivering Cobalt Strike. [Link](https://www.zscaler.com/blogs/security-research/squirrelwaffle-new-loader-delivering-cobalt-strike)
- Palazolo, G. (2021, October 7). SquirrelWaffle: New Malware Loader Delivering Cobalt Strike and QakBot. [Link](https://www.netskope.com/blog/squirrelwaffle-new-malware-loader-delivering-cobalt-strike-and-qakbot)

---

#### ELMER
**Overview:**
ELMER is a non-persistent, proxy-aware HTTP backdoor written in Delphi, used by APT16.

**References:**
- Winters, R. (2015, December 20). The EPS Awakens - Part 2. [Link](https://web.archive.org/web/20151226205946/https://www.fireeye.com/blog/threat-research/2015/12/the-eps-awakens-part-two.html)
