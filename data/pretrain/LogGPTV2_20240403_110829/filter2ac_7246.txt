**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group has been known to abuse the `KernelCallbackTable` to hijack process control flow and execute shellcode.[1][5]

---

### Explanation:
- **Lazarus Group (G0032):** The Lazarus Group is a well-known, state-sponsored threat actor that has been involved in various high-profile cyber attacks.
- **Abuse of KernelCallbackTable:** By manipulating the `KernelCallbackTable`, the group can redirect the execution flow of a legitimate process to their malicious shellcode, allowing them to maintain persistence and evade detection.
