User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 15.1, Establish and Maintain an Inventory of Service Providers?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and maintain an inventory of service providers. The inventory is to list all known service providers, include classification(s), and designate an enterprise contact for each service provider. Review and update the inventory annually, or when significant enterprise changes occur that could impact this Safeguard. CIS Control 15.1 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Identify.
