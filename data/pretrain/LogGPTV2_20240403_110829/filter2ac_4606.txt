**ATT&CK ID:** G0125  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** HAFNIUM

HAFNIUM has exploited the following vulnerabilities—CVE-2021-26855, CVE-2021-26857, CVE-2021-26858, and CVE-2021-27065—to compromise on-premises versions of Microsoft Exchange Server. This exploitation allows them to gain access to email accounts and install additional malware. [33][34][35][36]
