**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

**Description:**
The Lazarus Group, a notorious threat actor, has been known to employ a specific tactic in their cyber-attacks. After rendering the system unbootable through a Disk Structure Wipe, they replace the background wallpaper with a threatening image. This method not only disrupts the target's operations but also serves as a psychological intimidation tactic.
