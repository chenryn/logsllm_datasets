**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group, a notorious cyber threat actor, employs `cmd.exe` to execute commands on compromised hosts.[186][187][188][189][190] Additionally, a variant similar to Destover, which is also used by the Lazarus Group, utilizes a batch file mechanism to delete its binaries from the system, thereby evading detection and removal.[191]
