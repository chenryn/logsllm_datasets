In python2.6.6 (I do not have more recent versions available at work) there is
a problem with scipy.special.gammaincinv with the second parameter (the
quantile) set as 0.25. For example
> > > gammaincinv(1.0, 0.25)  
>  0.0  
>  gammaincinv(2.0, 0.25)  
>  0.0  
>  gammaincinv(3.0, 0.25)  
>  0.0  
>  gammaincinv(3.0, 0.24)  
>  1.6894710461335511  
>  gammaincinv(1.0, 0.24)  
>  0.27443684570176036  
>  gammaincinv(1.0, 0.26)  
>  0.30110509278392172
