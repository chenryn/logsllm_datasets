**ATT&CK ID:** G0022  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT3

APT3 employs the Remote Desktop Protocol (RDP) for persistence. Specifically, APT3 enables RDP on compromised systems to maintain access over time. Additionally, APT3 has been observed using RDP sessions to browse and copy files from these systems.
