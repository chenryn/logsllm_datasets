### Optimized Version:

**ATT&CK ID:** G0102  
**ATT&CK Group Name:** Wizard Spider

**Description:**
Wizard Spider, a threat group, has utilized scheduled tasks to achieve persistence for TrickBot and other types of malware. This technique allows the malware to maintain a foothold on the compromised system, even after reboots or other interruptions.[178][179][180][181]
