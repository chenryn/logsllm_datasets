**ATT&CK ID:** C0024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SolarWinds Compromise

During the SolarWinds compromise, APT29 employed the `Get-AcceptedDomain` PowerShell cmdlet to enumerate accepted domains via the Exchange Management Shell.[28] Additionally, they utilized AdFind to enumerate domains and to identify trust relationships between federated domains.[29][30]
