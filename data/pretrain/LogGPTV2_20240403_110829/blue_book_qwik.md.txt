**Qwik** is a cutting-edge web framework designed to deliver instantly loading web applications, regardless of their size or complexity. It enables your sites and apps to boot with as little as 1KB of JavaScript, ensuring consistent performance at scale.

For a comprehensive overview, you can watch the [Qwik presentation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0dC11DMR3fU&t=154s).
