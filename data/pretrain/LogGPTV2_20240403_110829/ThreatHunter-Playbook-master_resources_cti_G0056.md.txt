# G0056: PROMETHIUM

## Description
[PROMETHIUM](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0056) is an activity group that has been operational since at least 2012. The group launched a significant campaign in May 2016, primarily targeting Turkish victims. [PROMETHIUM](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0056) exhibits similarities to another activity group, [NEODYMIUM](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0055), due to overlapping victim profiles and campaign characteristics. (Citation: Microsoft NEODYMIUM Dec 2016) (Citation: Microsoft SIR Vol 21)
