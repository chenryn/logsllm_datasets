**ATT&CK ID:** S1030  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Squirrelwaffle

Squirrelwaffle malware typically relies on users enabling malicious macros within Microsoft Excel and Word attachments. This method allows the malware to execute and compromise the system. References for this technique can be found in sources [220] and [221].
