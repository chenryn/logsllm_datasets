User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 11.1, Establish and Maintain a Data Recovery Process ?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and maintain a data recovery process. In the process, address the scope of data recovery activities, recovery prioritization, and the security of backup data. Review and update documentation annually, or when significant enterprise changes occur that could impact this Safeguard. CIS Control 11.1 has the asset type Data, and has the security function Recover.
