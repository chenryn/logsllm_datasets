**ATT&CK ID:** M1045  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Code Signing

**Description:**
Several cloud service providers implement content trust models that mandate container images to be signed by trusted sources. This ensures the integrity and authenticity of the images, reducing the risk of deploying malicious or tampered-with code.
