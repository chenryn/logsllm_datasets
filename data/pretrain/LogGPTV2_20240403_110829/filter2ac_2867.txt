**ATT&CK ID:** M1030  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Network Segmentation

**Recommendation:**
Segregate SNMP (Simple Network Management Protocol) traffic on a dedicated management network. This separation enhances security by isolating critical management communications from other network traffic, reducing the risk of unauthorized access and potential disruptions.
