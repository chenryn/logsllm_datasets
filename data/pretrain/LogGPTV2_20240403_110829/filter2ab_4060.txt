**ATT&CK ID:** S0263  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** TYPEFRAME

One variant of TYPEFRAME decrypts an archive using an RC4 key, then decompresses and installs the decrypted malicious DLL module. Another variant decodes the embedded file by XORing it with the value `0x35`.[241]
