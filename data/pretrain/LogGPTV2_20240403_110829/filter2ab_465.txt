**ATT&CK ID:** G0094  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Kimsuky

Kimsuky has been observed to disable the Windows Security Center and can hide the antivirus (AV) software window from the view of the infected user. [46][47]

---

This version is more concise and maintains a professional tone. The use of "disable" instead of "turn off" is more precise, and the addition of "antivirus (AV)" clarifies the type of software being referred to.
