**ATT&CK ID:** C0022  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Operation Dream Job

During Operation Dream Job, the Lazarus Group employed DOCX files to retrieve a malicious document template or DOTM file. This technique allowed the threat actors to execute their payload and compromise targeted systems.
