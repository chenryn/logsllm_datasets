**ATT&CK ID:** T1025  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Data from Removable Media

Adversaries may search for and collect sensitive data from any removable media (such as optical disk drives, USB memory devices, etc.) connected to a compromised system. This data collection typically occurs before the exfiltration of the information. Adversaries might use interactive command shells and common command-line utilities to gather and identify files of interest on the removable media.
