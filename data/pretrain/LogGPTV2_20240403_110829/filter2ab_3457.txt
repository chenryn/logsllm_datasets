### Optimized Version:

**ATT&CK ID:** S0689  
**Technique Name:** WhisperGate

WhisperGate employs the `AdvancedRun.exe` utility to disable the Windows Defender Threat Protection service and to create an exclusion path for the C:\ drive. This allows the malware to evade detection and persist on the system. [94][95][96]
