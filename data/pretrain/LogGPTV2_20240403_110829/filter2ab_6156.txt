**ATT&CK ID:** G0059  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Magic Hound

Magic Hound has been known to acquire and utilize a variety of tools, including Havij, sqlmap, Metasploit, Mimikatz, and Plink. These tools are often employed in their cyber operations to exploit vulnerabilities, gain unauthorized access, and maintain persistence within target networks. [68][69][70][71][72]
