**ATT&CK ID:** C0024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SolarWinds Compromise

During the SolarWinds compromise, APT29 (also known as Nobelium) added credentials to OAuth applications and service principals. This tactic allowed the threat actor to maintain persistence and access within the compromised environment.
