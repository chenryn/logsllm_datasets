**Title: Black Hat US-20: Unprecedented Felony Arrest Records from an Iowa Red Team Engagement**

Join us for an in-depth discussion and review of a red team operation that targeted Iowa courthouses, leading to an unprecedented outcome. Gary and Justin will guide you through the entire process, from the initial engagement and subsequent arrest to the ensuing legal battle. They will conclude with key lessons learned and insights on how the cybersecurity community can benefit from this experience.
