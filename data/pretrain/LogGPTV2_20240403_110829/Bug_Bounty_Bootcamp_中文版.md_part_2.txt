from compromise. A co-chair of Cloud Security Alliance's IoT Working
Group and a technical reviewer for several published security books, he
also spearheads many open-source initiatives, raising awareness about
IoT hacking and proactive defensive strategies under OWASP's IoT and
Embedded Application Security projects. He has extensive public speaking
experience, delivering conference presentations, training, and workshops
