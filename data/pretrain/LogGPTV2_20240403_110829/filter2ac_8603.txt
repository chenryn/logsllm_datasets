**ATT&CK ID:** G0034  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Sandworm Team

The Sandworm Team, a well-known threat actor, has leveraged various open-source tools in their operations. For instance, during the 2018 Winter Olympics attack, they utilized **Invoke-PSImage** to establish an encrypted communication channel between a compromised host and their command-and-control (C2) server. Additionally, in their 2022 Prestige operations, the group employed **Impacket** and **RemoteExec**. These tools were instrumental in facilitating their malicious activities.
