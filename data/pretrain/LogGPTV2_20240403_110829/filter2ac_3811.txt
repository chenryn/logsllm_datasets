**ATT&CK ID:** S0042  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** LOWBALL

**Description:**
The LOWBALL command and control (C2) communication is conducted over HTTPS on port 443. This method leverages the standard encryption and security features of HTTPS to mask the malicious traffic, making it more difficult for network defenders to detect and block the C2 activity.
