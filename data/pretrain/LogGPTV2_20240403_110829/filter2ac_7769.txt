**ATT&CK ID:** G0010  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Turla

Turla, a known threat actor, has employed WebDAV to upload files stolen from USB drives to cloud storage. Additionally, Turla has exfiltrated stolen data to cloud services such as OneDrive and 4shared.
