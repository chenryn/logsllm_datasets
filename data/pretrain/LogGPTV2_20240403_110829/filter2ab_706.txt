**ATT&CK ID:** G0049  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** OilRig

OilRig has employed credential dumping tools, such as LaZagne, to steal credentials from accounts logged into the compromised system and from Outlook Web Access. [62][63][64][65] Additionally, OilRig has used a tool called PICKPOCKET to extract passwords stored in web browsers. [65]
