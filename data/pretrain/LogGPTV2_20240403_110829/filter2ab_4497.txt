**ATT&CK ID:** G0067  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT37

**Description:**
APT37, also known as Reaper, has been observed downloading second-stage malware from compromised websites. This technique allows the threat actor to deliver additional malicious payloads to infected systems, enhancing their ability to conduct further attacks and maintain persistence. References for this activity can be found in sources [27][28][29][30].
