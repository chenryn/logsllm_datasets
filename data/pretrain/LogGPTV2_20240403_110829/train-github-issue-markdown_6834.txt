The navigation bar's child items function correctly in the desktop view. However, on mobile and tablet devices, the collapsed child items do not navigate to the URLs defined in their `href` attributes and instead remain on the same page.

To reproduce this issue, please use a mobile device to open the following jsFiddle link and click on any item in the dropdown menu:

[http://jsfiddle.net/wXuqU/5/](http://jsfiddle.net/wXuqU/5/)
