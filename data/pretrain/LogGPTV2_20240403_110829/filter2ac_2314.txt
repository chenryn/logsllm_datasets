**ATT&CK ID:** G0016  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT29

APT29, a sophisticated threat actor, has been known to employ various tactics for command and control (C2) communications. One such method involves the use of algorithmically generated Twitter handles, which are leveraged by malware like HAMMERTOSS. Additionally, APT29 has utilized legitimate web services, including Dropbox and Constant Contact, as part of their operational infrastructure.[3][4]
