### 6. Discussion

Advancements in natural language processing, tokenized models, and other neural network architectures may enhance the effectiveness of password guessing. While our study focused on measuring client-side strength metrics based on guessing effectiveness, a significant challenge remains: providing user-interpretable advice to improve passwords during the creation process.
