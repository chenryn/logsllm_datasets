**ATT&CK ID:** S0002  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Mimikatz

Mimikatz is a tool used for credential dumping, which involves extracting account and password information. This information can be leveraged to gain unauthorized access to additional systems and enterprise network resources. Mimikatz offers a variety of methods to acquire credentials, including DCSync and NetSync techniques.[12][8][13][14][15]
