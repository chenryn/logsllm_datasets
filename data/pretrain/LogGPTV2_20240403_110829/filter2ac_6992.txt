**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group, a well-known threat actor, has been associated with the use of SHARPKNOT malware. This malicious software is designed to overwrite and delete the Master Boot Record (MBR) on the victim's machine. The Lazarus Group has been known to possess MBR wiper malware since at least 2009.[17][18]
