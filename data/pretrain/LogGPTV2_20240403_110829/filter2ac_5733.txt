**ATT&CK ID:** C0024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SolarWinds Compromise

During the SolarWinds compromise, APT29 (also known as Nobelium) exfiltrated collected data using a simple HTTPS request. The data was stored in a password-protected archive that was staged on the victim's Outlook Web Access (OWA) servers. [3]
