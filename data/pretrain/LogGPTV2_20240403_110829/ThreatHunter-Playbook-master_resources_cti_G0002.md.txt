# G0002: Moafee
## Description
[Moafee](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0002) is a threat group believed to operate from the Guangdong Province of China. Due to overlapping tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs), including the use of similar custom tools, Moafee is suspected to have a direct or indirect relationship with the threat group [DragonOK](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0017). (Citation: Haq 2014)
