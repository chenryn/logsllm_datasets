**ATT&CK ID:** S0372  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** LockerGoga

**Description:**
LockerGoga is a ransomware variant that has been observed to change account passwords and forcibly log off current users. [1][2]

This behavior can significantly disrupt normal operations by preventing legitimate users from accessing their accounts and systems, thereby increasing the pressure on victims to pay the ransom.
