**ATT&CK ID:** S0249  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Gold Dragon

**Description:**
Gold Dragon is a technique that involves listing the directories for the Desktop, Program Files, and the user's recently accessed files. This method is often used to gather information about the target system and identify potential targets for further exploitation.
