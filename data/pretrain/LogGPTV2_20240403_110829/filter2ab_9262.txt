**ATT&CK ID:** C0011  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** T1573 - Encrypted Channel: SSL/TLS

For the technique identified as C0011, the threat group Transparent Tribe has been observed setting up SSL certificates on typo-squatted domains that they have registered. This approach allows them to establish encrypted communication channels, enhancing the stealth and effectiveness of their operations.
