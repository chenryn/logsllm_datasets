**ATT&CK ID:** C0024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SolarWinds Compromise

During the SolarWinds compromise, APT29 (also known as Cozy Bear) utilized privileged accounts to replicate directory service data from domain controllers. This action allowed the threat actors to gain access to sensitive information and further their infiltration into the targeted networks. [17][18][19]
