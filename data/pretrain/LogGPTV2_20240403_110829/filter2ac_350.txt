**ATT&CK ID:** C0024  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** SolarWinds Compromise

During the SolarWinds compromise, APT29 employed a Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) event filter to trigger a command-line event consumer at system startup. This technique was used to launch a backdoor via `rundll32.exe`.[19][20]
