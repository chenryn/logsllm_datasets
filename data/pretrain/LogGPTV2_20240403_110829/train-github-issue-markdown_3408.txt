## Environment:
- **Windows Build Number:** `10.0.18363.657`
- **PowerToys Version:** `0.14.1.0`
- **PowerToy Module:** `FancyZones`

## Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the FancyZones zone editor dialog.
2. Navigate to the `Custom` tab.
3. Click the `Edit selected layout` button.

## Expected Behavior:
The dialog should provide an option to choose between the additive and subtractive methods for editing the layout.

## Actual Behavior:
The dialog launches directly into the additive mode without offering a choice to switch to the subtractive method. There is also no indication or hint on how to access the subtractive method.
