**ATT&CK ID:** S0249  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Gold Dragon

**Description:**
Gold Dragon is a technique that involves checking the running processes on the victim's machine. This allows the attacker to gather information about the active processes, which can be used for further exploitation or to avoid detection by security software.
