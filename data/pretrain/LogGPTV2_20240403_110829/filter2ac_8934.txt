**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT28

APT28, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed using various tools to capture screenshots from victims' devices. This technique is often employed as part of their broader espionage and data exfiltration activities. References to this behavior can be found in the following sources: [10][11][12][13].
