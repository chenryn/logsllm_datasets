**ATT&CK ID:** S0689  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** WhisperGate

**Description:**
WhisperGate is a malicious technique that overwrites the Master Boot Record (MBR) on targeted systems with a 16-bit bootloader. This action can render the system inoperable and is often used to cause significant disruption. [22][23][24][25][26][27]
