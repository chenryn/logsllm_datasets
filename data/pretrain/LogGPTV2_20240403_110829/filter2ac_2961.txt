**ATT&CK ID:** S0093  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Backdoor.Oldrea

The Backdoor.Oldrea ICS malware plugin utilizes Windows networking (WNet) to identify all servers, including OPC (OLE for Process Control) servers, that are accessible from the compromised machine over the network. [2]
