**ATT&CK ID:** G0032  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Lazarus Group

The Lazarus Group has been known to utilize the Windows API function `ObtainUserAgentString` to retrieve the User-Agent string from a compromised host, which is then used to establish a connection with a command-and-control (C2) server. [106] Additionally, the group has employed a variety of often lesser-known functions to perform different types of discovery and process injection. [107][108]
