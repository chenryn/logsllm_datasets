**ATT&CK ID:** G0034  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Sandworm Team

**Description:**
The Sandworm Team, a known threat actor, often sets up an internal proxy within the target network before installing backdoors. This technique facilitates their malicious activities by providing a more stable and less detectable communication channel. [3]
