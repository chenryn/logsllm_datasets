**ATT&CK ID:** C0022  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Operation Dream Job

In the Operation Dream Job campaign, the Lazarus Group employed various encryption techniques to obfuscate their malware. Specifically, they used XOR encryption for the DRATzarus malware and base64 encoding for DLL files. These methods were designed to evade detection and analysis by security systems.
