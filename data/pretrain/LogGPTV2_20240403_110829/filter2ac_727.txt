**ATT&CK ID:** S1065  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Woody RAT

**Description:**
Woody RAT is typically deployed through the execution of a malicious email attachment. Users are often tricked into opening these attachments, which then initiates the malware's execution on the target system.[257]
