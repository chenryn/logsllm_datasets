ATT&CK ID:S0251
ATT&CK Technique Name:Zebrocy
Zebrocy searches for files that are 60mb and less and contain the following extensions: .doc, .docx, .xls, .xlsx, .ppt, .pptx, .exe, .zip, and .rar. Zebrocy also runs the echo %APPDATA% command to list the contents of the directory.[334][335][336] Zebrocy can obtain the current execution path as well as perform drive enumeration.[337][338]
