**ATT&CK ID:** S1072  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Industroyer2

**Description:**
Industroyer2 is a malicious tool designed to manipulate specific Information Object Addresses (IOAs) for designated Application Service Data Unit (ASDU) addresses. This manipulation can set the targeted IOAs to either the ON or OFF state, thereby disrupting the normal operation of industrial control systems. [2][3]
