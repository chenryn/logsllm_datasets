Seen from this perspective, the ﬂaws in the
r-utilities are clear.
One more point should be mentioned: it
is often the availability of auxiliary data that
makes attacks possible. An attacker who
can create just one TCP connection can-
not guess the correct sequence number.
This is often a fruitful mechanism for block-
ing (or at least detecting) attacks.
