**Title: Botconf 2015 Takedowns: Case Studies and Best Practices for Success**

**Abstract:**
We have all encountered sensational headlines about major cyber threats being dismantled, only to resurface within days or even hours. However, some takedown operations have achieved lasting results. This presentation will delve into the strategies and methodologies behind these successful takedowns, highlighting the most effective tools and techniques. Additionally, we will explore actionable steps that can be taken to increase the frequency and effectiveness of future takedown efforts.
