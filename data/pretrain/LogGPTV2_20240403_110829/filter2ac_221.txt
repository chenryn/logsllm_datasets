**ATT&CK ID:** M1040  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Behavior Prevention on Endpoint

**Description:**
On Windows 10, enable Attack Surface Reduction (ASR) rules to prevent the execution of processes created by PsExec. [71]

**Steps to Implement:**
1. Open the Group Policy Editor or use a management tool such as Microsoft Intune.
2. Navigate to the ASR rules settings.
3. Enable the specific ASR rule that blocks processes created by PsExec.
4. Apply and enforce the policy across your organization.
