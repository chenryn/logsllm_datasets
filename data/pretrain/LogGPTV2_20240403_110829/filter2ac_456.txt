**ATT&CK ID:** T1037  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Boot or Logon Initialization Scripts

Adversaries may use initialization scripts that are automatically executed at system boot or user logon to establish persistence. These scripts can be used to perform various administrative tasks, such as executing other programs or sending information to an internal logging server. The specific implementation of these scripts can vary depending on the operating system and whether they are applied locally or remotely.
