**ATT&CK ID:** T1597.002  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Purchase Technical Data

Adversaries may acquire technical information about their targets through the purchase of such data. This information can be obtained from a variety of sources, including reputable private databases and less-reputable sources on the dark web or cybercrime black markets.

Reputable sources for this type of data often include paid subscriptions to scan databases and other data aggregation services, which provide detailed and up-to-date information. On the other hand, adversaries may also turn to the dark web or cybercrime black markets, where they can purchase technical data, though the reliability and accuracy of this information may vary.

This acquired technical data can be used by adversaries to enhance their targeting efforts, enabling them to better understand and exploit their victims' systems and networks.
