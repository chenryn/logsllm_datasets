Obtaining a Ham Radio License
 Easy for anyone with ½ a brain
 Only need a Technician class license to do the things in
this talk
 Study the ARRL (arrl.org) study guide
 Memorize the test online (not recommended)
 Take the test (check arrl.org for times & locations)
 Wait for FCC to post your license record
 Once a call-sign is assigned….You’re Good To Go!

About APRS (aprs.org)
 Developed in the late 1980’s
 Developed by Bob Bruninga, WB4APR
 Real-time tactical digital protocol using AX.25
 Shares position and ID (call sign) info among a large
number of local stations
 Digipeater enables sharing over wide area
 Traffic routed to internet via IGate
