**ATT&CK ID:** G0094  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Kimsuky

Kimsuky, a threat actor, has employed spearphishing campaigns that involve sending emails containing malicious attachments. These attachments typically include Word, Excel, and HWP (Hangul Word Processor) documents. This tactic has been observed in multiple instances.[122][123][124][125][126][6][127][128]
