**ATT&CK ID:** T1630  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Indicator Removal on Host

Adversaries may delete, modify, or conceal generated artifacts on a device, such as files, jailbreak status, or the malicious application itself. These actions can interfere with event collection, reporting, and other notifications that are used to detect intrusion activity. By doing so, adversaries can compromise the integrity of mobile security solutions, leading to significant events or information going unreported.
