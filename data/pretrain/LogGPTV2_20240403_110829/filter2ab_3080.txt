**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**Technique Name:** APT28

APT28, a well-known threat actor, frequently employs both publicly available tools (e.g., Mimikatz) and custom-developed password retrieval tools to compromise victim systems. This dual approach enhances their capability to extract credentials and gain unauthorized access.
