**ATT&CK ID:** G0027  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Threat Group-3390

Threat Group-3390 actors are known to search for and exploit Virtual Private Network (VPN) profiles during their operations. By doing so, they gain access to the target network using external VPN services.[42] Additionally, this group has been observed obtaining Outlook Web Access (OWA) account credentials during intrusions. They then use these credentials to attempt to regain access to a victim's network if they are initially detected and removed.[43]
