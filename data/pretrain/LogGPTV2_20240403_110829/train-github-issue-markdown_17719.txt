To improve the formatting and readability of your document, please follow these steps:

1. **Enable Normalize Whitespace Option:**
   - Locate the setting for "Normalize Whitespace" in your text editor or word processing software.
   - Enable this option to ensure consistent spacing throughout the document.

2. **Remove Inline Formatting:**
   - Identify and remove any inline styles or formatting that may be causing inconsistencies.
   - This includes removing unnecessary inline CSS, HTML tags, or other formatting elements that are not essential to the content.

By following these steps, you will achieve a more uniform and professional appearance in your document.
