**ATT&CK ID:** S0499  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Hancitor

Hancitor, a well-known malware, has been observed using Base64 encoding to obfuscate malicious links. Additionally, it has been known to deliver compressed payloads in ZIP files to victims. These tactics are designed to evade detection and increase the likelihood of successful infection.
