**ATT&CK ID:** S0670  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** WarzoneRAT

WarzoneRAT is capable of installing both live and offline keyloggers. This functionality can be achieved through the use of the `GetAsyncKeyState` Windows API, allowing the malware to capture keystrokes even when the system is not actively connected to the internet.
