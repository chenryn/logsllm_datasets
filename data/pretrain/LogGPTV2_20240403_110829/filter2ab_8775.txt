**ATT&CK ID:** S0002  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Mimikatz

Mimikatz is a tool used for credential dumping, which involves extracting account and password information to gain unauthorized access to additional systems and enterprise network resources. It provides a wide range of functionalities to acquire credentials in various ways, including by extracting data from the Local Security Authority Subsystem Service (LSASS) memory.[56][57][58][59]
