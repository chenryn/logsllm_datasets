User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 17.2, Establish and Maintain Contact Information for Reporting Security Incidents?
Assistant: An organization should Establish and maintain contact information for parties that need to be informed of security incidents. Contacts may include internal staff, third-party vendors, law enforcement, cyber insurance providers, relevant government agencies, Information Sharing and Analysis Center (ISAC) partners, or other stakeholders. Verify contacts annually to ensure that information is up-to-date.CIS Control 17.2 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Respond.
