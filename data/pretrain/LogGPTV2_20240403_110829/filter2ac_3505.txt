**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Application Developer Guidance

**Recommendation:**
To enhance security and prevent the side-loading of malicious libraries, it is advisable to include hash values in manifest files whenever possible. This practice ensures that only verified and authentic libraries are loaded, thereby reducing the risk of unauthorized or malicious code execution.
