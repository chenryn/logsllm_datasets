# G0091: Silence
## Description
[Silence](https://attack.mitre.org/groups/G0091) is a financially motivated threat actor that targets financial institutions in various countries. The group first emerged in June 2016, with primary targets located in Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Azerbaijan, Poland, and Kazakhstan. Silence has compromised a range of banking systems, including the Russian Central Bank's Automated Workstation Client, ATMs, and card processing systems. (Citation: Cyber Forensicator Silence Jan 2019)(Citation: SecureList Silence Nov 2017)
