**ATT&CK ID:** C0005  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Operation Spalax

In Operation Spalax, the threat actors employed packers that extracted pixel data from images embedded in the resource sections of PE (Portable Executable) files. This extracted data was then used to construct the next layer of the execution process.
