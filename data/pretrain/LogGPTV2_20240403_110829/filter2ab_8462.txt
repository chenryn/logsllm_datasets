**ATT&CK ID:** G0007  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT28

**Description:**
APT28, a well-known advanced persistent threat (APT) group, has been observed engaging in the collection of files from network shared drives. [1]

This activity is part of their broader strategy to exfiltrate sensitive information and gain deeper access within targeted networks.
