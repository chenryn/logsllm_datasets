**ATT&CK ID:** S1059  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** metaMain

**Description:**
When executed with SYSTEM privileges, the `metaMain` technique can modify the following file time attributes:
- **CreationTime**
- **LastAccessTime**
- **LastWriteTime**
