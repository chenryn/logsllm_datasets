**Title: BotConf 2013 - Participatory Honeypots: A Paradigm Shift in the Fight Against Mobile Botnets**

The mobile security landscape presents a significantly different and more complex ecosystem compared to traditional computing environments. This complexity makes it challenging to detect and respond to malware attacks using conventional methods. To address this, we introduce the concept of **Participatory Honeypots**. This privacy-by-design system engages users as partners in the collection of valuable information, which is then used for detailed analysis and threat mitigation.
