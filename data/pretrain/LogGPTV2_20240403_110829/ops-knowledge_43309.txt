User: What are the main procedures an organization should follow in order to implement CIS Control 17.7, Conduct Routine Incident Response Exercises?
Assistant: An organization should Plan and conduct routine incident response exercises and scenarios for key personnel involved in the incident response process to prepare for responding to real-world incidents. Exercises need to test communication channels, decision making, and workflows. Conduct testing on an annual basis, at a minimum.CIS Control 17.7 has the asset type N/A, and has the security function Recover.
