**ATT&CK ID:** G0034  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** Sandworm Team

**Description:**
The Sandworm Team, a known threat actor, has been observed creating email accounts that mimic legitimate organizations. These spoofed accounts are used in their spearphishing campaigns to deceive targets and gain unauthorized access to systems and information.[16]
