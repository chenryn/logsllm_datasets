### Ethical and Legal Issues in Research Using Illicit Datasets

#### Summary of Legal and Ethical Issues

The following table summarizes the legal and ethical issues, as well as the justifications provided by the authors for each paper. Legal issues are defined in §5, ethical issues in §4.3, and justifications in §7.3.

- **•** indicates that the legal issue is applicable (even if it was not discussed).
- **** indicates that the issue was discussed or the justification was used.
- **** indicates that the issue was not discussed or the justification was not used.
- **G** indicates that the authors decided the work could not be justified and did not use the dataset.
- In the REB approval column, **∅** means not applicable, and **E** means exempt.
- **Safeguards:**
  - SS: Secure Storage
  - P: Privacy
  - CS: Controlled Sharing
- **Harms:**
  - I: Illicit Measurement
  - PA: Potential Abuse
  - DA: De-Anonymization
  - SI: Sensitive Information
  - RH: Researcher Harm
  - BC: Behavioral Change
- **Benefits:**
  - R: Reproducibility
  - U: Uniqueness
  - DM: Defense Mechanisms
  - AT: Anthropology and Transparency
