**ATT&CK ID: M1017**  
**ATT&CK Technique Name: User Training**

**Description:**

It is essential to educate developers and system administrators about the risks associated with storing plaintext passwords in software configuration files. These files, if left on endpoint systems or servers, can be a significant security vulnerability. Proper training should emphasize the importance of using secure methods for managing and storing credentials to mitigate the risk of unauthorized access.
