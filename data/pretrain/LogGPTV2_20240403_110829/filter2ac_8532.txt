**ATT&CK ID:** G0061  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** FIN8

FIN8 has been known to employ various obfuscation techniques to evade detection. Specifically, they use environment variables and standard input (stdin) to obscure command-line arguments. Additionally, FIN8 obfuscates malicious macros that are delivered as payloads, further complicating the identification and analysis of their activities. [6][38][39]
