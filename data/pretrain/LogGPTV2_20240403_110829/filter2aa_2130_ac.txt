### Product Overview

#### PHOTO ID
**Product Name:** Wiegand Photo ID Badge  
**Base Part Number:** 17X  
**Features:**
- Combines Wiegand technology and photo identification on a single card.
- Accepts both Polaroid film prints and video image prints.

#### POCKET TAG
**Product Name:** Wiegand Access Control Tag  
**Base Part Number:** 145  
**Features:**
- Compact design, suitable for carrying in a pocket or on a key ring.
- Compatible with Classic, Epic, Turnstile, and PINPad readers.

#### SENSORKEY™
**Product Name:** Wiegand Access Control Key  
**Base Part Number:** 25  
**Features:**
- Key-style Wiegand tag designed for use with low-profile, in-the-wall SensorKey readers.
