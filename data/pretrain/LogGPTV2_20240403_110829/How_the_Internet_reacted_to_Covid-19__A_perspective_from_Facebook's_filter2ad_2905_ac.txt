### Network Challenges and Recovery During the COVID-19 Pandemic

The unprecedented surge in internet traffic during the COVID-19 pandemic led to several network challenges, including broken links, overutilization of Content Delivery Network (CDN) servers, and congestion in Internet Service Provider (ISP) access networks, particularly in mobile networks in emerging markets. However, operators implemented various measures, such as capacity additions, rate limiting, and capping video bitrates, which, along with the eventual stabilization of network traffic, allowed networks to recover to their pre-COVID-19 performance levels relatively quickly.
