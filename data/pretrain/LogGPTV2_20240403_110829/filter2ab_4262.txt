**ATT&CK ID:** S0036  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** FLASHFLOOD

**Description:**
FLASHFLOOD is a technique used to search for and copy interesting files from removable media to a staging area. The files targeted can be either a default or customized set of file extensions. By default, the technique copies specific data types that may have been transferred to the drive by another tool, such as SPACESHIP.
