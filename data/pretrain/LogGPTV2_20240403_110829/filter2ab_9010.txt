**ATT&CK ID:** G0022  
**ATT&CK Technique Name:** APT3

**Description:**
APT3 achieves persistence by replacing the `sethc.exe` binary, located at `C:\Windows\System32\`, with a malicious version. This technique allows the attacker to maintain access and execute code each time the Sticky Keys utility is invoked.
